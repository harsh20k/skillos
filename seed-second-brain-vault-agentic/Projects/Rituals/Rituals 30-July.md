## ✅ Feasibility & Tips

- **Yes, it’s doable**, but it’s a **tight sprint**.  
- **Scope discipline**: If anything threatens the schedule, defer non-critical polish to post-launch.  
- **Review buffer**: Apple review can take 2–5 business days. Submitting by **8 Aug** gives you a slim margin for approval.  
- **Stay focused**: Use Pomodoro sprints, block distractions, and batch your testing.

With clear priorities and disciplined execution, you can absolutely wrap up your MVP and have your meditation ritual app live by the second week of August. Good luck! 🙏🏼
