In the midst of all the daily activities a question lingers behind the "I have to do this/ Why did he say this to me?". The question is very profound and can open up a whole new unfamiliar world of philosophy, meaning and context of reality. 
	
The question of "What is all this?". And that question is not alone, it has many siblings and friends, like "Who am I?" or "What is my purpose here?".

I had all those questions lingering too, but I did not shy away from asking them to myself. Asking these questions has led me to this journey in meditation and spirituality that I could never have imagined. It was rough in the beginning but then with time all the questions start to dissolve into silence. 

For the past 10 years I have been sitting in silence for around an hour everyday in my living room or a public bus or a metro.
