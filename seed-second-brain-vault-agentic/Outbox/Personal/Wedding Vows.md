# **Sacred Marriage Vows**

## _A Divine Pact of Love, Trust, and Spiritual Growth_

---
<p align="center">The only reason to get married is to foster an environment of divine friendship with the two souls on the divine path. Premanandji describes it beautifully, that since we could not walk alone on this divine path our divine mother came in the form of our partner to help us on this journey. If we cannot help each other on this journey easing each other's spiritual struggles and shoulder each other weights then its best the two souls should not embark on this journey together.</p>

---
### _This document serves as a sacred commitment between_
**Suman Khera & Harsh Pandey**

---
<p align="center">On this auspicious day of 2025-01-26, in the presence of God and our sacred purpose, we pledge to uphold these vows as the foundation of our marriage.</p>

---

- To always try to uphold [God](app://obsidian.md/God) in our lives. To always support each other with patience, compassion and understanding, that spiritual growth is a journey with its own challenges.
 - To create a "home environment" with a foundation of spiritual friendship based on the [Guru](app://obsidian.md/Guru)'s words and our [Dharma](app://obsidian.md/Dharma).
 - To always be loyal to the truth and be faithful to each other.
 - To always make all the decisions mutually irrespective of their significance.
 - To protect each other from wrong company or wrong decisions throughout life.
 - To address each other with utmost respect and affection.
 - To resolve conflicts with wisdom and love, remembering that our ultimate goal is spiritual progress and not worldly victories.
 - To forgive each other without judgement and move on from past mistakes.
 - To not be afraid to reject anyone or anything that hinders the spiritual progress.
 - To always put [God](app://obsidian.md/God) first in our lives and always put our trust in the divine plan, even when challenges test our faith and remind each other of our sacred purpose.
 - To never try to display our spiritual wealth to anyone outside this relationship.
 - To value our household privacy with great respect.
 - To not look at other people with pride, jealousy or avarice.
 - To not forget about our partner when finally God decides to meet any one of us.
 - To always treat wealth, respect and bodily desires as horrific poisons.
 - To never accept personal service from anyone else than our partner hereafter.
 - To faithfully perform household duties as a service of [Divine Mother](app://obsidian.md/Divine%20Mother).
 - To handle worldly responsibilities(family, work, community, any service) without attachment, always keeping our higher purpose in mind.
 - To see each other as companions on this divine journey, never as obstacles, and to walk together with love, trust, and devotion until we merge into the Supreme.
 - To re-promise these vows each year and make necessary changes as we grow.
