Student life is not just learning about certain technologies and concepts. 

It is much more than that. 

The most essential part of student life is to learn how to "think". No matter who or what you encounter in that period, EVERYTHING can be a lesson for how to orient oneself.

This not only builds a professional, but also one with character, integrity and a "wholeness" that can't be put in words.

Grateful to get another chance for being a student, going to make the most of this.

I am hearing lots of things about the cold job market for tech. 

It can be discouraging, if I go into this rabbit hole of biased articles and opinions that reinforce such narrative.

When I got my first job in 2013 after graduation, can you guess what people were saying then? 

Exactly the same thing! "Its a tough market right now".

Jobs and businesses were being created then as they are now. 

Its all about what narrative you chose to hear. 

When my wife recently was searching for her first full-time job in Halifax, she was anxious and started believing the same. 

But as soon she got an offer all that negative self-talk was gone. 

It doesn't make sense until it does.
