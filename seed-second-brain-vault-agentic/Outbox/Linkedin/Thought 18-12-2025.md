## Something about the structuring of notes and all course materials. 


Before beginning grad school, the best thing I did was have a plan for structuring all the courses and their notes and any resources. 

At first it was a bit hard to navigate and clunky but as I got the hang of it, the benefits started to appear.

I was not confused about the deadlines, the project meetings and all the class schedules. 

All the syllabus, slides, notes were at my fingerprints and everything searchable, tagged and ready to be studied. 

All my course content stayed in obsidian app and my choice of calendar was "Structured" app which showed all the tasks only for today(no overwhelming week/month views).

This is what my typical folder structure looks like along with my typical Monday in this semester.

I wonder how everyone else at university is managing all this.
