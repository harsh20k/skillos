# Aligning with Divine Purpose: Reflections on Spirituality and Daily Life

The ultimate core of the being is spirituality, the soul which is a part of [[God]] needs to be aligned towards God. That is the final and most important objective of this life for sure. 

![[Pasted image 20250118085418.jpg]]

---
Rest there are the duties or [[Dharma]] of the body and mind other than anything related to the spiritual sphere. [[Premanandji]] and my [[Gurudeva]] says that, the work other than spirituality is not actual work and they also say that the [[Divine Mother]] should be remembered even throughout our most mundane daily routines. So that is clear that every work be it your [[Sadhana]] or some professional job or some duties of the household all lie within the boundary of soul and spirituality. 

---
Its through grace that every thought comes to all of us. So the thought of writing and inspiration for putting words to paper to better align my thinking with my objectives was actually lord's inspiration. 

So, I think this is also [[Divine Mother]]'s inspiration to write about my professional goals and journey and create a newsletter. I've heard many advantages of writing a newsletter from various content creators and today I got to read a newsletter which I have subscribed to. "Lea Wei", a portrait artist based from France sends out a weekly newsletter and she shares her professional and art journey.

Since it is a good thing and sanctioned by [[Divine Mother]] herself I think we should begin doing it and think later what are the benefits or cons of this process. **If there is anytime a feeling that this process is either helping my ultimate objective or hindering my main path then I will have to take the respective corrective actions.** 

---
Let's think about what should we write in this newsletter. First of all, it will not include anything about my [[Sadhana]] or my [[Divine Mother]] because that's too private, we don't share the stories of our home with our friends or strangers. 

![[Pasted image 20250118085451.jpg]]

This letter will be composed of my thoughts on my professional and a little bit of personal/home/relationships. That would be a way to think and work out the current issues of the current state of mind and this letter will be like a **manager** responsible for the duties of this body/mind.

---
I have experienced this first-hand that the mind is very stubborn in finding the ways to get bored even with new and fresh experiences. This sometimes lead to problems in managing short-term finances and sudden peaks and dips in perceived productivity. Writing about this hopefully will help better manage the stress involved through all of this and I might be more helpful to others and become a better servant. 

---
### TL;DR: Reflections on Spirituality and Daily Life

- **Core Objective**: Spiritual alignment with God is the ultimate purpose of life.
- **Duties and Work**: All work, whether spiritual or mundane, should align with the soul’s spiritual journey.
- **Divine Grace**: Every thought and inspiration comes from divine grace, including the idea to start writing.
- **Newsletter Vision**:
	- Focus on professional and personal growth without sharing private spiritual practices.
	- Serve as a tool to manage thoughts, goals, and challenges effectively.
- **Challenges Addressed**: Combatting boredom, managing finances, and maintaining consistent productivity.
- **Commitment**: Continuously evaluate if actions align with the ultimate spiritual objective, taking corrective measures as needed.
