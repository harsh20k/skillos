# DeepSense

Ocean technology and AI organization based in Halifax, Nova Scotia, operating within the COVE (Centre of Ocean Ventures & Entrepreneurship) ecosystem. Focuses on applying machine learning and data science to marine and ocean industries.

## Key Details
- Part of Halifax's ocean-tech community alongside COVE
- Runs co-op and internship programs (partnered with Dalhousie)
- Works with marine data, fish population monitoring, and distributed AI/ML systems
- Involved in agentic AI and production ML deployments (e.g., n8n, context engineering)
