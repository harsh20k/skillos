**Why Canva qualifies as SaaS**
- **Cloud-based**: Runs entirely on Canva’s servers.
- **Browser access**: No local installation required.
- **Subscription model**: Free tier + paid plans (Pro/Teams/Enterprise).
- **Automatic updates**: New features roll out centrally.
- **Multi-tenant**: Same platform serves millions of users with isolated data.

In short: Canva delivers design software over the internet, managed and maintained by the provider—this is the core SaaS model.
