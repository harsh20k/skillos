SwiftUI provides a variety of built-in view modifiers that allow you to easily customize and modify the appearance and behavior of your views. Here are some common view modifiers provided by SwiftUI:

### Layout and Position

- **`padding`**: Adds padding around the view.
  ```swift
  .padding()
  ```
- **`frame`**: Sets the view's size and alignment.
  ```swift
  .frame(width: 100, height: 100)
  ```
- **`position`**: Positions the view at a specific point within its parent.
  ```swift
  .position(x: 50, y: 50)
  ```
- **`offset`**: Offsets the view by a certain amount.
  ```swift
  .offset(x: 10, y: 10)
  ```

### Styling

- **`background`**: Adds a background view.
  ```swift
  .background(Color.blue)
  ```
