## 🌱 Structure of the First Article

### 1. **Start with a feeling.**
Open with a sentence that captures the quiet ache many feel but don’t express. Something like:

> "I used to look okay on the outside — smiling, working, showing up — but inside, I was always carrying questions I couldn’t share."

Or:

> "I didn’t start meditating to become peaceful. I started because I was tired of being quietly lost."

Be real. Be human. This connects people instantly.

---

### 2. **Share your personal turning point.**
Describe how and why you began meditating. Was it pain, curiosity, a friend? Don’t overexplain — just one or two sincere moments.

---

### 3. **Describe what it has given you.**
Gently talk about how it changed your relationship with the world — not perfection, but presence. You might say:

> "It didn’t solve all my problems. But it gave me a place to rest. A place that I now want to offer to others."

---

### 4. **Invite them in.**
End by saying this is the beginning of a new offering — a small space where people can:
- Read short reflections
- Join free meditation sessions
- Share a quiet journey together

---

### 5. **Link to the first meditation session.**
Even if it’s just you sitting there — offer a time, date, and how to join (Zoom link or simple form).
