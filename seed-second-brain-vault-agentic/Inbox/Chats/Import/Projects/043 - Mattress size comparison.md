# Mattress size comparison

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

Approximate North American mattress sizes (in inches):  

| Type | Width × Length | Notes |
|------|----------------|--------|
| **Twin** | 38 × 75 | Single adult or child |
| **Full (Double)** | 54 × 75 | One adult; tighter for two |
| **Queen** | 60 × 80 | Common for couples |
| **King** | 76 × 80 | Two adults, large space |
| **California King** | 72 × 84 | Longer, slightly narrower |

So:  
- **King > Queen > Full** in both width and length.  
- Queen is 6" wider and 5" longer than Full.  
- King is 16" wider than Queen (same length).
