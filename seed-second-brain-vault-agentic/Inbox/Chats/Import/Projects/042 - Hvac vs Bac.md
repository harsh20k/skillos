# Hvac vs Bac

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

HVAC (Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning) and BAC (Building Automation Control) differ in scope:  

**HVAC**  
- Refers to the *mechanical systems* that regulate temperature, humidity, and air quality in a building.  
- Includes equipment like furnaces, chillers, boilers, ducts, vents, and thermostats.  
- Focus: *Environmental comfort and air management.*

**BAC**  
- Refers to the *control system* that automates and optimizes building operations, including HVAC, lighting, security, and energy usage.  
- Integrates sensors, controllers, and software to monitor and adjust systems in real time.  
- Focus: *Automation, efficiency, and centralized monitoring.*

**Relation**  
HVAC is one subsystem *within* a BAC.  
BAC systems monitor and control HVAC units for better energy efficiency and comfort management.
