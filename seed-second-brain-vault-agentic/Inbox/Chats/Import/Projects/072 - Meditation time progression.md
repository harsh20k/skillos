# Meditation time progression

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

Let’s break it down for all three cases: increasing **meditation time by 30 seconds** at regular intervals (weekly, fortnightly, or monthly) starting from **5 minutes per day**.

---

### ✅ Assumptions
- Starting time = **5 minutes/day**
- Increase = **30 seconds** per interval
- Duration = **1 year = 52 weeks ≈ 12 months ≈ 26 fortnights**

---

## 📈 Case 1: **Increase every week (30 sec/week)**

- Weekly increase: 30 seconds = 0.5 minutes  
- After 52 weeks:  
  - Total increase = 0.5 × 52 = **26 minutes**
  - Final time = 5 + 26 = **31 minutes/day**

---

## 📈 Case 2: **Increase every fortnight (30 sec/2 weeks)**

- Fortnights in a year ≈ 26  
- Total increase = 0.5 × 26 = **13 minutes**
- Final time = 5 + 13 = **18 minutes/day**

---

## 📈 Case 3: **Increase every month (30 sec/month)**

- Months = 12  
- Total increase = 0.5 × 12 = **6 minutes**
- Final time = 5 + 6 = **11 minutes/day**

---
