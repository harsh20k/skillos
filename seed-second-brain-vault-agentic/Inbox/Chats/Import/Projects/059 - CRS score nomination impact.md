## 📌 Likely TEER Category for You
- **NOC Code (2021):** 21232 — *Software developers and programmers*  
- **TEER Category:** **TEER 1**  

---

## 🔎 Why TEER 1?  
- **TEER 0** → Management jobs (directors, senior managers).  
- **TEER 1** → Jobs that usually require a **university degree** (bachelor’s or higher).  
- **TEER 2 & 3** → College diploma or apprenticeship jobs (technical but not degree-level).  
- **TEER 4 & 5** → Jobs needing only high school or short training.  

Since software/app development requires a **degree-level education and specialised knowledge**, it is classified under **TEER 1**.  

---

## ✅ What This Means for You  
- If you get a job in Halifax as a **software developer, programmer, or mobile app developer**, your job offer will count as **TEER 1**.  
- This is good because for PR (Express Entry / Nova Scotia PNP), **TEER 0, 1, 2, and 3** are considered “skilled jobs” and qualify.  
- With TEER 1, you’re in a strong position for both **Express Entry** and **NSNP nomination** after you gain work experience.
