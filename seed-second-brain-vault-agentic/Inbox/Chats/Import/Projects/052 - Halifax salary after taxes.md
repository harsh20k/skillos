## ­čĺí Tips & caveats

- **Audio quality matters**: If the lecture room is noisy or the lecturer speaks softly, the transcription and summary accuracy will drop. Use an external mic or sit near front if possible.
- **Review the full transcript**: AI summaries are shortcuts. They may miss subtle but important points, nuance, or context. Always skim / cross-check.

- **Cost**: Many apps offer a free tier with limited minutes or features. For heavy use (e.g. full semester of lectures), you might need a paid plan.
