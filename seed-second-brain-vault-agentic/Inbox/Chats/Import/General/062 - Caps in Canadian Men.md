# Caps in Canadian Men

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

Mostly function and culture.  
1. **Climate**: Canadian weather shifts quickly—sun glare, wind, light rain—so caps serve a practical purpose year-round.  
2. **Casual style**: Canadian men generally dress informally. Baseball caps fit that aesthetic across workplaces, universities, and outdoors.  
3. **Sports identity**: Local teams (NHL, MLB, CFL) anchor community pride; team caps act as everyday symbols.  
4. **Hair and convenience**: Caps hide unstyled hair and protect from UV, snow, or drizzle without needing extra gear.  
5. **Cultural influence**: North American pop culture—especially American streetwear—normalized caps as standard male accessories.
