# Mop usage instructions

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

That is a spin mop head. To use it:

1. **Attach head:** Ensure the mop head is securely locked into the circular socket at the bottom of the handle.  
2. **Dip and spin:** Place the mop head into a water bucket (usually with a built-in spinner).  
3. **Press handle vertically:** Push the handle straight down; the mop will spin and wring out excess water.  
4. **Mop the floor:** Move it in figure-eight or circular motions.  
5. **Rinse and repeat:** Dip and spin again when dirty or too dry.

If your bucket has no spinner, manually wring the mop by twisting the handle or squeezing the head by hand.
