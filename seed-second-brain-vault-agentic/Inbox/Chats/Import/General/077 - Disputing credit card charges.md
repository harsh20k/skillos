# Disputing credit card charges

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

No. Disliking a product is not a valid reason for a dispute.  
You can only dispute if:  
- The product was not delivered,  
- It was defective or misrepresented,  
- Or the seller violated their terms.  

If you simply changed your mind or didn’t like it, you must handle it with the merchant’s return or refund policy, not through a credit card dispute.
