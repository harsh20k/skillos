# Alternatives to Interdisciplinary

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

Here are some alternatives to the word **interdisciplinary**, depending on the context:

1. **Multidisciplinary** – emphasizes multiple fields working together but not necessarily integrating.
2. **Cross-disciplinary** – focuses on bridging two or more fields without full integration.
3. **Transdisciplinary** – suggests collaboration that transcends traditional boundaries of disciplines.
4. **Integrated** – highlights the blending of ideas from different fields.
5. **Collaborative** – emphasizes teamwork between fields.
6. **Hybrid** – suggests a mix of methodologies or disciplines.
7. **Convergent** – implies coming together of diverse fields to address a problem.
8. **Holistic** – focuses on a comprehensive, multi-faceted approach.
9. **Multifaceted** – implies drawing from different areas to address complexity.
10. **Synergistic** – emphasizes the interaction between fields to create greater outcomes. 

The best substitute will depend on the nuance you want to convey! Let me know if you'd like help refining it further.
