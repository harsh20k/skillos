A **hot dog** 🌭 is a type of food made by putting a cooked sausage (usually made from pork, beef, or chicken, sometimes mixed) inside a sliced bread roll (the bun).  

- The sausage itself is often a **frankfurter** or **wiener**, which is a kind of cured and cooked sausage.  
- People usually add toppings like **mustard, ketchup, onions, relish, cheese, or sauerkraut**.  
- It’s very popular in the U.S. and Canada as street food, at sports games, and barbecues.  

👉 So in short: **Hot dog = sausage + bread bun + toppings**.
