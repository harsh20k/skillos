# Puta madre meaning

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

“Puta madre” is a Spanish slang phrase. Literally, it means “whore mother,” but its meaning depends entirely on context and tone:

- **Positive (colloquial):** “¡Está de puta madre!” = “It’s awesome!” or “It’s great!”  
- **Negative (angry):** “¡Puta madre!” = “Damn it!” or “For f***’s sake!”

It’s very informal and often vulgar, so use only among close friends or in casual settings.
