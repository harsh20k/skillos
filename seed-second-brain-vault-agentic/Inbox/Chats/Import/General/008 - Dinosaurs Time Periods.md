**Dinosaurs lived during the Mesozoic Era**, which lasted from about **252 to 66 million years ago**. It is divided into three periods:

1. **Triassic Period** (≈ **252–201 million years ago**)  
   - First dinosaurs appeared  
   - Mostly small and early forms

2. **Jurassic Period** (≈ **201–145 million years ago**)  
   - Dinosaurs diversified and grew very large  
   - Famous dinosaurs like large sauropods and early birds

3. **Cretaceous Period** (≈ **145–66 million years ago**)  
   - Peak dinosaur diversity  
   - Ended with a mass extinction (asteroid impact)

**Dinosaurs went extinct ~66 million years ago**, except for birds, which are considered their modern descendants.
