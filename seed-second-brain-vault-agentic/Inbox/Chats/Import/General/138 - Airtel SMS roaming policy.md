##  Can You Receive Incoming SMS on Airtel While International Roaming?

###  What Airtel Officially States
You must **activate an International Roaming (IR) pack** to use your Airtel SIM abroad. This is required to receive calls, data, **and SMS (including OTPs)** citeturn0search1turn0search4.

###  What Recent Reports Indicate
- As of **May 18–May 29, 2025**, Airtel **changed its policy**.
- Incoming SMS while roaming is **no longer free** without maintaining an active IR pack (or "World Pass") citeturn0search11.

###  A Summary of Key Points
| Scenario                          | Outcome                              |
|-----------------------------------|--------------------------------------|
| No IR pack active                 | **Incoming SMS will be blocked**     |
| IR pack (e.g., ₹98 for 28 days) active | **Incoming SMS works as normal** |

##  What Should You Do If You're Abroad?

1. **Activate an Airtel International Roaming pack** before or during your travel:
   - You can do this via the **Airtel Thanks App** (toggle IR on/off).
   - Or send an SMS: **IRSTART to 121**.
   - Or contact customer support (e.g., via WhatsApp at 99100-99100) citeturn0search1turn0search4.

2. AirTel recently started enforcing charges for incoming SMS while roaming—so you **must maintain an active IR pack** (even if your domestic plan is long-term, like 365 days) to continue receiving messages.

---

###  Example Use-Case
If you're living or traveling abroad and rely on OTPs for banking or other services, ensure your Airtel SIM has an IR pack active. Without it, those messages will stop.
