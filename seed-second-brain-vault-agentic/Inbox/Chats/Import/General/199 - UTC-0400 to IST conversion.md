# UTC-04:00 to IST conversion

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

To convert the time from **UTC-04:00** to **IST (Indian Standard Time)**:

IST is **UTC+05:30**, so there is a difference of **+9 hours and 30 minutes**.

- **8:00 a.m. (UTC-04:00)** → **5:30 p.m. (IST)**
- **8:45 a.m. (UTC-04:00)** → **6:15 p.m. (IST)**

The converted time in IST is:  
**5:30 p.m. - 6:15 p.m.**
