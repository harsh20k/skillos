### **Lower-Cost or Free Options**
**1. Buffer** — Social media scheduler with LinkedIn support  
- Affordable plans start around **$6/month per channel** with unlimited scheduling and basic analytics.  
- Simple UI, multi-platform scheduling (LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, etc.).  
- Good pick if you mainly need scheduled posting without heavy AI content generation. citeturn0search27

**2. AuthoredUp** — LinkedIn writing and scheduling tool  
- Plans around **$14.95–$19.95/month** with unlimited drafts, hooks, formatting support, and scheduling.  
- Cheaper alternative focused on content quality rather than heavy AI suggestions. citeturn0search7

**3. Contentide** — Low-cost content creator tool  
- Offers a **free tier** and a **one-time purchase (~$24.99)** lifetime option instead of monthly fees.  
- Good for affordable LinkedIn post writing tools, though scheduling capabilities may be limited depending on plan. citeturn0search13

**4. Postify** (from LinkedIn post generator listings)  
- Provides **free plan with 7 posts and daily scheduling**, so you can auto-post basic content.  
- Best for simple batch scheduling without subscription commitment. citeturn0search20

### **Basic Social Media Scheduling Tools (Generally Cheaper than AI-Heavy Tools)**
These don’t focus on AI post generation but **let you schedule auto-posting** efficiently at lower cost:

**5. Planable**  
- Free and low-cost plans available with collaborative scheduling and batch publishing.  
- Suited for teams or individuals planning LinkedIn content (plus other platforms). citeturn0search26

**6. SocialBee** / **Pallyy** / **Sendible**
