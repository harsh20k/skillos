# Court Hearing Summary Zettai

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

The document is a correspondence from the Singapore High Court regarding case HC/OA 861/2024 involving Zettai Pte. Ltd. It includes a Zoom chat log from a webinar hearing held on September 25, 2024. The correspondence outlines that the queries raised during the hearing need to be addressed by Zettai Pte. Ltd. and presented in an affidavit within three weeks, to be shared with all creditors. The document also mentions that the chat log is attached for distribution to all concerned parties, and directs Zettai Pte. Ltd. and their solicitors, Rajah & Tann Singapore LLP, to make this available to creditors promptly.

The chat log itself contains messages from various participants, addressing concerns and raising questions regarding the handling of funds by WazirX, a subsidiary of Zanmai Labs Pvt Ltd, which is associated with Zettai Pte. Ltd. Topics discussed include alleged financial irregularities, a hack of crypto assets, disputes over the voting process for a moratorium, transparency issues, and concerns about how funds are managed and returned to users. Participants express concerns about the fairness of the process, lack of transparency, and the alleged use of user funds to support legal costs.
