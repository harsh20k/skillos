This document is a sealed order from the High Court of Singapore, concerning Zettai Pte Ltd and filed under the Insolvency, Restructuring, and Dissolution Act 2018. Key points include:

1. **Moratorium Order**: A four-month moratorium has been granted from the date of the order (26 September 2024). During this period:
   - No resolution to wind up Zettai Pte Ltd can be passed.
   - No legal proceedings against Zettai can be started or continued without court permission, except for certain proceedings initiated by Zettai before 23 August 2024.
   - No enforcement action against Zettai’s assets can be taken without court approval.

2. **Applicability**: The moratorium applies to anyone within Singapore or under the court's jurisdiction, regardless of the location of the act.

3. **Conditions of the Order**:
   - Zettai must file an affidavit within three weeks detailing information about a hacked wallet and the current location of users' remaining cryptocurrency assets, to be shared with creditors.
   - Zettai must respond to users’ queries raised during a hearing on 25 September 2024 and file an affidavit with the record of those queries and responses within three weeks.
   - Zettai is required to file another affidavit within six weeks providing updated financial documents (management accounts, balance sheets, profit and loss statement as of 27 August 2024).
   - All future electronic voting related to court applications must be overseen by an independent party.
   - The positions of Zettai’s top 22 creditors must be identified in all future support or opposition for court applications.

Failure to comply with this order may result in enforcement proceedings, including contempt of court.
