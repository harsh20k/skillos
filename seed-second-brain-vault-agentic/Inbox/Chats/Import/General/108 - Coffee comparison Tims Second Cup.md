### What to ask for

Second Cup doesn’t usually name a “Double Double” as such, but you can ask for:

- **“Brewed coffee with two creams and two sugars”**  
- Or **“coffee with 2 milk / cream & 2 sugars”**

That should get you very close to the Tim’s Double Double flavor.

---

### Alternative drinks that might be close

Second Cup offers a few drinks on their menu (hot & iced) that might approximate the taste:

- Their **“brewed coffees & teas”** section (just a basic brewed coffee) — you can customize the cream and sugar levels. citeturn0search14  
- Their lattes or “white coffees” (if you make them weak) with added sugar might approach a creamier, sweeter coffee.  
- You could get a **latte with light espresso and extra syrup** (adjusting strength) to mimic the sweetness + cream.
