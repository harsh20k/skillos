# Watering Sansevieria Hahnii

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

This is a **Sansevieria trifasciata ‘Hahnii’**, commonly called a **bird’s nest snake plant**.  

### Watering Guidelines
- **In general:** Water sparingly. These plants are very drought-tolerant and prefer to dry out between waterings.  
- **Indoor environment:** About **every 2–3 weeks** is typical, but it can be more or less depending on light, temperature, and humidity.  
- **Check soil:** Stick your finger 1–2 inches into the soil—if it feels completely dry, it’s time to water. If it’s still damp, wait a few more days.  
- **Seasonal adjustment:**  
  - **Spring/Summer (growing season):** Water a bit more often, but still let the soil dry first.  
  - **Fall/Winter (dormant season):** Water much less, sometimes just once a month.  

⚠️ **Important:** Overwatering is the main killer. Always make sure the pot has good drainage and never let it sit in water.
