# Easiest languages for Hindi speakers

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

If a person’s mother tongue is Hindi, they're fluent in English, and have some basic knowledge of Spanish, then languages that are relatively easy to learn would include:

1. **Spanish**: Since you already know some Spanish, continuing with it would be one of the easiest options. It has straightforward pronunciation, a simple grammar structure, and shares many vocabulary similarities with English.

2. **French**: French shares many similarities with English and Spanish, such as vocabulary and grammatical rules. Hindi speakers might also find the pronunciation manageable since they are accustomed to more complex sounds.

3. **Portuguese**: If you know some Spanish, Portuguese can be quite accessible because of the shared Latin roots and similar grammatical structures.

4. **Italian**: Italian is another Romance language like Spanish and French. It has a phonetic pronunciation, and learning it would be relatively smooth if you're familiar with Spanish.

5. **Urdu**: Given its close similarity to Hindi in terms of grammar and spoken vocabulary (albeit with a different script), Urdu might be easy to learn. Additionally, if you have a cultural or regional exposure, it will come quite naturally.

These languages leverage your existing skills in English and Spanish, making them easier to pick up quickly.
