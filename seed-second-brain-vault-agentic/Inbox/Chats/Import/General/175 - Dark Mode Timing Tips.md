# Dark Mode Timing Tips

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

If you wake at **6 a.m.** and sleep around **10 p.m.**, the ideal time to activate **dark mode** on your phone would be:

### **Around 7:00 p.m.**

**Why 7:00 p.m.?**
- It aligns with the beginning of natural dimming in your environment (post-sunset).
- Reduces blue light exposure before bed, which can help your body produce melatonin and prepare for sleep.
- Gives your eyes a rest from bright screens during the wind-down period before sleep.

You can also consider **sunset-based automation**, if your phone supports it, so it adjusts automatically based on your local sunset time.
