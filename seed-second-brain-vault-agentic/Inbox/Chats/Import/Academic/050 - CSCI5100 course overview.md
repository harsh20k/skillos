```markdown
# Sample Cover Letter – Writing Centre Assistant

---

**Harsh Pandey**  
Halifax, NS  
harsh.pandey77@gmail.com | (782) 555-1234 | LinkedIn: linkedin.com/in/harshpandey  

September 25, 2025  

Hiring Committee  
Dalhousie Writing Centre  
Killam Library  
Halifax, NS  

---

## Greeting
Dear Hiring Committee,

---

## Opening Paragraph (Hook + Intent)
I am writing to express my interest in the Writing Centre Assistant position at the Killam Library. As a graduate student in the Master of Applied Computer Science program at Dalhousie, I bring a strong background in academic writing, communication, and mentoring, alongside a commitment to helping students succeed in their studies.

---

## Why You (Core Skills & Experience)
In my academic and professional journey, I have consistently worked in roles that required both technical clarity and strong communication. While working as a Systems Engineer at Infosys, I prepared documentation and training resources for cross-functional teams. Later, during my freelancing career in design and animation, I regularly translated complex technical ideas into accessible content for diverse audiences.  

Additionally, I have supported peers in refining essays, presentations, and technical reports, which has sharpened my ability to provide constructive feedback. These experiences have strengthened my skills in editing, mentoring, and communicating ideas clearly and effectively.

---

## Why Them (Company Fit)
The Writing Centre’s mission to support students across disciplines resonates with me. I value the opportunity to help fellow students improve their academic writing, especially those from diverse linguistic and cultural backgrounds. Being part of the Writing Centre would allow me to combine my technical and creative communication skills to help others achieve clarity and confidence in their work.

---

## Closing (Call to Action)
I would welcome the opportunity to contribute to the Writing Centre team and support Dalhousie’s academic community. Thank you for considering my application, and I look forward to the possibility of discussing how my background and skills align with this role.

---

## Signature
Sincerely,  
Harsh Pandey  

---
```
