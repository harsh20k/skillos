My content strategy is LinkedIn-first, using it as a public thinking log rather than a growth hack. I post regularly across four connected quadrants—mindset and philosophy (how I think), technical craft (what I understand deeply), projects and execution (what I build and learn by doing), and professional growth and industry awareness (how it applies in the real world). LinkedIn acts as the distribution and credibility layer, while deeper ideas compound into long-form writing, visual frameworks, and eventually paid knowledge assets, allowing me to build authority, optional income, and long-term value alongside my MACS degree and full-time career.
