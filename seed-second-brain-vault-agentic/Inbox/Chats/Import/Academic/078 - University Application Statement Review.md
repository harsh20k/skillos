# University Application Statement Review

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

Generally, there is no strict rule requiring you to either justify or left-align the text of your Statement of Purpose (SOP). Most academic and admissions offices are accustomed to documents that are left-aligned. Left-alignment is also the default formatting in many word processors and is often preferred for readability, especially with longer, text-heavy documents.

Full justification can sometimes give a clean, uniform look, but it may also create awkward spacing between words, making the text harder to read. Ultimately, either approach is acceptable, but a simple, left-aligned format is a safe and common choice.
