# Search for Dalhousie MACS students

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

On LinkedIn, there isn’t a direct filter for “incoming students” in a specific program, but you can still find them with a few strategic search methods:

### 🔍 Step 1: Use LinkedIn Search Bar
- Type queries like:
  - `Dalhousie University "Master of Applied Computer Science"`  
  - `Dalhousie University MACS 2025`  
  - `Dalhousie University CSCI MACS`  
  - `Dalhousie University incoming MACS`  

Often, students include **“Incoming student at Dalhousie University”** or “Starting Fall 2025 – MACS” in their headline or “About” section.

---

### 🔍 Step 2: Apply Filters
After searching:
1. Click **People**.
2. Use filters:
   - **Current School** → Dalhousie University  
   - **Keyword in Headline/About** → “MACS”, “Applied Computer Science”, “Master of Applied Computer Science”  
   - **Location** → Halifax (optional, since many students update after landing).

---

### 🔍 Step 3: Look at Groups & Posts
- Search for groups like **“Dalhousie University Graduate Students”**, **“Dalhousie MACS Students”**, or **“Dalhousie University 2025”**.
- Use hashtags like `#DalhousieUniversity #MACS #StudyInCanada #Fall2025`.

Many incoming students announce admissions/acceptances publicly with those hashtags.

---

### 🔍 Step 4: Connect via Alumni Tool
- Go to Dalhousie University’s **LinkedIn page** → Click **Alumni**.
- Search within Alumni for **“Applied Computer Science”**.
- You’ll see both current students and recent admits. Check “Years” filter to spot people starting 2025–2026.
