1. **Acceptance Recommendation (Acceptance Letter)**:
   - The Graduate Committee has recommended your acceptance.
   - Final approval will be made by the **Faculty of Graduate Studies**, who will issue an official acceptance letter.
   - If approved, you are officially admitted into the program.

4. **Fees**:
   - Tuition and other fees for international students are approximately CAD **$29,400** per academic year (this includes tuition and international differential fees but excludes health insurance).

5. **Conditions**:
   - The decision is conditional until final approval by the Faculty of Graduate Studies.
