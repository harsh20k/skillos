# Hi and Summary

**Priority:** Medium

### Assistant

For organizing your move to Canada and tracking progress, here are some mobile apps that can help you manage everything efficiently:

1. **Trello**: A flexible project management app that allows you to create boards, lists, and cards to track different aspects of your planning, such as documentation, finances, and timelines. You can also collaborate with your partner on shared tasks.

2. **Notion**: A comprehensive workspace app where you can create databases, checklists, notes, and calendars. You can share pages with your partner to keep all information like important dates, documents, and progress updates in one place.

3. **Asana**: A task and project management tool designed for team collaboration. You can set milestones for your move, assign tasks to each other, and keep track of deadlines and requirements for your move to Canada.

4. **Google Keep / Google Drive**: Simple but powerful tools to create notes, checklists, and store documents. Google Drive will be helpful for keeping all important documents securely stored and accessible.

5. **Todoist**: A task management app that allows you to create to-do lists, set reminders, and prioritize tasks. You can use it to keep track of day-to-day tasks related to your move and stay organized with your partner.

6. **Evernote**: Great for taking detailed notes, saving web clippings, and organizing documents. It can help you keep track of research on the immigration process, budget planning, and create to-do lists for each step.

These apps will help you break down the planning process into manageable tasks, keep track of timelines, and collaborate with your partner efficiently.
