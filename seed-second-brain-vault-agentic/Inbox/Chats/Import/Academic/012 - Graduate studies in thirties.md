### Assistant

a cycle count of 8 means the macbook battery has gone through only eight full charge cycles  

in simple terms it indicates the battery is almost new  

how to understand it  
one battery cycle equals using a total of 100 percent of the battery over time  
for example  
using 30 percent today and 70 percent later counts as one cycle  
charging from 0 to 100 percent counts as one cycle  

what cycle count 8 tells you  
the battery has been used very little  
battery health should be close to 100 percent  
there is minimal chemical aging  
you should expect full day or near full day battery life depending on usage  

for context  
most modern macbook batteries are designed for about 1000 cycles before noticeable capacity decline  

in short cycle count 8 means your battery is in excellent condition and practically new
