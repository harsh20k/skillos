# Amuse-bouche definition

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

A **“amuse-bouche”** is a small, bite-sized appetizer served before the first course in fine dining.  
Purpose: to “amuse the mouth” — a chef’s complimentary sample showing their style and setting the tone for the meal.  
Usually one or two bites, often presented creatively, and not ordered from the menu.
