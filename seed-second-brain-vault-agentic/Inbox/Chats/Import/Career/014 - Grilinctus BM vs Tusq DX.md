# Grilinctus BM vs Tusq DX

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

**Grilinctus BM** and **Tusq DX** are both cough syrups but have different compositions and uses. Here’s a comparison:

### **1. Grilinctus BM Syrup**
- **Composition:**
  - Bromhexine (Mucolytic) – Helps break down and thin mucus.
  - Terbutaline (Bronchodilator) – Relaxes airway muscles to ease breathing.
  - Guaifenesin (Expectorant) – Aids in clearing mucus from the airways.

- **Uses:**
  - Recommended for **productive (wet) cough** with mucus.
  - Helps in conditions like bronchitis, asthma, and other respiratory disorders with mucus build-up.

- **Side Effects:**
  - Nausea, vomiting, headache, dizziness, palpitations.

---

### **2. Tusq DX Syrup**
- **Composition:**
  - Dextromethorphan (Cough Suppressant) – Reduces the urge to cough.
  - Phenylephrine (Decongestant) – Reduces nasal congestion.
  - Chlorpheniramine (Antihistamine) – Relieves allergy symptoms like runny nose and sneezing.

- **Uses:**
  - Recommended for **dry cough** without mucus.
  - Helps in conditions like allergic cough, throat irritation, and common cold-related cough.

- **Side Effects:**
  - Drowsiness, dry mouth, dizziness, nausea.

### **Key Differences:**
| Feature         | Grilinctus BM | Tusq DX |
|---------------|--------------|------------|
| Type of Cough | Wet/ Mucus Cough | Dry/ Irritating Cough |
| Action | Thins and clears mucus | Suppresses cough reflex |
| Main Ingredients | Bromhexine, Terbutaline, Guaifenesin | Dextromethorphan, Phenylephrine, Chlorpheniramine |
| Use Cases | Bronchitis, mucus-related cough | Allergic cough, throat irritation |
| Drowsiness | No | Yes (due to antihistamine) |

### **Conclusion:**
- If you have a cough with **mucus/phlegm**, **Grilinctus BM** is better.
- If you have a **dry cough** or an **allergic cough**, **Tusq DX** is more suitable.

It’s always best to consult a doctor before using any medication.
