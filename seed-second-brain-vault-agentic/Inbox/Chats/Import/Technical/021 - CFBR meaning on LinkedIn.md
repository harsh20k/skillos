# CFBR meaning on LinkedIn

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

On LinkedIn, **CFBR** usually stands for **“Comment For Better Reach”**.  

It’s part of engagement groups or informal strategies where people drop comments like *“CFBR”* under a post. The idea is that commenting increases the visibility of the post in LinkedIn’s algorithm (more engagement → more reach).  

So when you see “CFBR” in the comments, it doesn’t have a literal meaning in the discussion—it’s just a signal that the person is helping boost the post’s visibility.  

👉 In short: It’s a growth/engagement hack, not an actual conversation.
