# Clean wood floor tips

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

Yes. You can clean wood floors in a Canadian apartment with mop water and a wood-safe floor cleaner. Use these steps:

1. **Dilute properly:** Mix a small amount of hardwood or laminate-safe cleaner in water (as per label).  
2. **Wring the mop well:** The mop should be damp, not wet. Excess water can seep into joints and damage wood.  
3. **Mop along the grain:** Work in small sections.  
4. **Dry immediately:** Use a clean microfiber cloth or dry mop to remove remaining moisture.  

Avoid steam mops and all-purpose cleaners with ammonia or vinegar—they dull or damage finish.
