## 🩸 What Does HbA1c of 14% Mean?

HbA1c measures **average blood sugar over the past 2–3 months**. Here’s the reference:

| HbA1c (%) | Interpretation         | Estimated Avg Glucose (mg/dL) |
|-----------|------------------------|-------------------------------|
| < 5.7     | Normal                 | < 117                         |
| 5.7 – 6.4 | Prediabetes            | 117–137                       |
| ≥ 6.5     | Diabetes (diagnostic) | ≥ 137                         |
| **14.0**  | 🔴 **Severely uncontrolled diabetes** | **~355 mg/dL average**      |

## 🧭 What Should Be Done Immediately?

### 🔹 **Medical Steps**
- **Consult a diabetologist** or physician **urgently**.
- Start or revise **diabetes medication** (very likely needs insulin).
- Monitor **fasting and post-meal sugar levels** daily.
- Check **kidney function (creatinine, urine microalbumin)** and **eye screening**.

### 🔹 **Lifestyle Changes**
- Strict **no sugar, no sweets, no sugary drinks**.
- Low-carb, high-fiber meals.
- Moderate exercise (walking), unless advised rest.
- **Daily blood sugar monitoring**.
- Regular follow-ups every 1–2 weeks initially.
