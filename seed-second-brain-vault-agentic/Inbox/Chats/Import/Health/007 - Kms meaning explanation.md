# Kms meaning explanation

**Priority:** Medium

## Conversation

### Assistant

Obsidian has no fixed vault size limit. It depends on your device storage and file system.  

- **Local vaults:** Limited only by available disk space. Users have reported vaults of **10–50 GB** working fine.  
- **Performance factors:** Markdown files load instantly; lag starts mainly when there are **tens of thousands of notes**, many large attachments (PDFs, images, videos), or heavy plugins.  
- **Optimization:**  
  - Split huge vaults into smaller ones by area or project.  
  - Avoid indexing non-Markdown assets.  
  - Disable unused community plugins.  

For best responsiveness, keep total note count under about **20 000** and total attachments under **a few GB** per vault.
