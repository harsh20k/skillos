Nothing exists except God. But the perception, that something exists other than [[God]] is the real [[Maya]], the root cause of all suffering. 

Perception of multi-formed world is because of [[Maya]] or it is [[Maya]]. Some are rich, some are poor, something is alive and something is dead. All this constitute the domain of [[Maya]]. 

```timestamp 
 06:20
 ```
Whatever there is, [[Sri Krishna]] has taken that form. But this can never be conceived by a devotee, until there is importance of what is visible externally. Male, female, things to consume, convinced of their truth, gives importance to that form, instead of the eternal divine nature of Divine behind the external form. Unless devotee doesn't give up this way of witnessing the world he will never be able to cognise that [[Sri Krishna]] is in this or that, behind every thing that exists. This delays [[Self-realisation]].

```timestamp 
 07:05
 ```
[[God]] is the cause of every form, and **God is every form**. 
But giving importance to the form (object, desired object), and the relationships to the forms prevents us find [[God]]. 

That's why when we find our [[Gurudeva]] the first blow is on these relationships we have thought of as real. 

```timestamp 
 07:31
 ```
Blessed are those who accept these blows to the ego and all relationships to the forms, and then become a one and only devotee of [[God]]. Without the grace of true [[Guru]] we cannot remove the bondages of these relationships. Each utterance of [[Gurudeva]] will free us from the world each thought at a time. Only a few blessed souls follow the [[Guru]]'s words verbatim and do the prescribed [[Sadhana]]. Rest just do what their [[Man]] says.
