## STATEMENT OF PURPOSE TO STUDY IN CANADA

### Introduction

I, Harsh Pandey, have been accepted into the Master of Applied Computer Science (MACSc) graduate program at Dalhousie University, Nova Scotia, Canada. I am writing this Statement of Purpose to express my academic interests, career goals, and the reasons behind choosing Dalhousie University and Canada for my graduate education. My aspiration to pursue this program stems from my deep-seated passion for technology, creative problem-solving, and my commitment to contribute meaningfully to the evolving tech landscape in India.

### Academic and Technical Background

My journey into the world of computing began during my school years when I discovered my fascination with computers and programming. I spent countless nights in high school working on C++ code, exploring how machines think and behave. Inspired by my uncles, who worked at a renowned tech firm in the USA, I was motivated to take my passion seriously. During my B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication, I undertook projects using Python, MATLAB, and C++, and in my final year, I developed a solution to maintain digital ownership integrity in photography. This experience deepened my understanding of real-world problem-solving, enhanced my collaboration skills, and strengthened my mathematical foundation.

Courses such as Digital Electronics left a lasting impact, as I was fascinated by the logical systems that underpin all of computing. I also actively pursued professional training in Advanced Java and web application development, creating applications for my hostel’s LAN network that encouraged interactive communication among students. An introductory AI course in my final semester inspired me to build a basic Ludo-playing bot using machine learning concepts, further sparking my interest in intelligent systems.

### Professional and Creative Experience

After graduation, I joined Infosys as a Systems Engineer where I worked on large-scale software development and optimization projects. Collaborating across teams, I gained hands-on experience in Java, web technologies, APIs, debugging, and managing complete software life cycles. My contributions to backend optimization for an e-commerce client were well recognized and helped solidify my practical technical foundation.

In 2015, I transitioned into freelancing, focusing on animation and design. This path took me through diverse projects across advertising, marketing, and music industries, where I collaborated with talented artists and innovators. My portfolio includes hand-drawn animations, promotional videos, and interactive visuals. For instance, I created an animated campaign for an eco-friendly brand and used word cloud generation through machine learning in a music video to enhance visual storytelling.

In parallel, I explored technical tools such as Adobe Suite, Blender, 3DS Max, and Maya, and even combined programming logic with animation to develop interactive media. I continually upskilled by taking courses in Data Science (using SAS, R, Python) and Swift programming, the latter helping me build apps to manage a growing library of over 300 creative projects.

### Why Graduate Study Now

As my work matured at the intersection of technology and creativity, I recognized the need to formalize my skills and gain a broader, structured understanding of applied computer science. I’m particularly interested in areas such as data management, mobile computing, and user experience design, which are vital in today’s software-driven landscape. A graduate degree will not only refine my technical abilities but also sharpen my problem-solving and innovation skills.

### Why Dalhousie University

Dalhousie University stood out for its unique blend of academic rigor and industry relevance. The Master of Applied Computer Science program offers courses like Advanced Cloud Architecting, Mobile Computing, Data Management, and Designing for User Experience — all of which align with my interests in building efficient and user-centric digital solutions. I am particularly excited about Dalhousie’s association with DeepSense, a groundbreaking initiative that uses AI and ML to solve real-world challenges in the ocean economy. It presents an unparalleled opportunity to work on applied research with industry impact, while the Entrepreneurship option further enables me to explore the business side of technology development.

### Why Canada

Canada represents an ideal destination for higher education due to its progressive research culture, global reputation in applied sciences, and welcoming multicultural environment. The country’s commitment to academic excellence, post-study work opportunities, and inclusive values offers international students like me a chance to grow both professionally and personally. Having close family members settled in Canada has given me a deeper understanding of its work culture and lifestyle, reinforcing my confidence in choosing it for my graduate studies.

### Future Plans and Return to India

After completing my program at Dalhousie University, I plan to return to India and apply my skills in roles such as Business Intelligence Analyst or Technology Consultant. I aim to work on real-world challenges in fields like healthcare, education, and creative media. Inspired by my mother, who always emphasized the transformative power of education, I hope to contribute to more accessible learning platforms and smarter diagnostic tools for underserved communities.

This program will empower me to work on innovative solutions and drive technological advancement in India. I am also rooted here personally and financially. My tuition for the first year (16,000 CAD) is already paid, and I have a GIC of 12,000 CAD along with sufficient funds secured for the next year. My family is financially secure with rental income, property, and ongoing businesses in India. My mother is well-supported, and I look forward to returning to contribute to our family’s future and the broader Indian tech industry.

### Conclusion

I am eager to join Dalhousie University’s MACSc program and immerse myself in its academic, entrepreneurial, and research opportunities. With my diverse background in software, design, and animation, coupled with a passion for applied computing, I believe this program will enable me to evolve into a well-rounded professional ready to make meaningful contributions. I kindly request the approval of my study permit to fulfill this vision.
