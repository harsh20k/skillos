# A Cry for the Divine Mother

The fight for the [[Divine Mother]] is a constant battle and war. The [[Maya]] of the lord is very strong and is fuelled by the same power of God from which we get our power. Only through the grace of the [[Guru]], we can have the strength to face against the forces of [[Maya]]. 

---
In our daily life we get so many opportunities to choose between maya and [[Divine Mother]], and it might be hard at first to recognise. But with practice and [[kripa]] we start to witness them clearly just like two plain options in front of us.

![[Pasted image 20250126093416.jpg]]

Even with [[Satsanga]] its not given that we will go for [[God]] each time because we have practice from our countless lifetimes to always choose maya over [[God]]. Therefore, we should be much more thoughtful and elaborate in our decisions and indecisions. Without our own effort and intention [[kripa]] can do only little. If our intention is always to put [[God]] in front seat then we will witness the true power of [[Divine Mother]] and her immense love for us. 

---
[[Divine Mother]]'s affection for us is not limited like any worldly attachment rooted in personal desires and predisposition. Her love is all-encompassing without any limits of space and time. We just have to have that intention to look for her love. The intention for find her and that craving to find that restful place in her lap. Everything else will fall exactly in the right place and every obstacle(perceived) will act like a way to uplift our spiritual life to a whole new level.

Since we cannot measure her love and care for us, we can't even imagine to repay her or love her back with that intensity. But she is content in the fact that her child is trying to pry through the dirt and look for her true [[Mother]]. She will be content and happy to finally see her child throw away every toy that she provides, every distraction she gives to him and cry only for her mother, nothing less, nothing more. 

---
[[Premanandji]] gives us hope that we should have all the emotions reserved for the [[Divine Mother]]. If we are angry we should be angry with the [[Mother]]. If we are lustful we should be hungrily lust to be with her body and cling to her bosom. If we are feeling hate we should hate her with as much intensity as possible and tell her that we hate her that she put us here in this world to suffer.

Any link we can create in our waking moment we create with her with any feeling will become our salvation. If not in our lifetime then at the moment of death we will be welcomed by her with open arms and warm love of her body. 

---
Let's pray, or shout or cry whatever is our language of love and direct it towards our [[Mother]] and ask to free us from all this suffering and to show us the way to our home on her lap.
