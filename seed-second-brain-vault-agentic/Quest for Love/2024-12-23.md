# The Soul’s Return to Divine Embrace
---

[[Vivek]] is the faculty of the soul that distinguishes from true and false, from what is right and what is wrong and from what is [[Sat]] and what is [[Asat]]. 

As [[Premanandji]] says "binu [[Satsanga]] [[Vivek]] na hoi, Guru binu bhav nidhi tare na koi". It means that without [[Satsanga]] we cannot separate the truth from the lies. The soul of each person has already these qualities but these are only expressed when the ignorance is cleared up by the grace of saints and the graceful words they say to us. 

![[Pasted image 20241223084851.jpg]]

Through the grace of God almighty we find satsanga and by the grace of satsanga ignorance is cleansed that has been covering the soul for lifetimes. Then only the soul qualities like [[Vivek]], true [[Love]] and other eternal qualities within start to reveal themselves. 

The identification with the body is thus cut away and real identification as the soul, servant or lover of [[God]] is established or one might say re-discovered. Since we all are eternal and [[God]] is also eternal our relationship with him is also eternal and cannot be broken by any amount of ignorance we might think we have. 

Just like darkness is not a real thing, cold does not exist fundamentally, ignorance has no real fundamental existence. As the light shines the darkness seems to disappear and as the sun warms the world the cold escapes into nothingness, the sun of [[Satsanga]] rises and all the imperfections and shortcomings of body and mind complex just vanish and our true qualities of eternal love, joy and [[Vivek]] shine forth. 

The biggest regret of life is that although we got the opportunity of inquiring this through this human body and mind which is the only one that can understand the nature of ourselves, we got side-tracked and wasted our lives in the pursuit of bodily pleasures. We might not get this chance for a very long time. The value of this body is greater than the angels and demons, because through the true use of this we can transcend this cycle of death and re-birth. 

At the end of life, I don't wish to have this regret above all else that I could not face my beloved divine. I could not look at her face and quench by eternal thirst. However, I am already in her lap and I know that she will reveal herself to me not by my effort but by her merciful nature. Through her grace I found a true [[Guru]] and through her grace if I can follow just one word of my Guru, I know that I am saved from the fiery end of life and the cycle of suffering. No amount of ignorance will take away the fact that I am faithful to her in my heart at least! 

![[Pasted image 20241223084916.jpg]]

If this is not Vivek then I don't want any other kind. That I, a repugnant being steeped in worldliness and bodily pleasures is her own.

[[God]] never breaks the trust of the devotee is the only [[Vivek]] that I wish for. For that is the only knowledge that can survive in the face of extreme bodily suffering and mental agonies. And it ensures my seat on her lap once again lost by my own ignorance in the annuls of time immemorial. 

Let's go back to our home now, it is time. She is waiting and searching for so long now. She gave us the world because we were crying for it. It is time to go back to her because she is crying for us, searching us like a mad woman looking for her lost child. I know that she regrets her own doing of giving us this world but, if we resolve for even once to come back to her this time she will not make the same mistake and won't let us go no matter how much we cry for the world!!

Let's go back..
