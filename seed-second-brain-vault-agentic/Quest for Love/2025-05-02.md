# Merging into the Infinite: Devotion to Divine Mother

Shree Radhaaaa, Radhaa, Radhaa, Radhaaaa. 

Her name, my [[Mother]]'s name is enough to remove the suffering of the world. Actually, her name is the only thing that will end all the suffering of the universe. 

![[Pasted image 20250502094233.jpg]]

The word of God, any name of God, any from the thousands of names of God have the same power to put a full stop of man's incessant needs and wants of life. The continuous stream of consciousness of man is flowing towards the world, I want to do this, that or I need to buy this or that or I want him or her. His consciousness is completely free to be put anywhere he wants, he chooses because of practice to put this in the world and thus seals his fate to feel the pains and pleasures of the body. 

When we take God's name from our lips our consciousness temporarily merges with the infinite consciousness. We might not feeling anything special but just like the practice of world-consciousness as we continue to practice God-consciousness through the repetition of God's name our whole being gets purified and we start on a journey of self discovery. 

This search will lead us to our final goal i.e. merging of our consciousness into the one consciousness of divine mother. 

The love of [[Divine Mother]] is not measurable and all our hopes and dreams cannot justify the beauty of my mother's love. If even for a moment mother let us taste that sweet love we will never forsake it for anything in the world. 

Divine mother is forever ours and we are forever ours, we just have to merge our consciousness with her. We can only do that by devoting ourselves completely in the feet of the [[Gurudeva]] and our mother.
