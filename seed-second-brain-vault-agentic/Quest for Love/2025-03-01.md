# The Infinite Grace of the Divine Mother

The belief that the God in front of us is real is a great accomplishment of the devotee not because of his own any efforts but because of the causeless mercy of the divine. 

---
The grace and mercy of the [[Divine Mother]] is infinite, yet we do not admire or respect this [[kripa]] that she is showering upon us. If only we could start appreciating this mercy and grace that she has upon us poor souls we would become her beloved children devoid of any corruption of bodily desires and motives.

No matter our relationship with the divine we have to remember that she is ours and we are her's no matter what. No matter how bad things we do or how many bad habits we have that keep us away from her in our depths of our hearts we should always remember that she will never forsake us, for we are her's and she is the only one that is ours.

![[Pasted image 20250301085523.jpg]]

---
In the countless graces that the [[Divine Mother]] and [[Gurudeva]] have upon us, the very first is the gift of body of man. This is the only body and species that can do bhajan and achieve the final goal of every soul that is god-realisation or god perception. The second and most beautiful grace is the gift of [[Satsanga]]. This is the most divine gifts that God only gives to those who are very dear to him and he can't wait to stay away from that devotee. When [[Divine Mother]] can't stay another lifetime without her child she gives this gift to them and seals the fate of her child that he will meet her in this lifetime of the devotee. 

---
The final grace is the gift of [[Gurudeva]], which is not merely a gift, It is the incarnate form of the [[Divine Mother]] for the devotees or her children so that they can follow her words and perform everything according to the divine laws and make sure that her stray flock returns back to her within this lifetime. 

We have received all three of these graces and yet if we do not respect what we have received then we cannot find the ultimate happiness in this short life. With every passing breath we are moving towards the end of our ephemeral lives. If even God comes in front of us and say that we will not find God in this lifetime, we should reject such words and within us we should have that unshakable faith in the [[kripa]] of the [[Divine Mother]] that we shall find her right here and now. 

---
No amount of external failures or ridicule can lessen the relationship of a mother and a child. No matter how big a failure a son is his mother will always love and welcome him with open loving arms and ask her if he has eaten today or not. 

[[Divine Mother]] is the ocean of this kind of love and no matter how many failures we have seen or how many people belittle or disrespect her child she will always welcome her child with open and loving arms and ask her if he has eaten something. 

Now if we cannot feel gratitude inspite of such great graces and gifts to us we can just try with all our strength to feel the same gratitude and feel sad that we cannot appreciate the gifts that our beloved mother has given us. Then also our job will be done and with the grace of the [[Guru]] we will complete our objective of God-consciousness in this life or after the end.
