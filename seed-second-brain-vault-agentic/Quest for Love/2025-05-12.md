Wow! What a wonderful turn of events. Now I have a partner with whom I can go to my [[Gurudeva]]'s ashrams and attend or try to attend long meditations. Now I do not feel threatened or even alone at these retreats and ashrams. 

If only we could listen to God and wait for his divine plans to come to our attention. But instead we are busy making our own plans and schedules. The plan passed by divine is the most beautiful and shocks the devotee to his core.

I could not have imagined that now wherever I am I can talk about God, my partner, my wife too has a predisposition for spirituality like me. Now she is the one who encourages me to attend these long meditations where I would have left after half way, she encourages me to stay and meditate fully for the full length at least. How beautiful!!
