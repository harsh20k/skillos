# Harmony in the Pilgrimage of Life
 
It is worth noting that sometimes [[Satsanga]] tells us to not seek the love of family and other close loved ones, but on the other hand tells us that mother and father are like [[God]] himself. This might seem contradictory to a beginner, but through the grace of [[Guru]], this resolves into a beautiful harmony. 

![[Pasted image 20241229090224.jpg|413]]

It is [[Dharma]] of the body to take care of the mother and father of the body, and it is the [[Dharma]] of the real me the soul to love [[God]] and [[Guru]]. 

---
There is not contradiction in this path of love towards God on which we all are walking. Like walking on a pilgrimage the whole crowd walks along, sometimes chatting to the nearby devotee but they don't stop and forget where they are going or why they have gathered there at the first place. Exactly like that we all are walking towards God and for a finite time we are given this body and with it all its relationships, mother, father, son, wife etc.

We take care of each other living beautifully together serving each other along the way, but we can't forget where we are going or the whole purpose of this human life. Together we can save each other from the various pitfalls if we have the goal set in our minds. But if we forget our true goal the whole idea of family and friends becomes poisoned by the deadly poison of selfishness. When [[God]] is the aim of everyone in a family then their every action becomes a beautiful symphony, singing together the lord's name. Together they can make this difficult path very easy and lovely.

![[Pasted image 20241229090453.jpg]]

Although I tried to run away from home, again and again I came back, because I thought I was not strong enough, but now I know that is the will of my true mother to live harmoniously with family she has given me with and that will become a part of my [[Sadhana]]. 

It is not always easy to keep the objective at our sights however, because through the power of [[Maya]] and body consciousness we get sidetracked into our body selfishness and start to advantage of these people who were given to us by the grace of [[God]], for making our path easier and more enjoyable.
