# Power of God's Name

The power of God's name cannot be realised just by saying it once or twice.

When the consciousness slowly absorbs the tremendous power embedded in the seed form in the lord's name, it starts to acquire its normal form.

![[Pasted image 20250321213320.jpg]]

---
The Guru's word is also like taking the God's name. When we follow the teachings of the Guru preceptor we are in the boundary of the lord, or at the house divine. 

No matter our condition, no matter how bad deeds we have done in our lifetime we can find that home whenever we realise its importance and set our objective and goal.
