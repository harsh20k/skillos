# Quest for Divine Love in the Chaos of Life
I want one thing only in this life. To remember [[God]] continuously and ceaselessly. His love is the only cage that will set me free from every shortcoming I have in this little thing that I have which I call "**life**". The process of finding this love is my quest for meaning in such [[chaos]].

![[Pasted image 20241217083822.jpg]]

This quest for meaning is the only thing that is keeping me sane and ordered. If God come in front of me right now I wouldn't even recongnise. This act of finding him myself with the resources I have, will give this life and the achievement of ultimate goal meaning and relevance. Right now even though God is looking at us right now it has no meaning for us because of our ignorance. The act of looking for this the [[Guru]] given [[sadhana]]. The act of love that is the action of love. 

Even though, there is no guarantee that we achieve everything we aim for doesn't discourage us from not pursuing it. Likewise is this [[Quest for Love]] and meaning through love. 

When we find love we will have achieved everything then why make other aims and goals that even after achievement will only give a taste of burnt ash in our mouth of life.
