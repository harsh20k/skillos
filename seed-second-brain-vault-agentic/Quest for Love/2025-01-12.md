# Shining the Light on Truth

It might not feel that writing is such an important tool for anyone on the surface. If we look closer however, we will find the real reason why it is so powerful for one’s growth and evolution. 

#### So why we think we can just evolve slowly living the same life everyday?

It is an illusion that by living the same life, by being in the company of the same people, by being surrounded with the same literature we can somehow improve our conditions. If we keep doing the same routines everyday we will be standing at the exact same spot where we are standing right now. We are just the sum total of all our habits right now. Then how can we think that suddenly our life will take a different path or route one day. This assumption is not at all rooted in the truth and just a false hope we might have deep inside.

![[Pasted image 20250112103920.jpg]]

Also this desire inside is not even known by the people who have them. All this confusion and false hope only works when we don’t shine light on it. Once we actually think and then shine the light of knowledge on this false knowledge it will crumple and evaporate like a wisp of smoke because it has no identity. 

This takes, however continuous efforts to put our thinking into words which will then remove the mental cobwebs and the words uttered about the truth will banish this ignorance. 

When we do stand up in the face of the light there are no more false identities, false hopes but what remains is the burnt down version of the truth, the essence of what’s real which will then help to grow us into our best potential.

All the lies we have every uttered, the burden of every falsehood ever thought is on our shoulders and we carry it until the light of the truth is light upon us.
