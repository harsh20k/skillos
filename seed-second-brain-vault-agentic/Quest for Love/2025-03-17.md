# The Longing for the Divine Mother's Smile

I love you [[Divine Mother]], that's the only thing that I wish to know. All the universe of complete knowledge does not have any allure for me. All I care about is how I can get to you. How can I see you smile looking at me, her beloved son. 

Only once I want to see that smile of my mother and my whole being will devoid of this suffering.

![[Pasted image 20250317190956.jpg]]

Let me see you once and I will not ask again because then I won't have anything else to ask for. 

Let me cry for you... Even better than looking at your smile is the desire to cry for that smile. This torturous wait and slow burning in my heart to feel you close to me as real as I feel this body and air. 

Oh [[Divine Mother]] I have heard from the great ones that you easily give fulfilment to such kinds of wishes. Please grant this too to me. Either come in front of me not as all the forms, neither hidden behind the Guru's bodily temple. Or give me that supreme torture in the heart, that ultimate longing for that divine vision.
