# Longing for the Divine Mother

Today [[Premanandji]] said something that really resonated to me. He said that we cannot measure or exchange [[God]] by doing a certain amount of [[Sadhana]]. He said that the only thing that is important to get the divine mercy or the love of [[Divine Mother]] is the act of feeling for her. Just like a son living far from his mother sometimes just starts feeling sad and long for his mother, when we feel that same longing for [[Divine Mother]] she cannot resist staying away from us.

---
Wherever we feel that strong attachment of the heart in the world if we turn that attachment and mine-ness to [[Mother]] then she has no choice but to come running to her child.

![[Pasted image 20250124085928.jpg]]

What if we cry for our parents, we are lucky that we can feel that attachment we just have to turn that attachment towards the [[Divine Mother]] who in every birth has been watching over us and caring for us immensely. 

---
**The love for [[Divine Mother]] is not a very simple thing or trivial thing** to have. [[Premanandji]] says it is the **most important spiritual practice** that is awarded to those who have been on the spiritual path for long time and crave for that final union. Crying for the [[Divine Mother]] and longing for her is the ultimate gift that [[God]] gives to their true devotees who come to the lord not for the things of the world or any powers or any other imaginable things natural or supernatural, but for only [[God]]'s sake. These true devotees want nothing from [[God]] but they want God himself. These are the devotees that recieve that [[Divine Love]] and start to feel that mineness relationship with [[God]]. 

Just like we say "My Mother", "My wife" with love and devotion, if we say "My Mother" for the [[Mother]] our real mother, that is the culmination of our [[Bhakti]]. That is the end of our suffering and the dawn of true love in our hearts long tainted with selfish and bodily desires and attachment. 

![[Pasted image 20250124090021.jpg]]

---
I remember a beautiful talk delivered by Swami Sarvapriyananda, he was talking about the simplicity of [[Bhakti]]. He said that all of us have a long list of people and things that we consider ours, like my parents, my home, my car, my phone etc. Just like that we only have to add one more item to this list "My God(in the relationship we like)". With this practice we will witness that this single item in this list will engulf everything in our life until every waking moment we will be thinking about "My [[Divine Mother]]" and with extreme devotion only seen in renowned saints and fakirs.
