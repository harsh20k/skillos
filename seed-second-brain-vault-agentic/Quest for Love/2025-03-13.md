# The only Objective That Matters: Remembering the Divine Mother

The primary reason of suffering as told by the scriptures is our forgetfulness of God. When our centre of consciousness shifts from the [[Divine Mother]] to the outward forms and our own body and its desires then we forsake our divine kingdom within. 

No matter what we try to do outwardly, no matter how many desires we fulfil of this body and how many relationships we build in relation to this body we will never find even an atom of peace and happiness. 

![[Pasted image 20250313105943.jpg]]

When we set a worldly goal like getting a job or passing an exam we give our very best in the pursuit of that objective. If we by chance accomplish this objective we might for a mere second might get a relief of finishing something but we won't find that peace or happiness that we thought we might get.

For infinite incarnations we have been doing the same thing, creating various objectives for ourselves and then feeling the dust in our mouth by either accomplishing them or watching ourselves fail completely.

---
The only objective that matters in this world is to find God. To find [[Divine Mother]] we must at least set our sight on that divine objective. 

When we get [[Satsanga]] then we realise the importance of god remembrance and our powerlessness to realise our goal. Through Guru given wisdom our powerlessness realisation true power of God and Guru starts to flow in us and we find ourselves being continuously attracted towards our goal.
