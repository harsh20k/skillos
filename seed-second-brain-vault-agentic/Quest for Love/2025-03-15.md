# The Devotee's Path: A Life of Divine Grace

A devotee's life is very beautiful and full of happiness. Every moment is full of divine surprises and emotions divine. All his actions be them worldly or spiritual become beyond the three gunas. Waking consciousness becomes merged with the consciousness of the divine. 

[[Divine Mother]] swallows the consciousness of such a devotee by force of love and Guru's words. Any action of such a devotee becomes a spiritual offering to the divine mother. With utmost faith and spiritual fervour his day becomes like a flower offered in the deities's feet. 

![[Pasted image 20250315094407.jpg]]

By continuous attachment with the divine mother and longing for her only one thought remains in the back of the mind of the devotee and that is how to close to the one cause that is the cause of everything. With patience and gratitude he only witnesses divine mother's kripa and amazing and awe inspiring leela.

If a tragedy befalls such a devotee then also all he can see is the divine blessings and kripa. He no longer wishes for the riches and fame of this world and only wish to please God through his actions and inactions alike. 

---
Nothing can upset or incite anxiety in the heart of this devotee and he is already free with happiness bubbling inside of him. 

He has a direct line of communication with the divine mother and everything his divine mother do for him is for the spiritual upliftment. All the thoughts of devotee start from the divine mother and end at her feet. Hence becoming free of material desires and having complete faith in the guru's words he has already reserved his seat for the ultimate success of human life that is moksha or liberation. 

If there are still some aspirations for bodily pleasures left in the life of the devotee he does not need to worry about them since they cannot bring this devotee down and a faith falls over him that everything will be corrected in him through mother's infinite grace and love.
