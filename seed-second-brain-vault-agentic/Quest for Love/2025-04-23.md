The colour of the universe is the colour that is in the eyes of the beholder. If the observer is saturated with God consciousness then the whole universe is coloured with a divine colour, a divine aura is everywhere. 

But with the glasses of duality this world is [[Maya]], a play of good and evil, birth and death. The responsibility of the devotee is to get rid of this duality ridden glasses and wear permanent lenses coloured with Guru's love and God's presence.

Doing so will change the whole outlook of the devotee and all he sees is God everywhere and anywhere. No even the murderer will be visible to the Godly man as anything other than God. 

---
Oh divine mother colour these eyes of mine with your love so I can also see you everywhere I look. Change this heart of mine so it does not hold anything other than your love and memory.
