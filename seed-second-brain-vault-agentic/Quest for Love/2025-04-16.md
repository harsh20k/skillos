# Freedom in Divine Remembrance

We are all completely free of the bondage if we have God in our consciousness. 

The instant we come in contact with the body and its desires and forget that these are not my desires, and mistakenly start to feel its desires as our own we become entangled and lost and instantly become a slave of the body and our own senses. 

---
The same thing happens in the opposite direction also. When we give up these desires for a moment and start to feel the presence of divinity and start to feel that love and happiness within we are instantly free of this maya and the bondage associated with it. 

![[Pasted image 20250416092605.jpg]]

---
All these experiences are a product of Guru bhakti and complete surrender at the feet of the [[Divine Mother]].
