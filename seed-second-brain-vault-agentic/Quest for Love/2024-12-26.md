# By His Grace, Uplifted, Cradled in Divine Love
---
## **[[Cradled in Divine Love]]**

So I did just that as my [[Guru]] told me to do. It is his direct blessing that enabled me to try to finally give up this body too for him, by following his energisation exercises. I will try my level best to follow his dictum verbatim from now on. I have also downloaded the video on energisation exercises and through that I can simply follow along the saint doing energisation exercise.

![[Pasted image 20241226082304.jpg]]

With body and mind both in the service of my [[Gurudeva]] I feel much more relaxed and calm. There is a bit of carefreeness that I feel now which was not present before. Just like this there will come a time when I will be able to truly follow him through his teachings and then directly in flesh and bones. His one incarnation I could not witness but soon I will witness him walking along me throughout my life whatever is left. 

![[Pasted image 20241226082825.jpg]]

The joy I am feeling cannot be described in words and the soul upliftment that he is always imparting to my thirsty soul is always awe-inspiring. I am his and he is mine. I will never be worthy of his love and guidance, yet since he has promised it to me then its worthless to argue otherwise. His will will come true today or any moment whenever he wishes! 

![[Pasted image 20241226082604.jpg]]

---
When I look at myself I see this impotent little boy incapable of anything, incapable of even crying for his mother, and when I look at [[Guru|him]] all my doubts and worries melt away and I start functioning like a perfect tyre being rotated by his motor. 

![[Pasted image 20241226082729.jpg]]

As [[Premanandji]] describes, I have received the final pass and no one can take this away from me. Not because I am worthy or not(I will never be), but because he is by my side, my mother is behind me holding me on her divine lap, caressing me each and every moment. 

---
The falseness of this world is self- evident with only a little bit of enquiry. And I feel that intermittently by his [[Grace]].
