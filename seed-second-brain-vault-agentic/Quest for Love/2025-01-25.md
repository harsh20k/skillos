# The Power of Naam-Jap: A Journey Beyond Fear

The most difficult thing in this world is to do constant [[naam-jap]]. Its very difficult to do because it is the most important thing to do in our lives. The lord always stays with the soul who constants remembers him. [[Divine Mother]] stays with the child who is constantly saying her name or the name she likes to hear.

---
Everyone is scared of something or the other, since the announcement of this wedding I have been experiencing numerous fears and all of them are pointless if I look at them and all are being created by the uncertainty. If we analyse our fears and uncertainties we will observe that, all of those have become powerful because we don't have enough [[God]] remembrance in our lives.

![[Pasted image 20250125090029.jpg]]

Let's take an example of a fear I was thinking about. I was dreaming about me offering 10 rupee notes in the wedding ceremony and someone in the crowd yelling that it might not be enough. And then I was creatively thinking about various things I could say back at someone in a very clever fashion. Now what is the foundation of this fear? I see no real fact supporting this fear yet I actually feel the fear regarding this scenario. The only reason is that I am not doing enough bhajan and [[naam-jap]] in my day until the wedding.

---
All the fears melt away in front of [[God]] and god's devotees. Who can stand in the way of the lover of God. I feel energised by the fact that if someone disrespects me or someone doesn't respect me or my family enough our karma will get sorted or the worse that can happen in this wedding is that I just drop dead! What's the fuss then. I am already in my [[Mother]]'s protection and in the ship of my [[Gurudeva]]. Then what is the need to worry about anything in this world.

![[Pasted image 20250125090146.jpg]]

Whatever happens is the will of the lord I will just perform my duties with as much strength that I can muster. No amount of fear can stop that if I have the name of my [[Mother]] on my lips. If I have her in my heart every obstacle will become like a flower and an elevator in this journey.

---
The only fear worth having is the fear to forget the lord's sweet name. What if after this wedding and marriage I start to forget the lord? What if with all the different pleasures of new country and beautiful sceneries I forget what my aim is in this life? That is the fear everyone should have because through that fear we might be incentivised to stay in constant protection of my [[Mother]]. 

The power of fear is enough to paralyse even the mightiest, but if we have the fear of forgetting our [[Mother]] divine then only we can be truly free.
