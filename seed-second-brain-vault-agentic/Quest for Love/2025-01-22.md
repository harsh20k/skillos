# Embrace of the Divine Mother: Trust Amid Chaos

The divinity within everyone and the divine will within every event is only visible when the true love for [[God]] has taken hold of the heart. Otherwise, the lost soul always looking for the pleasures of the self, wanders here and there in search to be happy. 

---
True divine love can only come in the heart by being in the blessings of a true man of God. By listening to his words, by following his directions and by giving everything up to them true love starts to take root in the heart of the devotee.

![[Pasted image 20250122085843.jpg]]

In the times of struggle like this wedding its been very hard to keep the aim in the sight. Its very easy to go with the flow of all the chaos. But with divine blessings and with the little love that I have for my [[Divine Mother]], although I have doubts and anxieties, there is still peace and trust in the [[Divine Mother]] that everything will go as she planned, no one can change that plan. 

---
**This angst inside, then also would be according to her plan. This worry would also be through her own wish in service of some grand plan for me!**

That is a very liberating thought I think. If the worries and trials and hinderances are given to the devotee for his own good and by the [[Divine Mother]] herself then why would a true devotee shy away from taking this burden? He will happily be worried all day and night having solace in the fact that he is just a piece in the divine plan of the [[Mother]]. 

Wow! Just Wow! How merciful is the mother to dispel various negative thoughts in the devotee's heart. She is the true mother devoid of any oversight or any shortcomings which earthly mothers are often accused of.

My [[Divine Mother]] never loses sight of me, her beloved child for even an instant. With great care she creates situations for the child that might feel difficult for the child to overcome but they are for their own good and ultimate well-being.
