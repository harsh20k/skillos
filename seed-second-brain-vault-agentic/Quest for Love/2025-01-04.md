# The Wailing Child and the Loving Mother

I know you are by my side by the grace of the true [[Guru]] you sent to me. I know by his words that you love me beyond words. Through him, I know that you will always protect me, you have always protected me. I pray to thee my mother, come in my inner vision so I can finally witness you as the [[Guru]] sees you, as the saints see you. I want to see you oh divine mother.

I know you are listening to my prayers and watching over me every moment. This knowledge I seem to have that you are always near and dear is also your doing. But this knowledge is now not enough, I want to be by your side, hand in hand. I want to sit on your lap and speak gibberish once again oh mother. I want to play with you like a child plays with his mother. When will that day come, I wait for it with intense longing.

The love you have for me its time to stop controlling it let it express itself and show thyself to me in your full glory and warmth. I don't know how you look so I imagine you in the terms of earthly measures, whatever is my capability of imagination and thought you are the most beautiful thing I can imagine.

If this imagined form is also your doing then also its not enough because that doesn't solve my problem of my existence, this does not quench my thirst for you mother! Come to me mother so I can embrace your form in flesh and bones or whatever form you wish. I just want to be near you every second, every minute and every hour of my waking life. Claim my life, my waking state, my dreaming state and my deep sleep. Show me, so I can feel without doubt that you are the lap that I sleep on every night, you are the air that I breathe in every second and you are everything that I have desired throughout eternity. 

![[Pasted image 20250104104016.jpg]]

Sometimes it makes me sad, sometimes I am angry, and mostly I feel the love that you shower. Its not reasonable from my side to ask from you anything since I do not have any qualifications. Still, I am asking to hurry up, I feel living each moment without you is just wearing me down, killing me. The saints say that when we get so anxious to meet you that every breath becomes a burden, just like walking on a burning coals the anxiousness to step away then only you come to your devotee. I do not know when I will feel that, but I do know, that the way I feel right now, I haven't felt anything like this for anything in the world. I thought I was hard-hearted, but then you gave me a heart that only feels for you. 

Its time now mother, come to your wailing and weeping child, clean his face and eyes covered with delusion and [[Maya]], so he can see you as you are, true, love incarnate.
