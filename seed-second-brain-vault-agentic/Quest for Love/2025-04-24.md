# Return of The Prodigal Soul

What could be more re-assuring than God himself saying that no one in this entire universe can touch my devotee, or anyone who takes His name?

When the owner of this entire universe is proclaiming that then it is a shame that still we harbour anxieties of the world, of the future, past and present. Once we feel the devotion for the divine we will be forever calm and without any disillusionment. 

Oh! This wonderful world has too little to offer us, when will we realise this truth and stop chasing the shadows of pleasures and desires which will only keep us behind the bars of birth and rebirth. 

![[Pasted image 20250424094441.jpg]]

---
We have to, for once listen to the almighty and believe in his reassurance for a better future and spend our time saturated in His consciousness. It is the ego of the man that keeps him separated from the divine blessings. Until man thinks himself as the doer and the subject of pleasure and pain he will never enter the land of immortality and divine omnipotence. 

That divine land of omni-all is forever open for those who truly want God in their lives. When the man stops entertaining himself with the toys and start crying for the lord, He cannot keep himself away for the devotee for too long, He has to come running and embrace the crying soul. The lost prodigal son just has to return home and all the blessings are just waiting for him in that place. 

No matter how bad the son is, how many crimes he has committed the [[Divine Mother]] is ever merciful and welcomes her long lost son with open arms like it was just a moment ago that he went astray. With that all the things of the world are added to such a devotee without even asking just like a son inherits everything without asking for anything.
