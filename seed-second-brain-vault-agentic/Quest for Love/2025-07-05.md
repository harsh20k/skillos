Love letters to my Divine Mother. 

A SRF nun shared this idea to write letters to the Divine Mother expressing our emotions and having that openness with her. A child is not learning to be frank with his mother, he is only frank and open with his mother.

Through writing to the divine mother, a devotee starts to feel that omnipresent blessing of his mother, expanding his heart and love for all. 

No I am not talking about just telling her what you did or did not accomplish, rather also sharing with her your inner most desires and emotions and feelings that will remain between you and her only. 

We already know, no amount of earthly love can quench the thirst of our heart for our heart is divine and it only wants the divine beloved who can do anything for him, who can move mountains to save her child. 

Oh Mother, I have this fear of defeat, fear of losing it to others, fear of the world's cruelty. I see people eating each other alive and I get scared mother.
