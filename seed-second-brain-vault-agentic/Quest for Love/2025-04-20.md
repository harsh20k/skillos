# The Power of Divine Importance: Shifting Consciousness Through Grace

There is a constant struggle of the devotee of bringing the consciousness back to the divine. The consciousness has been trained for many lifetimes to stay in the world. We have default mode of concentration on the world because we inherently know its importance. 

When this importance is instead given to the divine one then automatically concentration flows to God and [[Divine Mother]]. 

There is no barrier or any actual obstacle in having God consciousness. We have all the tools for that within ourselves. Its just that we automatically concentrate on the things we give importance to. When we listen to [[Satsanga]], that importance is shifted form the body and the world to God. 

![[Pasted image 20250420090729.jpg]]

---
Meditation is just concentration in God. When the devotee places the importance on God for just a minute, then his concentration automatically is pulled toward the real thing. It leads to meditation automatically. 

No matter what consciousness we have right now; it is our responsibility to instantly hold the importance of God and his qualities and suddenly we would have transformed our consciousness in an instant from worldly to Godly.

All this knowledge is slowly imparted to the devotee by the Guru and the will to hold this knowledge is also given by this Supreme one to ultimately free the devotee from the pangs of [[Maya]].
