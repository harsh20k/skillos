# At the Feet of Grace: Guru Devotion

On this beautiful day I feel grateful and blessed to have found [[Divine Mother]] through the mercy of my Gurudeva. I have found the purpose and goal of my life and also the way. All three are the one and the same I have realised and i.e. [[Gurudeva]]! His holy feet are the only objective reality in this universe, rest everything is just a facade of play of lights and shadows. 

If only we could feel the devotion for the Guru's feet we would realise the tremendous transformation we are capable of. His one look can change the heart from inside out, wipe out all the karma good or bad. His love is not of mere human nature but of divine descent and is expressed in the form of his teachings and lessons he gives us in different forms. 

![[Pasted image 20250508092045.jpg]]

Its crazy to think that anything else would every help us. If we take refuge in a person we will soon witness the corruption inside the human being that we might mistake as "good". If we take refuge in any dopamine inducing substances, we will soon witness their poisonous nature.

---
Let's all spend our lives in the devotion of our Guru and through his grace we will find the one and only beloved that everyone is thirsty for.
