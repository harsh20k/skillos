# [[Love]] her

The love of [[Guru]] and God will remove all ignorance. Not only the ignorance right now, but also the root cause of all ignorance so it can never sprout in the future. Feeling the [[kripa]] of [[Guru]] and God continuously, a devotee will soon realise that they have taken over his life and doing what’s necessary and living a life that is best for him/her. The devotee who is in constant remembrance is free from all delusion right now. He is [[jeevan-mukta]] at the exact moment he says lord name once or he remembers his [[Guru]]. 

![](https://i.imgur.com/PiF1xI9.png)

If there is one very difficult misconception that assails a devotee, it is that God will show himself after certain time or after he has achieved something or this many hours of meditation. [[Truth]] as said by realised souls is that God is , that’s it. Its simple [[God]] is right here right now at this very moment if we have him in our conscious thoughts God is right here close to us. If we are in the midst of our daily duties and we devote everything to God it is a powerful spiritual practice. Nobody can weight God in the scale of sadhana. God cannot be counted like in an excel sheet. He is the source of all numbers and sadhana. The very act of sadhana is because he allows us to do sadhana. 

Self respect in spiritual life should be avoided like poison. The God must be the only one, from whom we want love and respect.>)
