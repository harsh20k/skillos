# God’s words are never false. 

Similarly, [[Guru]] given sadhana is never fruitless. When we begin on the spiritual path by his grace, giving up of all the worries related to body and mind is a mandatory requirement. 

![](https://i.imgur.com/9H6meg6.png)


True devotion brings in itself true renunciation from every possible thing that can happen in the devotees life. So, one should never be worried about the spiritual progress being elusive in one’s experience. How can someone call himself a devotee if he worries about his own progress. His sole responsibility is to keep his consciousness filled with divine presence amidst every action true or untrue. To stay inside the circle of protection of [[Guru]] given techniques is the ultimate duty of a devotee and I think it is the most difficult thing in the entirety of the universe.
 
There is also one interesting point to note in regard to doubt that generally assails a new devotee who is not yet deeply rooted devotion in his heart. As Premanandji describes, doubting in one’s own upliftment is tantamount to question the effectiveness of [[Guru]] given sadhana. This problem is deeply rooted in one’s own ego as a mortal being and also the reluctance to view the [[Truth]] that [[Guru]] and God are one and the same. 
 
As the grace descends upon a devotee all his doubts are instantly replaced by gratitude of what he has found by one and only grace. Initiation is not a simple ritualistic tradition, it is a promise that the [[Guru]] makes to his devotee to protect him in all myriad faces of fear and temptation. It is a timeless promise that no matter where the devotee is, he can rest assured that his work is finally done and [[Guru]] will ultimately take him to divinity. As premanandji says this ritualistic initiation is not for devotee because like the forget being we are, there will come a time when we forget this promise and steep down in really unholy acts. It is however, for the [[Guru]] because we may be liars, but [[Guru]] and God once they give their word it will come to fruition. Stay assured our job is done here.
