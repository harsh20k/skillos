# The Graceful Lesson of Detachment

Yesterday I was being given a really important lesson on the **importance of detachment.** 

From early childhood I don't know how, but sleep became a very important part of my day and many articles I read completely solidified that belief. Now also I have extreme almost martinet strictness around sleep hygiene. 

![[Pasted image 20250319184039.jpg]]

My [[Divine Mother]] is trying to teach me a lesson of detachment to sleep too I feel. Since marriage there has been a complete destruction of my sleep schedule and overall sleep schedule. Through this, I think my beloved mother is trying to free me from this attachment to sleep, and trying to take this dependence of sleep away from me.

With time I feel that I will get completely non detached with how much sleep I get. From now on I will try to lie down and do naam jap or meditate while lying down if my sleep gets broken and wake up at the exactly same time with as much rest as I can get. 

Sometimes its very hard to let of something but my omnipotent and graceful mother makes these lesson very easy and so painless that its very hard to ignore them.

On the face value I might feel agitated or even angry to get my sleep disrupted but with non detachment to how much sleep I get in familial life I think this victory of sleep is being slowly being awarded to me by my mother which might come handy later for her work.
