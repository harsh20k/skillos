# The Eternal Question: Seeking Divinity in Silence

What is the meaning of life? What is the purpose of one's existence? What should one do or strive to do in one's life?

At some point in life one questions these questions. Some try to uncover the truths but some just forget about these and look at others and keep imitating what they can see and hear. 

---
![[Pasted image 20250115101636.jpg]]

The purpose of life is in accordance with the divine law and order. If we do not follow that order we suffer immensely and question [[God]], that why is he making them suffer. Isn't this a double standard? 

We are his children and we have to love him and all the riches and all the problems in the world will be solved by him. And if he keeps us in poor conditions we will still have that rock-solid love of the lord that keeps us going through life without a trace of suffering. This place is world and it is suffering. What we try to do is eliminate the suffering individually by providing with different objects and pleasures. 

---
But this does not solve the real problem of suffering embedded deep inside by human condition. If we even try to solve this riddle we will get the [[Grace]] of the divinity in the form of true [[Guru]] and through him we will find the real knowledge and tools to end all suffering from its root.

Most of the people end up going with the flow just hoping someday all their sufferings will end which end up their life's end alas. In the end they don't have the tools or anything of this world all the possession will become dust and all the attachments in relationships will be rotted with corruption of selfishness.

---
Our [[God]] almighty is very kind, literally one name of the lord is "Ocean of Kindness". If we just even try for one second stop looking the answers outside and start to explore the answers within we will find his guidance and we will find our way to him which will be end of all our problems. We do not wish to even explore the possibility that all the problems can come to an end. We are so tied up in the routines and different pleasures of the senses that we ignore the possibility of nirvana. 

Its just sad to see people dying inside when all they need to do is just sit in silence and ask [[God]] why ? Why am I here oh lord!
