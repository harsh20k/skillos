# A Glimpse of the Divine Mother’s Grace

Today, when I was coming back after dropping Suman at _la gare_ I automatically started singing "Sundarta ki seema" and saw the divine form of my mother in front of my eyes while driving. The divine grace is upon me for sure because it happened spontaneously and without my any thought. I was just calmly driving and suddenly it hit me like a gentle breeze and made my eyes teary with love and affection. 

---
When experiences like this happen, it is the divine mother who is missing us and thinking about us and we get that thought instantly that takes us in their presence. 

I cannot express my gratitude towards the line of my Guru's and param-gurus who are showering me with such blissful experiences because I know that in this life I have done nothing but bad actions that should lead me to hell. I am graced with the blessings of such great Gurus, and that's the reason that I am in the memory of my [[Divine Mother]]. For I have nothing and I merely being ignorant, have been performing actions to take me away from my mother.

My [[Mother]], however is very pitiful and kind and do not take my faults seriously and takes the little sadhana I have performed by the will of my Gurudeva, as very substantially. 

---
Her love and kindness for me, her child is just undescribable beyond any comparison. Her mercy and grace is infinite as described by the scriptures, but this is when I actually get to witness her true divine nature.

![[Pasted image 20250221100546.jpg]]

Her beauty is not anything from this world. It is causeless and cause for every beautiful thing in this world. No matter the situation, she peeks at her children and gently whispers in their ears and confesses the love she has for them, if only we would listen.

---
Guru kripa and divine mercy together are what when combines lead to the ultimate salvation of the devotee leading to the complete surrender of the devotee's heart at the feet of the divine mother. The worries of all the world are not even comparable to the amount of happiness and contentment that a devotee receives when he understands this true nature of the mercy of [[Divine Mother]] and no longer he craves for the things of this world, for he has received that is more valuable than any riches or any friendships or relationships. The promise of love made by divine mother to her child is enough for the devotee that he can spend his entire life in difficult or pleasant situations without caring for them even for an instant.
