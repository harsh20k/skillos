# Unconditional Love: A Devotee’s Longing for the Divine Mother

What is the meaning of love? Today I heard its definition by [[Premanandji]] in his questions and answer session. He described love as to be able to give up one's own life to make the beloved happy. To love someone is to make them happy without any condition and not even a single thought of one's own happiness. 

![[Pasted image 20250108093432.jpg]]

All the relationships are poisoned by attachment and with selfish desires. If we can love anyone it is only [[God]] our true mother. If anyone can love us it is [[Divine Mother]], none other than her can truly love us for us. Only [[God]] can be unselfish because she has everything, yet she wants to love us for our own good. That is why saints beg mother to give her divine love. God made us in his own image so we do have the ability to love her. We have what it takes to leave all the thoughts of our own desires and love her truly, yet we have chained ourselves to our body consciousness and hence we forget our ability to love and start entangling ourselves in the attachment due to this body and mind. 

[[Premanandji]] also said that true love appears only when God herself loves us even more than we are feeling right now. When we cry tears for God, our divine mother has shed tenfold tears before that, hence we are able to cry for her! Such a beautiful fact, to hear that divine mother that has infinite children of the universe cries for me?, a disgusting individual enslaved by the disgusting habits of the flesh and bones?

---
Then how to feel her true love? It is through her grace and the [[Guru]]'s grace that we can feel that divine love within us. With steadfast devotion at the feet of the Guru we will eventually love God just as she loves us. Mother and child finally reunited by the bond of love, Guru's work is complete and he reveals his true identity as the diviner mother herself.

Guru is the living embodiment of the divine mother's grace and love for us. When she just can't stay without us she goes to the devotee in the form of Guru and help the devotee to remember his divine right to love her!
