# Fearlessness in God Consciousness

---
Fear is a feeling that paralyses the body and mind. The most feared thing in the universe is [[Death]], the end of one's individuality. If we can uproot this fear we won't fear anything in the world. If we have victory over death, even if the world start crashing upon us we won't give in a single step. 

![[Pasted image 20241224083416.jpg]]

The great saints have given up their bodies in excruciating conditions, of which merely talking can make us squeamish. Once a devotee comes on the path of spirituality, a true devotee no matter how weak can face the entire world. He is not fearful of any challenge, because he has given up the fear of death for the pursuit of death. He is still not free from threefold-death, only taking the first step in this path can grant the devotee fearlessness from death and every misery life can throw at him. I have witnessed it first-hand, just a boy I wouldn't mind sleeping in an open field, the langur's surrounding the road didn't scare me, or when everyone gathered around me and held me as a thief I did not get a hair of fear. 

---
Imagine the path on which the first step can give you such superhuman powers! [[God]] himself takes care of his devotees whose hearts are aimed at [[God|him]]. No matter the situation the devotee doesn't mind giving up anything or standing against anyone because he knows that giving up life in this path would make him immortal.

[[Kriya]] yoga is the ancient science of yoga and meditation in which anyone can become fearless and attain the true goal of life achieving liberation from all suffering. The garb of maya doesn't cover the devotee who is resolved in finding the devotee and even helps the devotee to reach his divine beloved through [[Guru]]. I found my Gurudev after I gave up every desire in the name of God. However, a word of caution, this cannot be done with mere outward resolve it is a blessing that is received in the temple of silence within.

So if the first step can make the devotee fearless, then what might the journey entail for the devotee, one might question. When the grace of Gurudev descends on the devotee's soul he enjoys the bliss of God in his every waking moment. I also live in the hope to achieve it but for now I am content in the blessings of my Guru. A devotee has complete and unshakable faith in his [[Gurudeva]]. He is aware of the fact that nothing in this world can touch him when he is in guru consciousness or krishna consciousness. If God wishes he will die still he knows that such death won't kill him, but make him immortal and liberate him from the cycle of suffering, by the grace of the Guru and the great one before him. 

---
Let not fear rule our lives. Let's face the life with utmost courage and God consciousness for which we all have been given this human life. Fear is paralysis, fear is death. Why die before death of the body. Leave all the fears and worries at the feet of Guru, for he will take care of every detail of the devotee's life. If we can't face little fears of daily life then even God can't save us. God comes for us at the right moment in the right garb to help us, and when the devotee gives up every pursuit for God, then God lovingly walks side by side with him, not leaving his devotee for even a moment.
