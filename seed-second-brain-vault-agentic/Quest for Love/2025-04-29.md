# Come Home: A Cry from the Heart of Divine Love

Its true! Its true!! Its completely true!! Oh world believe me it is completely true that a devotee can leave his beloved but the beloved never leaves the devotee.

Our beloved God will never forsake us and stay with us even when we completely forget about Him. Even when we are unconscious we will have him with us. And that too not far or a little closer right where we are He is. Not even a micron of distance is there between the devotee and God.

![[Pasted image 20250429210947.jpg]]

Its all true! Oh world! Listen to this drunk man, drunk in the love given by Guru. Drunk in the love of mother divine. Do not question anything just come and start running!!!!

Its not too late, you will never be a sinner in their eyes, come and reclaim your throne on the lap of divine mother. She will never forsake me, she has told me with clarity. She is shouting within for me to come back to her no matter my sins and shame. 

Everything will get better. Nothing will ever be the same again! Your life will only be dictated by her only her and her grace will be felt on every step, every moment. Oh world! why don't you listen to me, do not look for anything out here, it will all vanish I cry to you. Come home! Divine mother is waiting for us all with so much love we can't even imagine.

Take the shelter of the Guru's feet and this ocean will be dried up! Come see for yourself all this love, all this joy is waiting for us all. It does not distinguish! Come home!!
