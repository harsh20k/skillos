# The Path to Divine Love: Satsanga, Dharma and Salvation

The basics of life and the answers to the most complicated conundrums in life can be easily found in the most simplest philosophies of the scriptures as described by God. 

No matter how “successful” we become in life outwardly we have nothing if we do not have [[Satsanga]] in our life.

![[Pasted image 20250322211740.jpg]]

---
Only God’s love and his guidance through the Guru’s way is the only salvation in our life. 

Through constant listening to Satsanga and following the right path combined with [[Dharma]] in our life we will reach heights only reserved for the Gods. People will be awestruck with the person who has complete dependance on the lord’s name. 

---
While God is elusive we all get the chance to follow his words that will finally lead to his true love. This love is the only medicine for this disease of suffering and constant fight with the senses.
