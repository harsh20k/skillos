The Sanskrit word [[Dharma]] (or _[[Dharma|Sanatana Dharma]]_, "Eternal Religion") signifies what religion or spirituality can be when freed from stagnation-inducing dogmatism, superstition, and the poisonous attitude that any one sect has an exclusive monopoly on the truth.

[[Dharma]], from the Sanskrit root *dhri*, "to uphold or support," is a comprehensive term fro the natural laws and eternal verities that uphold the divine order and harmony of the universe - and of man, a miniature universe. [[Dharma]] is defined as "those immutable principles which protect man permanently from the threefold suffering: physical disease, mental unhappiness, and spiritual ignorance."
