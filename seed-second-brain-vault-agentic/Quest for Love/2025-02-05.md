# The Path to Divine Love: Overcoming Maya and Finding the Guru

Right after we start on the path of spirituality, we start to experience roadblocks and challenges on this path. We start to face lucrative options and fears everywhere to try to throw us down from this path.

Since we are always attached to [[Maya]] we get sidetracked by its forces into the world of delusion. We often forget our true aim in favour of some small pleasures. The [[Divine Love]] in its various forms start to vanish from our structured plan of life. 

All of the human plans and structured goals revolve around getting happiness and that kind of happiness which does not reduce but keeps increase every moment and quenches the thirst of our souls. The kind of happiness that can only be found after leaving all the worldly passions and desires. 

---
If we persevere on this path the presence of God and [[Divine Mother]] is felt in the most mundane places and times. The organisational tendencies of the mind that categorises the world into mine and not mine starts to create a poison of i-ness in this body and through extension to various loved ones and things related to this body. 

![[Pasted image 20250205090722.jpg]]

---

The kind of pressures we face through this body should be dispassionately observed and be faced with poise and internal peace which can only be found by concentrating our efforts in our sadhana. 

---
Love of [[Divine Mother]] is the ultimate form of devotional practice we can perform in our lives. When we love someone we are not concerned about the outcome, we just love with no return or incentive in mind. Its this selfless love that will free us from this delusion and we start working through our karma and finally by following the word of a [[Guru]] we start enjoying the fruits of love and divine grace.

As described by [[Premanandji]] no one can find the ultimate without the [[Guru]]. The Guru should be the object of our devotion and meditations everyday and soon in no time we will find that the divine hand comes to us whenever we want and we will find that ultimate, pinnacle of love and acceptance by the divine mother. All our actions become devotion to the divine mother. 

---
The guru sent by God to us is the humanised bodily image of the divine mother herself where we find the true path to her lap. A true path to her love is enlightened by the divine mother is shown by the divine mother herself in form of the Guru and shastras. These illumine us in a way indescribable and take us directly to God and no matter our religion or house or purity we all will find this Guru who will take us to our true home in the divinity.
