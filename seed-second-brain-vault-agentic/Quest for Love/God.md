My God is my [[Mother]], my everything my everyone and whatever I have and will have is by her grace. 

---
The way to divine is the only way of life. Everyone from a cat dog to highest intellectual person is walking the same path to God. All we have to figure out is how to find this path by our [[Gurudeva]].

![[Pasted image 20250111111103.jpg]]

All is well when we are with God and nothing is right if we are not looking at him. 

No matter how hard we try to find happiness in this temporary prison of the world and the body, we will only find refuge in the arms of the [[Mother]] and only then we can find our true meaning of life i.e. find happiness. 

God is described as ever conscious, ever present and every increasing bliss. If we just try to imagine what it will be in God's consciousness, we will realise the importance of finding him. Doing our part is the key to unlock God's grace and if we find our true [[Guru]] with his grace then we are finally just cruising along this life awaiting to be united with our [[Mother]] waiting at the end of the road or if she desires even in the flesh; we could witness her!
