# The Path of True Surrender

True trust or surrender to [[Guru]]'s words and blessings is a very important milestone on this spiritual journey of the soul.

Many kinds of object identifications and pleasure seeking has left us with a very dirty mind and body and we cannot trust anyone other than ourselves. 

---
No amount of [[kripa]] can stop this cycle of ignorance and again and again we get back to where we started identified with the body complex filled with the fear of death. 

When we get true understanding and trust of the words of shastras and Gurudeva, then we realise the true spirituality. True love start to become clearer in the mind of such a devotee who has given up all the bodily pursuits in the Guru's feet. His every action becomes like puja and he is free from bondage once and for all. 

![[Pasted image 20250206102617.jpg]]

---
The various situations that come in the life of such a devotee are faced with utmost dispassion and cunning because he does not let that entangle him in maya again. 

Organising his actions to align with the divine will his mere presence becomes a blessing in any place. His words become like nectar for the poor souls who are assailed with ignorance and unfamiliar with humility. 

With all the kripa, yet the devotee realises what his condition is truly without all the kripa and that brings about the birth of utmost humility and compassion. 

---
No matter our state we can give complete surrender at the divine feet. Our complete surrender will be awarded with total independence and our love for divine mother will become permanent. We must do our part in the sadhana and then when kripa follows we will find true happiness and fearlessness from death and disease. The end becomes like a celebration, unlike the death's of worldly men who are lamented over and cried upon by lesser men and ignorant crowds.

---
Divine love envelops this devotee and he feels it true to his bones that how much God and divine mother loves him in spite of his shortcomings and faults. All is forgiven by God, and that realisation removes all the karma bondages in his life gathered across lifetimes.

The true relationship with [[Guru]] will be enough for a devotee to win over death and cycle of maya. No amount of love and sacrifice for the world can light up this path to the ultimate, only by walking the small path lightened by the [[Gurudeva]], the devotee reaches his ultimate goal of finding true love in this life and post the final punctuation of death.
