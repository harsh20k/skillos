# The Gift of Suffering: Awakening the Soul to True Freedom

What constitutes a good life? Is it just having a lot of money or having a good relationship with your loved ones(family and friends)? Isn't that what everyone is after? Everyone is thinking the same thing that getting a lot of money and spending that money peacefully with people closest to you is the most that the life can offer. 

But what happens when suddenly there are financial losses out of our control? What happens when the loved ones are removed from our life without any intimation either by death or betrayal? Then the whole foundation of a person's psyche get's out of control because that was the whole meaning of life for them until it was not. Suddenly man turns to God, why? Because he is hoping can fix these problems so he can go about his life as he was going. 

![[Pasted image 20250422085635.jpg]]

When God takes something it is wake up the soul of the person that has lost its meaning, the true purpose for incarnating in this life. If only a person would know that he would have a sense of what is life all about. Life is about attaining unimaginable bliss and happiness. Yet the door of this place called life is marked with "Suffering House/Death House". When it is already marked as temporary and transient then why does everybody keep running after these transient things that give a momentary pleasure to the five senses? Why does man keep his eyes closed to the ultimate truth and purpose? 

Through God's grace man gets beaten by life outwardly or inwardly and suddenly only place he can find as a refuge is the God almighty. Then he realises his true purpose and who is his closes friend and beloved. Guru then appears to teach the soul how to get back to the lost kingdom within this life and how to balance the scales of bodily duties and soul duties. Now the individual is set on a quest that will last his lifetime and beyond. Now suddenly he has a purpose that is beyond this world of duality. Now he is free or on the way to freedom. But isn't walking on the path to freedom, real freedom?

All the soul feels is an immense gratitude towards the whole universe for the gift of life, the gift of suffering, the gift of guru/teacher, and the opportunity to walk on this quest for REAL meaning which he was completely unaware of.
