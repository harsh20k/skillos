#

The universe is calling all of us to find its secrets. Through thousands of lifetimes of sadhana, as described by Adi Shankaracharya, one gets a strong desire to get free from this maya.

Love is only received when we are ready for it to receive it from the universe. It is already showering everywhere with the same intensity, but we only can perceive it when we tune into it. 

Only God will quench our incessant thirst of this world. Then why do we keep running after the world. Because that is where we think contains lot of happiness. 

Oh God! Save me from this difficult maya of yours, send a Guru and rescue me! 

This is the condition of the devotee who wants to find Him and finally set ablaze all his karma and desires of the body.
