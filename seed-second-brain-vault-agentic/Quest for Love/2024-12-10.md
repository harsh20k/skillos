# The [[Guru]]’s Light: Rising Above Challenges

[[God]] is accessible to everyone. No matter their background or race or religion. The goal of every individual is to be one with him.

![](https://i.imgur.com/TlsMDs1.png|200x200)

When God sees it as the right time to reveal himself to a person or anyone he sends [[Guru]] to him. [[Guru]] is none other than god himself incarnate in his human form to take care of the devotee personally. The only goal of the devotee is to follow [[Guru]] given [[sadhana]] with devotion and love. 

The problems in one's spiritual path is not encountered unless it is prescribed by God himself. If an obstacle arises in front of the devotee it is for their own betterment. To rise above [[Maya]] God helps us by improving us bit by bit and that he does through presenting novel obstacles in from of us. God does this is in a way that it is also linked to our past [[karma]]. This is done so creatively that even if we keep doing bad karma the resulting situations will be designed by God in such magical way that they become the reason for our salvation.

A non spiritual man gets frustrated easily by these challenges arisen from God. He might even become even more atheistical and deny the existence of God altogether at his own peril, of course. A spiritual man, someone who in love with God or someone who has given the weight of his life on the shoulders of [[Guru]] does not even wink when such problems arrive at his doorstep. The love and devotion to [[Guru]] and God brings forth qualities like [[forbearance]] and disregard for the whims of body and mind.

[[Ignorance]], often uses the tool of shame or guilt, to break that strong bond between the [[Guru]] and his disciple. This leads to feeling of shame and guilt so strong that the devotee feeling unable to seek [[Guru]] and jumps into the fire of worldliness willingly.
