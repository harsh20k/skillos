# Divine Longing: A New Year’s Devotion

---
Its the first day of this year 2025. Maybe this is the year when I will receive what my soul have been waiting for so long. Or maybe yet another year will pass in this divine longing, shedding tears for my mother.

![[Pasted image 20250101085856.jpg]]

Happy new year Divine mother. Let me be close to you this year. Bless me with [[nirantar bhajan]] and akhand prem, your prem, undiluted by any worldliness. Bless your child to love you immensely, to be your good child, a good son. 

This year is very special of course because it started with your name oh lord. The name that my mother loves and repeats each moment. May this year bring joy and love to my heart, bless me oh lord, oh merciful. 

Right and wrong, truth and false, give me the knowledge to separate these and give my selfless service to the world while doing your [[Sadhana]]. 

![[Pasted image 20250101090621.jpg]]
---
My [[Gurudeva]], I plead you to come to me and shower your presence in each of my action and each of my thought. Give me strength to follow you to the end of the world and time. Bless me with the divine love which imbibe every atom of yours. Bless me with divine consciousness so I can witness the reality that you describe and see each moment. Oh divine one! last but not the least shower me with your [[kripa]] so I can move forward in this [[Bhakti]] marg. 

![[Pasted image 20250101090047.jpg]]

Give me the strength to perform my worldly duties and responsibilities with dispassion and love and kindness. Be, by my side each moment so I can stand fearless in every situation and face any challenge with a smile inspired by your divinity.

I thank you tremendously for the guidance that you have given me. I was truly lost and suffering in this world. You found me and gave me love, you called me your own and embraced me with divine love indescribable beyond words. Whatever I have you have given it to me. I might fall off from this path but I feel assured now that you will pick me up wherever I have fallen and stand by me and lift me up and carry me to the divine abode. I cannot repay even the thousandth part of this infinite mercy and love you have showered me with. You have given me the true wealth of divine longing and the name of lord. I thank you again and again for this divine gift of [[Kriya]] yoga path that you gave me not because I was qualified but because you are merciful beyond anyone. You overlooked my flaws and gaping holes in my character and brought me up close to your heart. 

![[Pasted image 20250101090218.jpg]]

Oh my [[Gurudeva]], how can I thank you I don't know. I could not even worship you or lord, you taught me even that. Make me your instrument, make this body your home, your tool and do as you please. I will just stand aside and enjoy the ride to the divine mother waiting for us on the other side of this journey. 

---
## Happy new year, oh timeless one!
