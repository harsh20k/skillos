# Divine Guidance in Chaos.

There are many things I want to think about in detail. First let's get this marriage thing out of the way. Yesterday 2025-01-10 [[Inbox/QuickNotes/Suman]] and me asked our parents that we wanted to marry each other this month ie January 2025. 

On this morning at around 6AM I woke up by a beautiful dream about her. [[Inbox/QuickNotes/Suman]] was ready to sleep with a well built man in front of me. It made me angry in the dream, but when I woke up I had this feeling that it was [[Divine Mother]]'s grace. 

![[Pasted image 20250111104621.jpg]]

---
She is letting me know that everything in this world, every relationship is temporary and not get attached to these ephemeral things. It is really wonderful how much [[Divine Mother]] loves me and don't want me to get hurt by anyone or anything. Just as the wind of [[Marriage]] started to blow she came to protect me with her guidance and wisdom. 

The fear and anger I felt in the dream felt really troubling, but it was a dream and as I witnessed my mother's words of wisdom I actually woke up from this attachment of marriage. I will do all my duties but my foremost duty will always be towards my lovely and one and only [[Divine Mother]]. She just can't let her child foolishly play in the mud while she is away. She can't resist the urge to protect her child that's how much she love me.

---
After that I was still feeling restless and disarrayed, so I went to take refuge in my daily meditation. During that I could not concentrate on her and my mind was just like a flock of birds flying away at the sound of thunder, total chaos! 

Then a few words of [[Premanandji]] came to me and I felt centred again in the divine and instantly felt her message [["Bakto ka Sang hi Mera Sang hai"]]. It was just undeniable to think of it as my imagination or hallucination. Through this I think my mother wants to explain to me what is really important. To be with [[Satsanga]] is same as sitting with divine mother herself.

---
So here are a few things that I have to continually keep doing in order to sit with my divine mother. Spending time, listening to [[Satsanga]], doing research and making notes on the [[Gurudeva]]'s words and satsanga, all of this will constitute being in the company of my beloved [[Mother]].
