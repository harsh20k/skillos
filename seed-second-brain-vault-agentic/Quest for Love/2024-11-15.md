# The Path of Surrender: Embracing God’s Name with [[Guru]]’s Guidance
 
I’ve always felt a sense of comfort and an indescribable feeling of begin at home while listening to God’s name. However, it’s only now that through Premanandji’s Guruvani I’ve begun to truly understand the significance of Name. 

![](https://i.imgur.com/i2g1Q9b.png)


He says after following a variety of spiritual paths throughout his life, he has finally come to conclusion that the only way an individual can be free is through the Name of the Lord. The reluctance of saying God’s name has always been inside me because of a filthy heart, i’m sure of that. Yet there is hope for me because he also adds that name is one of the limb of wider more profound Bhajan. Bhajan is much more deeper than saying His Name, it is that inner continuous and complete surrender to God, Premanandji describes. 
 
That is a good sign for me because this tool has been my constant companion throughout my life because of my Mother’s grace. Although it’s a long way to go, because it’s not easy to achieve continuous and utmost surrender. This can only be achieved through giving up every desire, when the whole consciousness removed from all the objects of desire is finally given to God, and that too without skipping a beat not even a millisecond. 
 
There are two important obstacles that one has to overcome in order to do so, Premanandji says. One begin fear and the other being temptation. These two can only be destroyed completely by the [[Guru]]’s given sadhana. Once the devotee becomes determined about the kind of Grace which God has spared him, through [[Guru]]’s teachings, he becomes free of any temptation and any fear that he might ever face in life. 
 
All this seems very easy to practice but in practice it is the most difficult thing for any person. The reason not being that it is difficult to say Name or practice kriya-yoga, but because of the amount of sins an individual carries with him actually prevent him to do so. Ego is the most powerful sinful attribute in a devotee. And this also can be easily given up by always taking comfort in [[Guru]]’s holy feet. 
 
One keen point to mention is that, while practicing all these steps, one is not alone, although it might seem like it. God always protects his devotees himself. It is mentioned in various scriptures. In essence the sinful nature of man has to contend with the Almighty himself. There shouldn’t be any doubt in who will win this battle and the ultimate war.
