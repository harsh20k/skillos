# Every Act, an Offering to the Divine Mother

The beauty of spiritual path that I feel is that every action throughout the day becomes an offering to the [[Divine Mother]]. From the very simple act of bathing or cleaning to the most demanding task of working on a project becomes a spiritual offering to the mother and the devotee start to think with which act the mother will be pleased and show herself to him. 

![[Pasted image 20250228105003.jpg]]

[[Premanandji]] beautifully describes that through giving up personal desires and goals all the duties does not cease to exist but they take the form of devotion and becomes an act of bhakti. This cleanses the heart of the devotee and takes him closer to divinity within. This also accelerates the hunger in the devotee to do bhajan continuously without break. 

No matter what the devotee keeps thinking that he cannot do bhajan as he was supposed to be do; he always feels that incessant hunger and thirst for bhajan. 

---
This is the condition of the devotee who through Guru has received actual love for God and divinity itself. This love is without any conditions or based on outer circumstances. 

---
No matter our situation our beloved [[Mother]] is watching over us and all we have to do is remember her true nature and keep her in our consciousness and rest everything will be done by her.
