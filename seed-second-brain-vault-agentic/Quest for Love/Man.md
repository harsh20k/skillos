Man is an instrument of the soul that does the thinking and loving. All the attachments, jealousy, [[raag]] and [[dwesha]] are the function of the man itself. 

God realisation happens because of man and the attachment to the world and ignorance happens in the man. This man stays in the soul when it leaves the body and mind and the affect of all our karma is acted upon it. 

Through it we feel happy and sad. Through it we do bhajan and through it we receive divine initiation.
