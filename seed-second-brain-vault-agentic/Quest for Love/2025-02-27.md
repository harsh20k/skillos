# Navigating Chaos with Grace: Finding Balance in a Shifting Routine

This co-living thing is very new to me and I have been struggling to keep my mind on track with all the changes going around here. With all this confusion and ongoing adjustments with each other, I have witnessed the immense love of my mother shining on me like a bright and warm sun on a cold freezing day.

---
With all that has been happening the most struggling factor is the adjustments of my routine which is seeming like an endless effort and doesn't seem to end at any point no matter how many changes I make to it. 

![[Pasted image 20250228095935.jpg]]

I combined the morning routine into one big chunk followed by meditation. Then a additional task of dropping Suman off to metro station. Creative writing task is also taking a hit whenever there is any change in the routine or any laziness I have been experiencing. I have now added a task of identification of single objective that I have to achieve or a task that I have to do today after writing, which is followed by cleaning and lunch. After that I have merged the meditation and duolingo practice as one task which I have been unable to complete daily no matter how much I try because of my stints off to the roof to meet the cat and trim tulsi. Following that I have created tasks of art and coding which are very hard cognitively and I have not been able to do them for not even more than a day. After that the day seem to end into dinner and picking up Suman and accompanying her at dinner. The night meditation session is also taking a hit because of delays and laziness because of fooling around with each other. 

---
All of this is taking a lot of energy from my side and I know that I cannot keep up with this. On top of this I started doing this tuition thing which comes up at very odd times and very sporadic in nature and I wish to end it as soon as possible. I know that without putting God in the front seat I cannot work like this and I have to find a perfect balance with all this drama going on in my life. Repeating God's name seems like the only solution because that will keep me centred on the divine presence and my true objective while doing the duties of the body and mind. 

I also wished to write a weekly newsletter but that didn't took place after just one instalment. I am also lagging behind in the financial tracking of all the expenses.
