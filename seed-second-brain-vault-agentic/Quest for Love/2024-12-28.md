# Dancing with Joy in the Lap of the Divine

If you give me the world in place of [[Bhakti]], I wouldn't even think of saying yes, because through the infinite grace of my [[Gurudeva]], I have found the way to source of all wealth, all happiness and end of all misery and suffering. 

Do you think the way to divine is without any pleasures? We are fools to compare the worldly path to the divine spiritual paths as described by the sages and rishis of India. Even a beginner in this path like me, can ridicule even the loftiest of attractions, then imagine the condition of a realised being. 

---
These beings come on this plane to coexist with us only to awaken our true love for [[God]]. Their sole purpose is to jog our memories of our innate divinity. 

![[Pasted image 20241228085003.jpg]]

If God is real, then why has he created all these paths that lead not to him, but to his [[Maya]], one might ask. The simple answer is that there are million shops in the shopping mall, do you enter every shop and browser or purchase everything. This [[Maya]] is many-fold but we have to chose which of the things we desire and what aims we want to set for ourselves. 

When this manyfold maya is visible then its impossible to see true divinity behind all the motion pictures in front of us. When a true devotee start following a true preceptor the [[Guru]] sent by God to him as his saviour, then only this manyfold maya start to unravel its true secret for the devotee and he witnesses the real [[Truth]] masquerading as innumerable false forms. False, because it is temporary and not free from death or decay.

---
[[God]] has created this world not because of any desire. But this world is his self-expression plaything. The formless spontaneously broke itself into many forms, and the game of suffering began.

Actually there is no beginning of this [[Maya]], as there is no beginning of [[God]], because as the infinity is infinite, so all the powers or tools of the infinite are also infinite. We cannot find an end of this [[Maya]] no matter how fast we travel, no matter how far we go, even at the edge of the observable universe, we might find the bubble of another universe. 

We have to go within, prostate in front of our Guru and then we see the whole universe is just a mere dream bubbling in the infinity, dancing with the joy of the divine.

---
My [[Divine Mother]] will slowly reveal her secrets to me and I will soon find myself in her lap dancing and playing with joy. Singing the name of [[God]], I will find the true happiness, the one that we look for everyday behind everything we crave to touch, eat, smell or hear.

My Mother doesn't care about how bad we have been, she is not unknown to the power of maya because she is the cause if herself. Yet, she loves us and impart true knowledge to those who love her for herself.
