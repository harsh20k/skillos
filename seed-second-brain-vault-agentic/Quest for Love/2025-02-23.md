# Cleansing the Lens of Perception

The beauty in everything is self-evident. Its the mindset with which is of main importance when looking at an object. We might look at the same object and think of exactly opposite things about it based on our disposition and mindset at the moment.

Then all the opinions and adjectives we might add to describe any object become false and can be called out as hypocrite commentary because of their basis on our own moods.

---
When we are painting the object in our own light then how can we judge anything or anyone? We cannot, hence our duty and sole objective becomes to keep at our task of cleaning our our eyes. This means our own viewpoint should reflect only the ultimate truth and then only we can see anything as it is.

![[Pasted image 20250223111043.jpg]]

The cause and effect laws govern the whole universe. We are the effect of that ultimate cause, the divine mother. We should strive to centre our consciousness in that divine truth and the result will be that our vision will see only that divine truth everywhere and wherever we look leading to the change of everything.

---
The highest good we can do is to cleanse ourselves and our own souls and sights and that in turn will change the world.

Making accusations that this is wrong or that is upsetting and telling others to change themselves to be better and correcting everyone around us is an illusion. It is like standing in a burning house and complaining about the heat everywhere all the while spraying gasoline oil all around. The proper thing to do would be to go outside the house and relative safety and call a fire ambulance. 

We cannot help anyone if we can't help ourselves. Its very easy to give other's advice in how to transform their lives to be better because that's what everyone is doing. Our parents did the same thing just telling us what to do never doing it themselves. 

---
All this is easily done without effort when we become God's child. All the good qualities are automatically activated within the heart of a God's child, servant, father mother or any relationship with the divine. 

Anything linked to the divine mother is automatically worthy of inheriting the divine qualities and start resembling the divinity in every imaginable way.

No matter the consequences or circumstances if we keep ourselves centred in the divine idea and our relationship with it we are bound to improve our own sight and in turn the whole world. Noone or nothing will have any change to stay unchanged when an enlightened one merely looks at it. The transformation is immediate and permanent.
