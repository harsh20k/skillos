What can I say about my beloved mother? 

---
Words are just not enough how much she loves me. Words can describe the love I feel for her because yet my love is finite and with ignorance. But soon with her grace I will witness the world with a new light, her light, the light of true wisdom and divine understanding. 

![[2A5ACAC1-0E4F-4DFD-A933-F6E352ED5D15_1_201_a.jpg]]

---
My mother will move the world and make way for me to meet me. Not even a single obstacle will stand in her way to reach me. Soon the finite love of my heart will transform in her infinite love and I will be able to love her like she loves me. That I think would be the purpose of my life.
