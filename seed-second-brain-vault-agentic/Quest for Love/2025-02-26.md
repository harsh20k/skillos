# The Path Beyond Suffering: A Mother's Love as the Key to Liberation

The noble path of Buddhism describes the path to enlightenment that is accessible for all of us. Without suffering this universe cannot work. The distractions of the world are continuously fighting for our attention all day. No matter our stage in life we are forever stuck in this cycle of suffering unless we end this cycle of birth death and rebirth.

---
My mother’s love is the only anti dote to this poison of suffering. Only she has the key to our heart and when we accept that process as inevitable the only way for our salvation then we can set ourselves free. All the pain and suffering cannot hide this hope inside all of us that we can actually in this lifetime achieve that indescribable happiness and bliss.
