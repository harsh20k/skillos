The love for God, is not exclusive. It should and will include everyone in this observable universe and beings beyond this too. The love of God, does not discriminate between anyone and is showered upon disregard of their status, home, or caste of belief system.

The people who are violent are also loved by God. They are like the children who have lost their way but God will never forsake them. They might be punished to nudge them towards God and that is also a part of God's love and mercy. Through punishment God intends to eradicate the filth of the heart. Through suffering God intends to deepen one's devotional attitude. 

The mother inspite of child's remonstrations bathes the child out of love. The child might cry out and think that his mother doesn't love me and doesn't do what I like. But every action of the mother comes from a place of love for the little one. Just like that a rapist might be hanged to die, but in that punishment too God is with him all along the way and is helping him to be a better soul. Each life after life slowly man realises this love and when he understands its meaning nothing will every be able to deter him from this path of devotion towards God and almighty Mother divine.
