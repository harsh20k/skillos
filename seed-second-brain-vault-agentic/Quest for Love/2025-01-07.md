# The Divine Struggle: Between Maya and Surrender

What a wonderful world it is? And such disgusting [[Maya]]. This place is full of so many traps and pitfalls in which even a sincere devotee can find himself trapped from time to time.

[[God]] is continuously trying to pull us towards him upwards, and we are constantly trying to fall down back to base pleasures attracted by maya and its various objects. These objects attract us to help us realise that they are the not ultimate source of reality and we might someday become dissatisfied with them and try to look for happiness elsewhere. 

This war is very hard and we have to think about it in terms of small battles and with the help of our Guru and divine mother, we will win this war too. With this mindset I think we can achieve so much and rule the world. But what instead we do is get entangled in Maya and start to feel attached to it and love which was to be directed to the divine mother we spend it on this worldly attractions. 

![[Pasted image 20250107095219.jpg]]

I sometimes also feel that relaxed state, that carefreeness that my divine mother and Guru will do what's needed to me, and they have never failed me they have been the directors of my life even before I took my first step towards them. Its a feeling out of this world and comes only after accepting their complete dominion over myself. That [[devotion and surrender]] is the most beautiful feeling in this spiritual path think. 
 
---
When you feel the hand of God by your side then how can you feel defeated by any circumstance? When you feel the warmth of the mother's hand on your head then how can any failure shake your peace and beatitude? This epic love story does not end at any time or at any point of space at even the edge of the universe. Her love, my [[Guru]]'s love is everywhere if only they let us feel it, if we just [[Surrender]] to them completely. 

That's the feeling that first attracted me to spirituality, to give up all this weight of my shoulders. To give up all the running around for pleasure and fear from suffering. The promise of divine peace was too alluring and I did nothing but accepted the fact that their love accepts all no matter how bad they are or how bad actions one has done in their life.
