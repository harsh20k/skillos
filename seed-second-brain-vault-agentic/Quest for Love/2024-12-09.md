# Divine Love and Spiritual Awakening

The kind of love we are searching in this world throughout our lives cannot be found because the everyone and everything is searching for the same thing. The only one that can fulfil that promise is [[God]] alone because he is complete in himself and no selfishness tarnishes his grace.

![](https://i.imgur.com/6ZXtDoS.png)

If only we could realise how much God loves us, we would be free only to become like him and spread that same unselfish love to others. The [[Guru]] is our personal God sent as our saviour and protector. 

Then the question arises, how one can realise the true love of [[Guru]] because since there is no difference in God and [[Guru]] the love of [[Guru]] is complete and unwavering. Following the prescription of the doctor cures the disease, unconditional to our believes towards the doctor. Just like medicine does its job the prescription prescribed by a true Godman will do its job of removing ignorance from our frail minds and hearts.

We can if we like do an experiment like a scientist, trying out the medicine of **[[Kriya]]** or any other **[[Sadhana]]**. The world will change only if we will, the glasses of ignorance tint our very view of the world and what we see is the delusion born from our own ignorance. 

After following a True master’s words and teachings the true nature of the being starts to shine forth. It has no other option, the long stagnant ignorance has to make way for the ocean of knowledge and [[Truth]]. The abundance is already there, yet we see ourselves as the little bodies and see others as the objects through which we can fulfil our own desires and we think that it might give us happiness. The hollow feeling after getting something is the proof that there is no true meaning in this world except God. Kriya is like an invisible whip that tames the wild animals of [[Maya]] and put them into their proper cages. The realised being doesn’t give up the bodily duties, rather does them perfectly all the while drunk in the divine love of the infinite.
