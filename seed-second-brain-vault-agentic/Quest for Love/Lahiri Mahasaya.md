Lahiri Mahasaya was the one to whom [[Mahavatar Babaji]] revealed the sacred knowledge of _Kriya Yoga_, ordaining him to teach this divine science. When [[Paramhansa Yogananda]]ji was but an infant, [[Lahiri Mahasaya]] took him on his lap and spiritually baptised him, saying to his mother, "Little mother, thy son will be a yogi. As a spiritual engine, he will carry many souls to God's kingdom." This was later confirmed when [[Mahavatar Babaji]] chose Paramhansa Yogananda to spread _Kriya Yoga_ in the West and throughout the world, instructing [[Swami Sri Yukteswarji]], a God-realised disciple of Lahiri Mahasaya, to spiritually train Yoganandaji for this mission. These four great _avatars_ constitute the line of Gurus of [[Yogoda Satsanga Society of India]]/[[Self-realization Fellowship]].
