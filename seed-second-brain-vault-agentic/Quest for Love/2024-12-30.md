# The Cries of a Seeker: A Longing for the Divine Mother

Oh mother! where are you? Can't you hear my cries? Can't you feel these tears that are coming from my eyes and wasted on this hard earth? Oh [[Divine Mother]], I have heard from your favourite children that you are very kind, that you are easily pleased by the children, that you give anything to your loved ones. Should I assume then that you do not love me? Should I accept that I will never see you? Should I accept the fact that no matter what I do, you will never look in my direction?

![[Pasted image 20241230084539.jpg]]

Oh mother! I know I am not a good person, not a good son, not a good boy. I know I will always have shortcomings and flaws so large that make me unfit for anything spiritual. **Oh mother! but what about your infinite mercy and grace?** 

---
What about your promise of love, to your devotees. 

Should I then accept that you don't even count me in your devotees let alone a son?

Should I abandon the hope that one day I will see you by the grace of my [[Gurudeva]]?

Oh mother! with infinite love for your children do not forsake me? Do not leave me all alone here. I am scared and suffering, oh mother!! 

Why don't you answer to my cries? I know you have ears everywhere, I know that you have eyes everywhere, but where are those beautiful eyes oh mother? Where is your gaze for which I have been looking everywhere for in everyone's eyes?

![[Pasted image 20241230084808.jpg]]

---
Let me sit on your lap and sleep under your [[Aanchal]]. Let me hold your face, your beautiful hair. Let me hug you and climb on you, and forget all this. 

All this seems like wishful thinking, oh mother! Is this all there is? Is this how I am going to live for the rest of life, full of misery and unfortunateness? 

Why are you hiding from your own child, please explain this to me! What kind of love is this? Are you that hard-hearted? This is not love?

Where are you mother? Come see you child playing with cow-dung and mud..
