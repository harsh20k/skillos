# The Awakening: Yearning for the Divine Mother

The reason for lack of focus and nonchalance is the lack of thought we have given to the idea of life goals. When we do not even know what is our goal how can we strive for anything? 

We all have been fooled by everyone around us that money is the ultimate goal and happiness = accumulation of large amounts of money and spending it on various bodily pleasures and experiences. 

Through Satsanga we get to know of our real objective. If we listen to our gurus and spiritual teachers carefully then we will give similar importance to the attainment of [[Divine Mother]]. 

![[Pasted image 20250316090137.jpg]]

---
Through this we get even more hungry for the divine words and scriptures and the words of God. Worldly passions and pursuits start to feel lacklustre and we start to develop our [[Vivek]] to separate truth from falsehood.

My [[Divine Mother]]'s love is greater than all the knoledge of the universe. If one can feel only a fraction of this love for even a moment all their life's purpose has been attained in that single moment. [[Divine Mother]] and her grace are without any conditions or needs any qualifications. It is for everyone and for all.
