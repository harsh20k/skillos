# I Am Her Son

Wow! These couple of days have been weirdly interesting. I haven't been able to concentrate or meditate properly. I believe this is definitely my [[Mother]]'s way of reminding me that I can't do anything, which I agree with but haven't been able to inculcate in my being. 

Otherwise there won't be any pride in me regarding these spiritual actions. All my strength is being dependent on my mother, nothing else.

I am not a meditator, not a pro concentrator, or have even a milli horsepower to ignore my senses. All I have is the power of being the son of my [[Mother]]. That is the only power I have, that is the only grace I have. 

And all this should be a part of the core my being, then only there will be progress of the highest order. Right now, this knowledge is not a part of me, but with constant remembrance and love I will definitely make this a part of my innermost being, my heart and soul. 

I am her son! How wonderful! How amazing and beautiful! What is left in the world? Nothing, I am her son, that's it.
