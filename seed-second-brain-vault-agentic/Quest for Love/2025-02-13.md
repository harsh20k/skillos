# The Graceful Unfolding: A Journey of Surrender

Earlier it was very easy to forget the cause behind all the forms. But as the devotee progresses the spirituality envelops his whole life slowly and he doesn't even recognise the changes taking place in his life.

When I started on this path it was very sweet and fruitful but then it became dry and like a routine and for a while I completely stopped and now its getting sweeter everyday. The grace of the [[Gurudeva]] is such that we have to progress on this path without our knowing there is no option of moving forward or backward, the Guru takes all of our life's responsibilities and we progress towards her with steadfast focus whether we know it or not. 

---
Its through the words of [[Guru]] [[Premanandji]] that I assimilated this simple truth of giving up everything to [[God]]. Giving up all our worries and attachment at the feet of the divine mother doesn't save us from facing difficulties, throughout our lives we will face difficulties but these difficulties doesn't bother us or hamper our progress but rather take us to whole new spiritual level closer to our goal.

![[Pasted image 20250213103855.jpg]]

When we take the charge of our lives the life doesn't become better but gets more and polluted with selfish desires and motives until we are completely corrupted with the only possibility of improvement upon the final blow of death. 

On the other hand when we accept the fact that [[Guru]] and [[Divine Mother]] are our shepherds and we are merely lamb then we become privy to the inside workings of our own minds and find the cause of every thing being the Mother. This realisation frees us from ego that separates us from the divine presence. 

---
Through constantly listening to the words of [[Gurudeva]] and following the prescribed sadhana we become carefree and witness all our flaws just melt away into nothingness like they were never present.
