# The Hand of Grace- A Devotee's Cry for the Divine Mother

The [[Divine Mother]] mercy is infinite. Her grace is boundless like milky way's all encompassing nature. We forget this and in delusion start to experience mine-ness in material things and ungodly people related to our bodies. 

This leads to our suffering for the time indefinite and leads to forgetfulness of our true nature and our relationship with the [[Divine Mother]]. The great saints lead the way to the infinite and show it to us with utmost patience like a mother. 

---
My heart is full of impurities collected from lifetimes of wrong-doing. I have not experienced brhamacharya since I was little more than 15-16 years old. I haven't experienced anything substantial in my 32 years of life. But I found the [[Guru]]'s guidance and grace and through him I was able to know my aim in life at least. 

Speaking of celibacy, the importance of which has been lost and not shared with anyone by anyone.

![[Pasted image 20250130084207.jpg]]

---
### A simple trick [[Divine Mother]] told me to control animal impulses is the simple pranayama technique just like kriya yoga taught by my [[Gurudeva]]. When assailed with this is impulse I can observe the channel of the spine as the highway through which prana is being concentrated near the sex-organs. By continually upward and downward channeling of prana with devotion to the [[Mother]] and concentration on her divine form I can completely win this impulse. 

---
This divine technique can be used any time whether I'm lying down or sitting on a chair or travelling and standing in metro where many forms start to attract the mind and body. 

Constant [[naam-jap]] and devotion to my [[Mother]] is definitely the best remedy to all our problems. But when we forget that when being forced by our own animal instincts some simple techniques can be employed with cunning and devotion to win them over.

---
I have seen myself growing impatient and finding faults in others. This is complete disregard and disrespect of the gifts [[Divine Mother]] bestow on me with grace and without judgement. I cannot describe how much love is present in her every situation that she sends to her devotee. She even tells these simple techniques to her devotee in need and who with great devotion wants to be free of this suffering and witness her in true form. 

The devotee no longer wants to be part of this drama and cosmic delusion and cries for divine mother. Oh Mother! Where are you I am lost. I cannot find any peace with my strength I have tried everything and anything that I could think of but everything ended in disaster, pain and suffering taking me deeper and deeper in this illusion. 

The divine mercy having no bounds reaches out to the devotee and pulls him with the hand of grace, ultimately providing him/her with her [[Guru]] form. By following [[Guru]]'s words devotee receives assurance that his work is done already and this lifetime will be his last if he keeps the devotion in his heart at all times.
