# The Hidden Gem Within: A Reminder for the Seeker

The ultimate reason for our life is very clear and it has been clearly outlined by all the saints before and present. In the daily grind we often forget that true objective. 

The pull of the senses is very strong and we cannot find any happiness or true lasting peace by fulfilling their desires. Only when interiorisation of the senses and pulling the life force away from the world we start to find some form of peace and calmness. 

Outward world is full of suffering and problems everywhere we look. This world is described by scriptures as "Land of Suffering", then how can we imagine finding any positive from this. We can only find true purpose of the world by studying the scriptures and following our [[Gurudeva]]'s words. 

---
**This story however many times being repeated, still needs repeating**. 

---
Forgetting continuously this ,worldly man mistakenly starts to look outward for that hidden gem which is impossible to find out here.

![[Pasted image 20250408105655.jpg]]

We all are looking for that same thing but we are mistaken again and again. All comes down to the search of meaning in the psychological sense too. If in this world no matter where, we look for meaning, we cannot find. The scriptures and god-realised men say for our own very good. The only meaning that will be found is in God and finding God.
