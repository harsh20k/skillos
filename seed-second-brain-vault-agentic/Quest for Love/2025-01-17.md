# The Eternal Embrace of the Divine Mother- Guru Pranami

Right after we were born we were destined to die someday. Right after we get happy we sign the agreement knowingly or unknowingly, to become sad one day. Its not hard to infer that this world is just duality, yet in the depth of our hearts we want something non-dual, something true, something that is not touched by any condition or affected by the blows of time. 

---
I am grateful that [[Divine Mother]] showed mercy on me and I found this path without much effort or wandering. With only a little curiosity I just asked what is all this and why am I here? And she gave me her path of love with utmost care and love. With open arms she welcomed me, she is truly called the ocean of kindness. Ignoring all my flaws she just said "Its ok, Come back to me my own!" Lo! Such powerful words and from then she made me my own.

![[Pasted image 20250117084616.jpg]]

---
It would be an understatement that whatever I am right now, whatever I know, whatever I have and whatever I aim in life all of that is because of constant guidance in one form or another. 

Sometimes its hard to bear the distance between me and her. Its hard to bear the thought I will soon die without seeing my mother with these very eyes. [[Viraha]] is a wonderful thing! When we are separated from our beloved we constantly stay in remembarance of her. All our actions become [[Sadhana]] because constantly, whatever we might be doing outwardly, our consciousness is being pulled towards her. 

---
Without [[Viraha]], the heart is not purified to tolerate the divine force of meeting the beloved. [[Premanandji]] says that it is also a form of the lord which precedes her actual arrival. When our [[Viraha]] reaches a boiling point that we cannot even wish to breathe without our beloved she comes running to her devotee. As a follower of this path one should never lose hope, or I must say one never loses hope because sooner or later one gets a feeling that my mother, the true mother of all the universes is with them, and was always with them and with the grace of the [[Gurudeva]]'s promise they will stay with them forever, no matter wherever we stray away.

---
The whole being of the devotee is just filled with gratitude with this ultimate promise, and he constantly starts to move towards the goal, or I must say one is pulled towards the goal no matter what they do or wherever they go. No depth of the universe is safe from my mother's beloved children, she will come running not in a few hours or minutes but in the very instant when with immense devotion we call her name.

With the [[Gurudeva]]'s timeless promise all the baggage on the shoulders is left aside and every situation becomes like a video game where the only objective is to look at mother no matter what is displayed on the screen.
