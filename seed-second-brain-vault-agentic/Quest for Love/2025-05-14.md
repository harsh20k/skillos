# Two Parts of Us

Sometimes we don't want to write, sometimes we don't want to meditate, we just don't want to do the things that we plan out to do. 

We ignore our own managerial identity and its plan. There seems like two identities living in our brain. One seems to be objective and other being very impulsive and selfish. 

The more we identify with the latter that identity becomes more and more stronger. And likewise when we identify ourselves with the objective much more wise part of our brain that part gets more and more strong and our natural state of being.
