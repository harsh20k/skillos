# Love her

The meditation today was unimpressive. My focus was along some past memories, just like a fly flying around a dead carcass. 

The time we spend in places or with people with materialistic tendencies, affects us in a way that is so powerful, with a force  so great that it will feel like the right way or path to think. Only with good company I.e. “Satsanga” can these effects be nullified. Ultimately I was able to feel a few seconds of devotion and love. The love of my mother. I often struggle with finding and then staying with good company. This however, changed when I call upon my [[Guru]]. The very nature of “Satsanga” can be distilled into the practice of living in the presence of [[Guru]] and God.

![](https://i.imgur.com/eMV2y6n.png)


This might also tie in with my predominantly inward approach, which is good to absorb the environment around me but also however, work against me when I’m around an environment much less moral than my liking. 
 
If that’s the case then a good thing to work on personally would be to exercise the part of me that creates structured thoughts and ideas. This could help round me out into an inward absorber with some tools to express myself outwardly, hence protecting me. For this I will be introducing daily journalling as a way to express myself concisely or in an elaborate fashion whichever might be the subject matter. 

The advantages of writing are innumerable. Firstly, we all need to communicate effectively. And for that the least we could do is to communicate with ourselves effectively. Writing could b viewed as a means for that, enabling oneself to be honest and clear with oneself and express needs, wants, thoughts and ideas in a way that is cohesive and fluent.
