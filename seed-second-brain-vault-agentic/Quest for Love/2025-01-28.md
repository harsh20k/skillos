# In the Arms of the Divine Mother: The End of All Fears

![[Pasted image 20250128091556.jpg]]f 

---

We all are afraid of one thing or the other. What If there is an accident when we are driving from work to home. What if we slip just taking a bath and get paralysed by hitting our head? These fears start to dictate our lives if we do not face them properly. 

The only way to properly face these fears is by cultivating that immense [[Shraddha]] on [[Divine Mother]] and her love for us. If we have this great wealth then no situation can cause fear in our hearts. And if we do have some fear in mind then immediately the knowledge of omnipotence of God will destroy that fear. 

---
Our ultimate fear of death is a constant one. Even the exalted yogis get a shudder at the certainty of death. But with right knowledge and the devotion of the divine will and Guru’s words free them from this fear also. They know what will happen after the death of this body, they will be with their divine beloved in whatever form they have done their sadhana. 

In every situation if we start taking into account the mercy of the divine mother then we will realise that there is nothing to be afraid of. Not even a leaf can move 1 millimeter without the will of [[Divine Mother]]. Why worry then if we are scared of something just talk to [[God]], tell him/her of your worries, no one else can free us from our fears and the fear of death.

---
The love of God is the end of all fears. If we know someone very established in the world with a lot of contacts, that too make us very fearless in many worldly matters and we proudly say that I know this and this person very closely. Then imagine the state of person who has this pride of being close to the ultimate power of this universe. The will of whom is the cause of all the universes, if we get that devotion of this entity and a personal relationship with that entity then who can instil fear in us or who can try to make us do anything with force!

---
Fear is reserved for the poor souls who have lost their way and have no relationship with the divine mother. They have all their support systems in the world which are sadly very limited in nature and often do not come to our instant rescue. Only [[Divine Mother]] can make this ultimate promise to rescue us from destruction at the exact moment we need it the most. This assurance can only come to a devotee with the grace of the [[Gurudeva]] and not by outward means and by cultivating that inner personal relationship with the [[Ishta]]. 

Personally, by Guru’s grace and divine mercy I have been able to remember that divine relationship with the infinite in the form of mother and child. In this relationship I am just a helpless child who wants to be happy, and my mother takes care of me every moment and it is her responsibility to make me happy. 

---
At every step when I left home I saw her presence in so many forms and immediate help whenever needed. Who gave me the strength to walk away from a comfortable home in the middle of the night? Who provided me with the contempt from my family and friends so that I only depend on God? It is my mother who did all this to bring me back to her and make a bond with me her beloved child. 

I can’t even begin to describe the constant gratitude I must feel towards that mercy. But why would I feel any gratitude? I am her son she is just doing her duty and job to serve her child. Why did she bring me in this world then if she did not want to raise me into a well behaved child who can maybe help others in need? I just need to be carefree in her devotion and live faithfully in the words of my [[Gurudeva]]. I know I am always in the arms of my mother without any doubt. Only when I forget this relationship only when I think of myself as the doer then this delusion separates me from her and that too momentarily as an illusion. Actually no one and no mountain of grief or suffering can come in between us mother and child. I am helpless but my mother is all powerful she can move mountains or dry the whole ocean for me. 

My mother can turn blades thrown at me into beautiful butterflies, she can break the laws of space and time cause and effect for her beloved child. Oh mother I love you and I know You love me much more than I can imagine!
