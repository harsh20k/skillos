A special yogic technique of meditation practiced by [[Yogoda Satsanga Society of India]]/[[Self-realization Fellowship]] members. _Kriya Yoga_ is a sacred spiritual science, originating millenniums ago in India. It includes certain techniques of meditation whose devoted practice leads to realisation of God. Revived in this age by Mahavatar Babaji (see chapter "A Blessing From Mahavatar Babaji"), _Kriya Yoga_ is the _diksha_ (spiritual initiation) bestowed by the Gurus of YSS/SRF.
