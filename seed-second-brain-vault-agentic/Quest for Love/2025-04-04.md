# "Only Love Can Take My Place"

The consciousness of God is the only real thing in this world. I heard this really great point by Swami Sarvapriyananda. It describes that the one and only one condition for God realisation is that extreme desire to know God. This thirst for God is the only means that will lead a devotee to his Goal or Ishta. 

I cannot be in divine presence if I do not have that thirst for the [[Divine Mother]]'s love and affection. All the habits that are wrong are like pipelines that take the prana energy to lower centres and root us to body consciousness. 

As my Guruji, [[Paramhansa Yogananda]] said "Only love can take my place". That is literally true on every level. When we experience that kind love for any thing or any one in this world without any selfish reason, it is the God's love manifesting itself in our hearts.
