My [[Divine Mother]] is showering me with blessings each moment and these continuous bouts of illness have made this perfectly clear to me that through this she is cleaning me and my heart and my soul so that she can let me see her.

Through suffering we get closer to God and Divine presence. When we get disease and bodily suffering we become purer in the heart to witness the divinity everywhere in this world. 

To bear the cross of life and suffering with dignity is doing our duties and responsibilities with the best of our ability in health or sickness. Since my wedding I have been getting sick continuously and this is the third time. I feel since my wife is now a part of me my mother has given a bulk load of her karma on my shoulders to bear with a smile. 

When in heart we know that any suffering is given by God himself as a gift then we can appreciate its beauty and feel the gratitude in that mercy and divine blessing. 

No one can treat our suffering if our time is not yet come to heal. And no one can harm us if it is our time of health. Every thing that happens to the devotee from the smallest of the coincidences are the divine will and that cannot harm the devotee but come as a medicine to purify the devotee so his heart can handle the bliss of divinity.
