# Only Her [[Love]] Will Set Us Free 

The easiest path to freedom and happiness is being dependent on [[God]]. It is the path for people like us who can’t control their senses, can’t control their thoughts, unable to purify ourselves we condemn ourselves to burn in the eternal fires of duality.

![](https://i.imgur.com/5m8w4LO.png)

Our lust to wander in the world to feel complete has led us astray from our one and only path and aim. The aim of everlasting happiness and love. Only She can fulfil that promise. My mother is the only one in this entire universe capable of manifesting anything and everything. Her words can alone remove our primordial chains of suffering. 
 
Oh divine Mother, instil in us your love, so we can call ourselves proudly one of your own. Premanandji argues however, the way to achieve it to first call and acknowledge ourselves as one her own, and She will have no choice but to make us worthy for her love. 
 
My divine Gurudev proclaims, if the stars come crashing down or the entire universe stands against us we are not alone, for she who has created everything is with us forever and ever. We just have to acknowledge her presence and everything required of us will be done by her. Only her love will set us free. Free from all our sorrows and sufferings, free from all the attachments to things and people who give us pain. Her love, Only Love.
