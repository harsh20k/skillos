# Marriage, Devotion, and the Divine Will: A Spiritual Reflection

This marriage is making me have very weird and disturbing thoughts. The idea of man and woman, their relationship and responsibilities all of the concepts are being mused into this one big confounding concept. 

Yesterday night 2025-02-14 (valentine) we were watching a film "The Last Duel" directed by Ridley Scott and it was exploring the idea of marriage, womanhood and rape. To say it felt disturbing would be an understatement.

![[Pasted image 20250215093158.jpg]]

The idea of marriage as ownership of a woman is a very confusing and poisonous one yet it feels that is what is desirable by the feminine gender. It seems that femininity might despise the idea of ownership but that is what excites them the most. It seems that it is very difficult to be a satisfying husband and companion and also be against this idea of ownership. 

---
It is feeling very hard right now to square all this chaos with spirituality. Let's think about it for a moment. All a husband and wife want is happiness through God union, there is no doubt in that. Now then how should a husband be with his wife and how should a wife behave with his husband? When we enter the realm of these details only the most resolved mind might find a way through all this. 

What would a devotee husband do when his wife tells him that she was raped? Its not that difficult to think about the answer if I stop thinking it about my wife telling me the same thing of getting raped by some other man. The devotee only accepts the relationships of this body as the gifts from Gods and also as the forms of God. He would see the situation as something as God's will and a result of his own karma and his wife's karma. 

Then the man does his duties and responsibilities like a robot and inside he loves only one, the one and only Divine mother. Since his heart only has one he will not be destroyed by such news and nor he will get swayed into despair or jealousy on such matters. All happens according to the will of the Lord and at this time his faith will be tested to the very last extent. 

---
Husband and wife are mere roles we have assigned ourselves but ultimately we are god's children trying to navigate our karma and try to walk to the divinity. When in way we encounter such things like a husband finds wife of committing adultery or a wife finds his husband of doing adultery, the devotees will see them not as the end of the world but as tests of their faiths, a test to prove their love for the divine mother. If the devotee was blinded with attachment then situation like this will be enough to end his progress spiritual or otherwise.  

True devotee does not even flinch when death arrives at his doorstep then how can situation like this take his balance. All that is visible to such a devotee is the divine hand and its will behind everything. Through wealth and poverty, through and health and disease all his eyes see is just the shower of blessings throughout. When he is assailed with such situations he witnesses the cleansing power of the divine mother to make his and her wife's heart pure to make them progress faster on this spiritual journey. 

---
Divine mother is not elusive to such a devotee at any time be it suffering or pleasurable time. Through enjoyment and pleasure he sees the divine blessings showering him with love and care and through suffering and pain he witnesses the pure mercy of the divine mother in the form of cleansing power of the bodily or mental pain and suffering. 

Then let's think about this situation in my life's context. What will I do when I find out my wife committing adultery or what will I feel when I hear the news of someone raping her? If I call myself devotee then all I should see is the will of the lord just like the will I saw through the Good and great times of my life. The news of cheating should not invoke rage or jealousy in my heart, it should however be seen as the hand of divine mother taking away the gift of a "wife" from my life because that would not help me progress further in life.

The news of rape of my wife would be very easy to dismiss as the fruits of our own actions and divine mercy of making us pay in this strange way. Outwardly however, in all the cases we will have to do our duties as what is considered proper way of handling such things. Inside however all there is love of the divine mother. No matter the situation the divine mother in all her intelligence has decided the perfect scenario for my upliftment and spiritual growth. 

---
When we accept everything as the divine will and move forward in life with the ultimate objective in mind, then this journey becomes so simplified and easy to navigate not because there won't be any unfavourable situations or conundrums faced by the devotee but the response will be completely different by a true devotee of the mother. 

My wife, my husband, my child and my family and friends these are false ideas and by nature ephemeral. Accepting this as their own the material man will be quick to judge and dismiss these relationships in unfavourable situations. A Godly man, a devotee will see these relationships as ephemeral and outwardly he will perform the respective duties with utmost integrity, inwardly knowing them just a play by God in his spiritual journey. 

At the end only the love of the [[Divine Mother]] is all that we need and that only can quench our thirst solve all conundrums, make us happy and whole, make us feel loved and secure.
