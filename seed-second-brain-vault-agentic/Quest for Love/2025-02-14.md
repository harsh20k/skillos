# The Guiding Light of My Gurudeva

I don't think I have ever felt this relaxed and peaceful in my life and not in a momentarily hourly way, for example after an hour of meditation I feel that peace, I mean in a way that I feel the peace inside throughout the day throughout the exacting duties and tasks. 

In the background I know that all my doubts and problems are resolved now. Thanks to my beloved [[Gurudeva]] and the devotion he gave for the divine mother. From outside it might look I am tightly wound up but inside I know that the knots of maya are being slowly opened and the doubts and clarifications outside does not matter anymore. 

---
The knowledge and surrender I feel is all given as gifts to me by my divine [[Guru]] every moment, without which I would have been nothing more than an animal. All of the things which might seem mine, my room, my computer, my money and my wife all are his properties and he can add to it or take it all away but inside my deepest depth of heart I know that the only thing that is mine is my beloved [[Gurudeva]], my true love and my light bringer. 

![[Pasted image 20250214105513.jpg]]

The pole star of my life I have found. Now the journey of my life I can easily navigate by orienting myself with his directions. Which path to take on any crossroad is easily resolved and even I feel I don't even have to decide anymore, only the things that he wants for me come into my life, like an impossible finality and all I have to do is to accept as his blessing and carry on be it disease or health, be it wealth or lack of it, be it living in a small hut or sleeping on the roadside. 

---
Everything has been resolved for me now because of my Guru and his blessings divine. No one or no situation can take that away from me! He is silently with utmost patience and care is guiding me to the divine mother. I just have to take one step in front of the other and everything else will fall into place. Everything in the world will move for me if my Guru wills it to be helpful for me. Not even Gods or their laws can stand in my way because I have taken the refuge in the feet of my master my soul, my [[Gurudeva]]. 

---
