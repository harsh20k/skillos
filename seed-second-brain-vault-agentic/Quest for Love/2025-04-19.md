# The Poetry of Mortality

The reasoning behind all the suffering is lack of knowledge or in other words ignorance of the truth. 

Without truth fear will always rule our consciousness throughout our day. If we knew that night comes after day in so and so hours then we won't worry or be scared of nightfall, because we have all the tools to know when the night will come and that knowledge has saved us from that fear.

Exactly like that we have to die that is the truth for all the living things right now. Knowing that how can we be scared to die or of seeing our loved ones on their deathbed. It is true for everyone that is living and breathing then how can we expect the story to not end.

A beautiful poem is the one that has a start, a middle and an end. Life is like that beautiful story that we should fill with beautiful flowers. We all have that fascination with the idea of immortality; the thought of all this drama coming to an end feels so sad isn't it?

But why? It is because of our own ignorance, our own induced coma from the truth.
