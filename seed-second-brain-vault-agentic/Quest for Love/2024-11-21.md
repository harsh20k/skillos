# Dependence on the [[Divine Mother]]
 
Its very hard sometimes to not get pulled into the daily struggles. If we stop being dependent on the divine mother no one even ourselves can help us overcome this dreadful world with its myriad struggles and temptations.

![](https://i.imgur.com/jNTxZxY.png)


By focussing our energy how little we have left towards the divine mother is the only way that will guarantee our success in this life and afterwards. No matter how hard we try by our own effort, intelligence and will power we can never stop the suffering we experience in this world. Only a little child can happily play with this world because all his needs and trials are taken care of by the divine mother herself. 
 
There is no doubt about it because [[God]] himself declares that whoever takes refugee, he himself will carry their burden on his head. We often get so mangled in our problems,  often because of our own doing, that we forget the goal of being carefree in the consciousness of the lord. There is no inherent reality in our problems. It is just a mirage we see because of our ignorance towards the ultimate reality.
 
The dream of this world is not possible to wake up from. Only through divine grace that we can fulfil our destiny. Only through dependence on the divine can give us ultimate independence.
