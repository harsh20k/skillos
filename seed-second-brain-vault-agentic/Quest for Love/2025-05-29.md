God created man, but was not sure if his creation will love him. So he gave man a boon, he will forever have that yearning in his heart for God.

With that He made sure that man will return to him and find in him true refuge and true love. 

Not even an atom is separated from God. Everything resides in Him for He is. Everything else changes throughout time.
