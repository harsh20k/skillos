# Grace and Love of the Divine Mother

It hits you with such a sweet force. Listening to [[Satsanga]], this feeling suddenly comes like a cool breeze while sitting in hot sun. It just hits you soothing every atom of yours. All the worries and anxieties weighing our consciousness just melting away even before this breeze even started to blow. If this is not the grace or [[kripa]] of the lord then I do not what is. 

![[Pasted image 20250116090315.jpg]]

---
From the moment we were born we started to suffer. We were told a story that if you complete your studies properly you will then be happy. After that we were told that going to a university and getting a good job will result in your happiness. Finally when we start to see through we are told that starting a family and having a home and children is actually what all this for and that will definitely make you happy. We all have that but sadly, do we have actually found any happiness? Any lasting peace? Not even once we even questioned this world's grand plan for us, thinking that it was the only right path. 

---
Through kindness of the [[Divine Mother]] we find out about the other path, the road to real happiness. It doesn't tell you that happiness is out there or in the near future or distant one. It promises happiness right at this very moment, this very instant if we wish to look within. 

All the knowledge and all the riches and fame will not quench our thirst, only the divine love can fill this wide hole in our chest and heart. 

![[Pasted image 20250116090357.jpg]]

---
My [[Divine Mother]] is truly very kind. When we wish to start to walk this path, she sends all the necessary tools, knowledge, situations to help us smoothly transition from the fake path to her real one. My mother doesn't care how much money we have, how do we look like, how many friends and family do we have, or how many people respect us, she just loves all her children exactly in the same way. So much mercy and yet she does not ask anything in return. The only thing she asks from us is to remember her constantly! Lo, is there any mother in the world that can come even close to this kind of love, let alone any stranger.

![[Pasted image 20250116090443.jpg]]

---
The motherly love is created from a small atom of the toe of the divine mother. Imagine the love she has for us then, Lo the whole multi-verse and all knows things can come in her lap and still there will be infinite space for more. She IS, that's it. No words can describe the love she can shower her devotees, if we are only willing to accept that love. Living in accordance with her laws is a good place to start on this path. And soon she comes in the form of the [[Gurudeva]] to dispel all our doubts and ignorance. Finally, set free when we feel totally dependent on her for all our needs then she reveals her true love in its all encompassing and infinite form.
