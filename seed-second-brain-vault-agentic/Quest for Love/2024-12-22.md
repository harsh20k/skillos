# The Convergence of Finite and Infinite

If the distance between the infinite and the finite is infinite then how can a finite entity fill this gap? 

The finite entity of this body and mind complex is unable and impotent to travel such great distance towards [[Infinity(GOD)]]. Then how does this gap come to shrink and then disappear.

![[Pasted image 20241222083752.jpg]]

The infinity or [[God]] itself is filled with infinite capabilities, including elimination of distance greater than infinity. That infinity removes this illusion of distance of space and time.

The divinity does not need to travel anywhere because it is everywhere and it is everything, within the smallest of the atom and within the largest mountain and beast. This paradoxical nature of divinity is incomprehensible for the finite mind of the finite living organism.

This is the deep science of religion. Just like mathematics, everything, however large it may be is tiny in front of infinity and however small is humungous in front of the infinite. 

When there is tremendous pull of the infinite in the finite then the infinity constricts itself to a finite thing and a [[Sadhana]] is prescribed to the finite entity. This encapsulation of the infinite in a finite form is the divine mercy and then the finite entity starts its journey towards the infinite. And since this distance is infinite, so the finite entity does not have to go anywhere in space and time to reach infinity. It is already exactly at the place where the finite entity is aware of its finite existence. 

When the finite entity takes this encapsulated form of the infinite with devotion, then the infinity reveals its all pervading nature and the finite entity becomes one with that infiniteness. Hence breaking all the barriers of space and time, he is forever free from the bondages of finiteness like death and aging. 

**This mathematics is revealed to the finite entity by the grace** of the infinity through its various devotees which have tasted this capsule and are one with the infinite. The true nature of the infiniteness cannot be grasped by the finite minds. Yet the infinity with its infinite mercy comes in the grasp of the finite and dances to its whim. Oh! how wonderful then the finite being's state would be. It is unimaginable indeed! The being crushed daily by the worries and tasks is finally able to breathe properly in its newfound home, which he witnesses as all pervading in space and time. 

When this happens space and time becomes like a plaything for such finite being's infinite perception. It just evaporates and only that remains which is [[Truth]] and which is infinite. It might seem from a finite perspective that a finite being is suffering yet there is no suffering and the finite being is rooted in the infinite.
