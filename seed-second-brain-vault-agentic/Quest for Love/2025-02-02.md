# Engulfed in the Tsunami of Divine Love

No one can escape the all encompassing nature of divine love. No matter how cruel we are, no matter if all the world is our enemy or we can't feel love for anyone. The true divine love is in every one's heart flowing directly from the [[Divine Mother]]. Her mere utterance create huge waves of love engulfing our whole being in its nature.

Just like a tsunami doesn't discriminate between the rich and the poor it engulfs everyone in its huge mass of water, the waves of love doesn't discriminate based on our qualifications. 

---
The love for God starts to surround us from every direction when we start to feels her presence around us. 

Today I heard about a great [[Satsanga]] by a SRF nun talking about divine love and how it is the most powerful force in the universe. [[YoganandaJi]] describes that once someone starts on the spiritual path nothing that happens is a coincidence! Every step of the way the devotee is protected by [[Divine Mother]] wherever he steps he can feel the hand of [[Divine Mother]] on the ground that he steps.

![[Pasted image 20250202092352.jpg]]

---
No one or any force can work against the child of [[Divine Mother]]. Even the will of the [[God]] bends itself to accomodate the will of the devotee. 

If we trust in the divine mother and her love we can be free right at this very instant. Her grace and mercy is beyond words and knows no bounds in this universe. It is impossible for the devotee to step outside her umbrella of protection. No matter how deep a hole we fall into, we will see and feel that ultimate love right then and there engulfing us in deep embrace. 

---
[[Premanandji]] also describes his experiences throughout life that before a problem appeared before him the solution was already devised by the [[Mother]] for him. Every calamity will uplift this devotee and every challenge will make the love even more purer in the devotee's heart.

The power of words is limited and cannot be used to describe even a fractional power of divine love. Its nature is only felt and that too always keep on increasing with every step, [[Divine Mother]] always keep her children close to her chest and makes sure that the child keep coming closer to her day and night every breath. 

---
The actions of this devotee will cleanse the whole universe and every thought is a gift from the divine mother to the child for the upliftment of the devotee and the entire cosmos. 

In spite of such power we should not forget what we actually are without this uplifting power of love given by the divine presence. We are mere mortals only concerned about the pleasures of the body and mind. Exploiting the nature and others is our true animal nature if our mother withdraws that power from us.
