# The Path to Freedom: Overcoming Maya with Faith

The path to divine is filled with pitfalls and various obstacles, until we believe in our own power or will. When we understand our dependence on God and [[Gurudeva]] and become convinced of the bond that Guru created during Guru-diksha we witness our freedom. 

Right from the beginning the suffering is immense and we get to witness this all around us in various relationships that we form. Maya in form of various relationships start to attack us and try to push us away from the divine path. 

When we give in to this maya and start believing the words of these foolish people then maya takes root in us and our shraddha and faith takes a hit. With patience and peaceful introspection we should let our karma work out in itself all while remembering God's sweet name.
