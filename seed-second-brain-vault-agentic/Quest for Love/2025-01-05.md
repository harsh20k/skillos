# The End of Suffering: Witnessing the Eternal Truth

---
Why we suffer is because of our own ignorance. When we don't see the truth then we suffer at the hands of our own body and mind. We are the owners of this body and mind. Both of these are natural and therefore, transient and temporary. At one point of time the body is healthy and young and the other point of time this body becomes old and weak filled with various diseases. Mind follows the same pattern. But we, identifying with the body and mind also suffer with them.

![[Pasted image 20250105100423.jpg]]

---
Its a choice to suffer with the body or to witness its decay over time. When we stop identifying with the body and mind our true identity as the child of [[God]] automatically starts to shine from within and that is the end of all suffering. 

We think we can change anything but that's also a paradox we have created because of our delusion. If we could create anything, do anything its only one thing to take the name of the lord and live a life dedicated to her. Our divine mother then lifts up the veil in our hearts to reveal her true identity without which we will always feel incomplete and unsatisfied, like a thirsty man walking endlessly in the dessert fooled by its mirage. Similarly we walk the world with ignorance and thinking that there is happiness in acquiring things and bodily pleasures we lose the goal from our sights. 

---
We might think that this is it. If I can get this then I will be happy, if I get this person on my side then only I can be happy! How foolishly man wastes away his life on this delusion! 

He forgets that seeking true knowledge at [[Gurudeva]]'s feet can we set our sufferings to rest once and for all. The deluded person fails to see this reality and accepts his mortality as the final end to himself. Death is just a transmutation of energy from one form to another. Why is then that people fear death? 

We are invincible, ever lasting just like [[God]], we have to realise this truth in order to be free. If yet we do not realise it, the devotion to the [[Gurudeva]]'s feet will grant us the knowledge at our bodies end and we can finally be free of all the drama of this world and finally rest in [[God]].
