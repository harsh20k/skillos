# The Weight of Grace

### _A reminder that all divine qualities are gifts of the Mother, to be held with humility and gratitude._

Lack of Humility leads to forgetfulness of our true condition. As the devotee progresses their is an increasing need for humility in life. As the divine qualities in the devotee start to shine and take root in daily life if the devotee is not careful the ego starts to swell and he starts looking down on other people. 

![[Pasted image 20250129092709.jpg]]

The ego doesn't let the devotee wear divine qualities for long because by swelling ego thinks it is his doing that such and such is taking place. Lately I have been experiencing this very harmful disease trying to destroy my spiritual progress.

---
I forget who was I actually without the [[kripa]] of the [[Gurudeva]] and [[Divine Mother]]. The condition I was in could be considered unimaginable by many but I am that I should not forget that. 

### **I am that poor individual who slept at 4am in the morning, who constantly chewed on tobacco like a poor person without any knowledge, who masturbated 2-3 times daily just to pass time and survive, without any income source or without any purpose in life just waiting to die.**

All I can feel today is given by my [[Divine Mother]] as a blessing not to feel sorry for others or look down on them but to serve others with much more humility and **compassion**. I have been struggling with this a lot throughout my life I think. I have never been able to handle any knowledge and gain in wealth or anything in general. As soon as I witness in increase of anything in my hand I start to believe that is my doing. 

---
I have to try to daily witness any goodness or divine qualities in this body and mind in their true form that is, [[kripa]], pure mercy and grace of my [[Mother]]. Then I think I might start feeling some humility and my branches might droop with the weight of the apples of divine gifts. Without it my spiritual progress will be restricted and stopped and [[Divine Mother]] will take away all those gifts because I could not be worthy to have them. 

### **The simple philosophy to remember is that I am just a small child who do not know what is good or bad, what is right and wrong, who just follows her mothers words like a chick following mindlessly her mother chicken.** 

I think I need a section in my PKM system to write a set of instructions or words to read everyday, to remind me what truly matters so that I do not fall prey of these spiritual life destroyers like ego and lust.
