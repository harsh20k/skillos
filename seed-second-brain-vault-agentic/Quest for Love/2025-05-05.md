# The Infectious Spark of Divine Love

Life is beautiful. Life is cruel. Life is lonely and sad. Life is amazing beyond any belief.

**Life is what we make of it.**

---
The life that we live everyday that we experience everyday is just God's expression and we can make it so joyful we can't even imagine. If we can just create joy out of thin air for even one second we would have accomplished something really substantial. 

But all this is just mere talk if we do not have God in our lives. If we do not have [[Divine Mother]] in our consciousness we might be just living a life full of misery and drudgery going through the same motions each and every single day without that _spark_. 

If only we change our perspective and look for divine mother we will see the beauty behind all this drama. After we fill our consciousness with the divine love we can spread this love all around us and everyone that will come in contact with us will definitely feel the same divine love.
