# The Heart's Final Refuge

Desires are not letting this heart rest, and krishna is not entering the temple of heart, how many ways should I try to control this heart, I do not know and how will this cross this ocean of maya I do not know.

How will this man get grounded in God's name? How will it continuously flow toward you my [[Divine Mother]]? Only you can accomplish this impossible task. Only your grace can save me. 

Only your love's consciousness can save this heart and make it rest at your feet and your own heart. Only heart can stop my heart. Nothing else, not even the greatest of the sadhana can stop this monkey mind and body of mine.

---
What else there is to learn then? If everything is to be done by the lord, by my [[Divine Mother]] then only thing that remains is to find refugee in her, and ask for that grace and love from our Gurudeva. 

![[Pasted image 20250418093602.jpg]]

The heart only knows how to love. Right now it is preoccupied in loving the people and forms and things of the world. For it to rest peacefully it has to love only one! When all the love has been collected into a single basket and offered it at the mother's feet she accepts those and give us the true divine love that will save us from misery and pain. 

All can be accomplished in this world but we cannot create this love from our own, whatever means we might have. Only source of that is [[Mother]], so oh! heart don't be scared or feel pain, its time to permanently end your suffering, its time to go to our mother divine. Its time to finally rest with her arms wrapped all around you.
