# The Light That Dissolves the Dark Within

There is this fine line that I feel I am walking on and on either side there is an endless pit and if I'm not careful enough its waiting to swallow me whole. 

But this feeling comes only when I forget my [[Divine Mother]] and her love for me. 

![[Pasted image 20250312104314.jpg]]

The fear or of being a failure becomes self-evident in the moment spent away from the [[Mother]] and her grace. In the deepest corners of my mind and ego I have protected this place of darkness and fear and chaos which can erupt at any time. Essentially it is just imaginary in the grand scheme of things. It is protected by my own ego and the boundary that I have created around it called "Mine" so the light of wisdom and freedom does not feel to to fall on this god forsaken place.

In short our deepest fears and desires are really unimaginably dark and sinister and everything in this place is forsaken by the divinity because we hide this place from our [[Gurudeva]]. If we can create [[Mother]]'s temple in the middle of this place we will see it dissolve into nothingness because light does not take ages to fill a room with brightness and warmth. 

Deep inside us, within our own ego we are carrying a place so horrifying that even we are scared to look directly at it. We get a hint of such place and we change the topic and fail to even acknowledge its presence. 

It is [[Mother]]'s love and grace that can only protect us from our own cage. My mother's love and light have no boundaries and it will break through this wall too and enter these chaotic halls and set us permanently free. We just have to keep ourselves centred in the divine presence.
