# Eternal Embrace of Divine Love

I am a sinner. I am selfish and worth less than dog roaming the streets for a morsel of food. If all of my characteristics and actions would be weighed against the truth it will be a dismal sight. 

And yet [[Divine Mother|She]] loves me! And suddenly I am something else altogether. The love of the divine mother doesn't weight anything against anything. Her love is not conditional it showers upon everyone without any discrimination. 

![[Pasted image 20241219081901.jpg]]

The rainfall of her love is constant throughout and I am cleaned by it every time I get soaked in her consciousness. I might be a sinner but I am also her child and no one not even death can stand in my way, because when I walk my mother's palm come in front of my every step. So blessed!!

Its hard to say in words the feeling this gives me, the joy it imparts on my being. My every atom become energised my the thought of her. As soon as I separate myself from her, I become the same dog with some skin disease who is shooed away by everyone with disgust. 

My mother doesn't look at our outward condition and state. We all are her only child. She takes care of each one of us like there is no other preoccupation in her mind. Her attention to us is like a Sun which shines without care of anything else on everybody. This truth is enough for living this life remembering her, crying for her. Only those will understand me who have showered in this love rain, even once. Those who are unaware of this are like a madman carrying all the luggage on his shoulders and walking in the train. If only we could just put everything we have on the train, her passionate love for us will carry everything and take us directly to our destination without delay. 

She loves us and cry for us too because her love is not limited natural limitations. I cry for her, for the blessing that someday I might bathe in that love continuously, constantly for every moment I breathe. I cry for her to come and talk to me like a mother talks to her child. She comes in so many forms in front of us and yet we fail to recognise her and we even feel disgusting emotions like anger and lust towards her. 

---
Yet she forgives everything and craves for our love, want us to come back to her. Oh my mother divine, how can I talk about your love. How can I begin to imagine your love for us all. When everyone rejects us like an insect, or a worthless dog you, only you accepts us with open arms and a smile that transcends even the beauty of the stars. Love me oh mother! and give me the secret of this love so I too can love thee in every breath and each living moment passes in your thought.
