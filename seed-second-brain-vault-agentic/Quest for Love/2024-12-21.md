# Self-Evaluation: The Path to Purity

[[Self-evaluation]] is a must for a good devotee. Looking for ways in how we can improve is a responsibility of everyone. A good devotee never shies away from it. This intuition, however is the result of [[Guru]] [[kripa]] and God's love.

![[Pasted image 20241221083948.jpg|center]]

The mind's restlessness and impurities within are the cause of mental problems like anger, lust , jealousy and countless of its children. We witness them everyday within us sprouting into various bushes and weeds which we have to be carefully trim and sometimes root out. This doesn't happen overnight. It takes time, effort and a lot of research on our part. 

Going from one task to another when we forget our goal we get sidetracked in our own little selfishness and the result is the full-blown attach from all our shortcomings. We get to a place of purity by [[kripa]], yet we also have to do our part. 

[[Anger]] is one of the most dangerous of them all. the cause of anger is lust. When we lust for something, and that something does not come to pass as we wish we get angry. This is not supposed to show our flaws or blemishes in our character, but to prod us to move in the right directions whenever we walk away from the real path that is God realisation. 

---
### Then how do I check this anger?

**Remove the root of the problem and the problem will cease to exist.** Just like a tree can't survive without the roots anger cannot arise without its parent lust, as described by [[Gurudeva]]. I have to give up all wishes from others, then true love can arise and true compassion and the all forgiving nature can shine from within.

If we walk 1 step God takes 9. We just have to start trying, and God will finish the job for us without even asking. When we give up our desires God takes care of our every aspect. Then why crave for something, why judge others because they could not fulfil what we desire.
