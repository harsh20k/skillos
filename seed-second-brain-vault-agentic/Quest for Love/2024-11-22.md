# Love her
 
In the morning today I had a very beautiful dream. Premanandji Maharaj told me that he would always protect me and my sadhana wherever I go. I felt there was a doubt in me that if I plan to go to Canada my spirituality will be left behind. And Premanandji himself told me not to worry with this eternal promise. 

![](https://i.imgur.com/mAvqJVJ.png)


The promises we make with each other are false. If we can’t even promise that if we live in the next moment, then how can other promises be true. But the promise of [[Guru]] is eternal, of ultimate benefit and true. Sun may change its direction but this promise can never be falsified.
 
I was touched by my mother’s simple words of assurance. One can only cry over the heavenly grace one is showered with each and every moment. It’s inconceivable for the ignorant, that someone could love them so passionately, so eternally, so beautifully. 
Yesterday satsang talked about, the same consciousness in both sadhana and other worldly activities. Everything should be done with the aim to please divine mother, always keeping in mind that mother is always watching what they are doing. The fruit of this practice is instantaneous answer to one’s dilemmas. 
 
[[God]] is not far away or abstract, he is the [[Truth]] behind all the farces. He hides behind everything waiting to be found by his devotee. Additionally, he is the only reason that we can find Him. Mind can neither grasp the vast nature of divine grace, nor it can comprehend the immense love of the mother. This problem of life is hard enough, why make it more complicated by questioning God’s love and grace. 
 
I will sleep more soundly now that my mother will protect me forever. No matter how steeped in worldliness I become, her long and soft hands of love will always bring me back to her. Only this knowledge can set me free.
