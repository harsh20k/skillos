# Breaking Free from Worry
 
It is the normal human condition to keep worrying and get stressed by continuously thinking about our problems. This conditioning is our habit since the time immemorial, since our countless births and rebirths. 

![](https://i.imgur.com/G2O8MMO.png)

This conditioning does help us to stay away from danger. But this line of thought doesn’t help us at all in performing our daily duties. Worry doesn’t solve our problems, instead it makes us less resilient to face future problems. And yet, it is comforting to worry about our problems because it gives us a false sense of accomplishment that we have done something about our problems.
