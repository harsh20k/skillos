# Embracing Divine Grace Amidst Chaos

This wedding is giving me some anxiety. Although I feel great in the mornings but after the tiring day I feel myself pulled in the anxieties of the details of the wedding which also hinders my sadhana in the nights. This is however, temporary because this ceremony is just for a day.

[[Divine Mother]] and her grace has already explained me through the rejection of the temple wedding ceremony idea, that this wedding is just a form of complete bastardisation of the original wedding ceremony. 

---
All this halla-baloo is for nothing more than the animalistic pleasures of the families of this body and I am just a part of their elaborate plan to start an animalistic relationship with another person. Deep inside this bastardisation is the idea of wedding of two souls so that they can enable each other in the divine search. 

![[Pasted image 20250121090840.jpg]]

That idea is completely lost nowadays, and all that remains is the dance and music and anything and everything that can separate us from [[God]]. 

---
But by giving me this lesson [[Mother]] is trying for me to understand the truth and help me separate the two stories and keep me grounded. Oh! How merciful is my [[Divine Mother]], how graceful is her presence and how wonderful is her plan! 

It breaks my heart to be a part of such thing and that I think is the underlying reason of all the anxieties. 

---
Drowning man will always pull the nearest thing and take that thing also underwater to drown with him. When people who are deeply drenched in [[Maya]] are so entangled in it, whenever they witness anyone trying to come up water in search of the divine mother, their only instinct is to pull them back down underwater and hinder their search for divine.

This however has an opposite effect, this helps the devotee to strengthen their [[Sadhana]] and breaks the illusory bond between the two no matter how close the relationship between the two is.

---
Like this also, my [[Mother]] helps our devotee in their journey upward and quicken their ascend. Whatever happens in the path of the Truth is for the upliftment of the devotee, yet it might seem like an obstacle. 

Exactly like this, this wedding ceremony might seem like an obstacle and a speed breaker but as long as my motives are clear this will ultimately be a blessing for my spiritual growth and help me every way imaginable, my mother will make sure of that.
