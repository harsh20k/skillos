# The Divine Promise - Beyond [[Maya]]

During [[Satsanga]] we find these hidden gems that take over our mind and plant themselves deeply in our hearts. I found one today, actually I have heard it countless times from [[Guru|Gurudeva]] Paramhansa Yoganandji and from Guru Premanandji. 

![[Pasted image 20241218082957.jpg]]

It is the divine promise that [[God]] makes to his devotee - When the whole earth is scorched in fire my hand will protect you, when the whole universe come crashing upon you, my chest will stand in front of you, and when there won't be any sun to give you light my eyes will provide you light.

When I heard this it did not affect me much. But slowly, it made sense to me more and more. I can't outrun my fate and neither this body's death. What I can count on is God alone that he will never let anything happen to me. I am his own and he protects everyone who is his own, who in his heart has resolved to be his own. Not even death can separate a devotee from his God, since power of death comes from the ultimate source of all power. 

[[Death]] is all around us we witness so many deaths everyday. A mosquito, an insect, so many microorganisms die each moment yet we live in delusion that we will outlive everything. Alas, [[Death]] comes for all no matter their beliefs, no matter how wealthy or how many connections one has in life, death spares no one. 

Then why does God proclaim that he will protect me from everything. This is where we need to understand the concept of [[Death]] itself. 

---
When we sleep and are in a dream we cannot distinguish it from reality. And when we are awake nothing can convince that this waking reality might also be a dream. Guruji tells us however, that this is an illusion of [[Maya]] that doesn't let us cognise the true reality which is beyond the waking and dream state and even beyond the deep sleep state. Just like the physical body might be sleeping comfortably in a cosy bed, yet a person dreaming of drowning will suffer really in his dream consciousness; this body consciousness feels real to us and the awakened ones tell us about a reality beyond this. 

A reality that is beyond our physical body, and in that sense God proclaims his promise, since even God incarnate in flesh and bones did not escape death. In this reality in which [[Death]] can't reach us and in which we are God's children, we can witness this true promise playing out in real time. Not a single moment passes in which He leaves our side. 

Not even God can't deny our right to be with Him, if only we just chose. Alas, [[Maya]] doesn't make this task easier, powered by the same God it manifests itself as the force keeping divinity apart from people.
