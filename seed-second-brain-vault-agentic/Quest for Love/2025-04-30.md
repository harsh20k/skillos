# At the Feet of the Master: A Glimpse into Divine Devotion

What I saw in this video https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9RK7TRqNqGI of [[Premanandji]] is the display of Guru-disciple relationship. The relationship not easily digestible by the human ego nourished for a long time with outer appreciations by the world. 

![[Pasted image 20250430115339.jpg]]

[[Premanandji]] enjoys a stature in the society which is hardly seen in a century. He is completely oblivious to all that when he is sitting in front of his [[Gurudeva]]. With extreme attention he worships him like a God indeed, nothing less. 

Oh how beautifully he worships his saviour Guru, like a child devoted to his mother.
