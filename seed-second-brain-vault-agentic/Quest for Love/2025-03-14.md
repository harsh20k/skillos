# A Child's Cry for the Divine Mother

No matter where I am, no matter who I am with Oh my beloved mother you are the one taking care of me each moment. It is your grace that has kept me alive and given me a hope that one day I will be with you again. 

Through the grace of your Guru form I came to know about the lord and the path to walk towards you slowly and easily. 

May all the colours in the world wash away from my mind and body and only your colours colour my whole being. Oh divine one, oh merciful and graceful please do not forsake me because I am yours and only yours.

---
I might forget you and walk on a path that does not lead to you but I have faith and undestructible belief that you will always hold me by my hand and put me on the right path and I still don't listen then you take control of my body and mind and do whatever is that pleases you.

My mother, oh the one who never abandons her children in the ocean of this samsara I love thee. Who tells me thou are dark oh my mother divine. My gurudev often sings this cosmic chant and I wish to stay within his kripa umbrella so I can get to you. My mother you are the only one who can shine the light on the path to realisation. You are the only one who wants the best for me.

---
My mother I know these problems and pleasures are all your forms but I do not wish to see them now, I have been staring at them for so long I don't even remember your face. My imagination is you, my desire is you, my meditation is on you, my love is for you and my everything is you. 

Just like [[Shiva]] takes the offering of burnt meat by the devotee and blesses the devotee, I have the offering of this rotten meat and rotten mind and that is the only thing I have for you. I hope you will be pleased with this only and bless me with the body of your true child and keep me near your bosom all the time. 

Oh timeless one, oh causeless one look at me once because with you one gaze no amount of filth can stay for even an instant. Bless me with your gaze and free me from all this delusion and suffering. Its not good for a mother to let the child play in dirt for long. It is time to pick up this child of your mother. Its time to take me home. Its time to take this child with you and clean him and bathe him with your love and put in bed of your lap. 

I am worthless if I can't even see my mother, I am hopeless if even my mother doesn't love me. I can't expect anyone to love me if my own mother has looked the other way. Its time to look at your child directly mother. Its time to break him from this bodily prison and take him back to the original throne of peace and eternal love and happiness.
