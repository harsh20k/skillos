# In the Divine Mother’s Circle of Grace

---
Past couple of days I've been experiencing some issues when I stopped my medicine [[Foracort 200]] inhaler. I stopped doing engergisation exercises and doing proper meditation.

I think this has always been my weakness when I suffer from breathing problems my [[Sadhana]] is deeply affected. This should not happen because a true devotee can given up his life but cannot live without doing bhajan of the lord. Divine mother doesn't wish us to be perfect but she does want us to try with whatever strength we can muster at the moment to be with her. 

---

![[Pasted image 20250110085800.jpg]]

The really interesting part of this malady was that not for once, did I experience any feeling of being without my mother's protection. I deeply and truly felt being in her circle of protection even when doing really bad things like [[Masturbation]] and tobacco consumption.

---
I feel that's what [[Premanandji]] means when he says to take refugee in the divine mother's arms. No matter how bad or how good we become we should only strive to be with her, and in turn by her grace we actually feel being in her protection. And when she wants to help her children, nothing is impossible!

She is the divinity herself, the ruler of the universes, how can it be difficult for her to do anything. When divine mother wishes to help her children not even [[Death]] can come in between.

There is also one thing clear, that all these trials and tribulations in devotee's life come to make him more resilient, more powerful. All this process for the applications were not for me to go to a better country or give me better education, but make me a better student of the [[Gurudeva]]'s words. All this was meant for me to better spend my time not on the worldly pleasures but do a deep dive research in the immense texts and videos of [[Gurudeva]].
