# The Transformative Power of Satsanga

The most powerful thing in the universe is the power of [[Satsanga]]. It has so much strength that with force it converts a non-devotee worldly person into a believer and God fearing individual. 

![[Pasted image 20250318105639.jpg]]

In the most harshest of the hearts it germinates the idea of God's Love. When it has locked its target no amount of crying or running can save a person it will convert that individual from the core and they have no say in that. 

How do people find [[Satsanga]] you might ask. As described by premanandji when God feels something special for a soul he gives that soul his greatest blessing i.e. [[Satsanga]]. 

When the individual with great faith and curiosity listens to these omnipotent words it almost seems like something takes over him and brings the best of him to the surface.
