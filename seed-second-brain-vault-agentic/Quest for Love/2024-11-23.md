# A Devotee’s Path to [[Divine Love]]
 
I could make some improvements in my life to better suit the role of a devoted child to the Divine Mother. One of them is removal of thirst for bodily pleasures. I could also add offering food to divine mother before eating it myself. Only having prasad not food.

![](https://i.imgur.com/csnYX72.png)


But then again when I start thinking about all the bad qualities I have I feel the conversation changes from being dependent on mother’s grace to becoming self independent. That is bothersome but I feel it is my duty because it is the proper use of intelligence and cunning that I inherited from my mother. 
 
Given this, I feel the work of constant improvement should continue in a devotee but there should not be any worry about when this work will come to fruition. The devotee’s duty is to be performed dispassionately disregarding any care about when all this will come to fruition. In the background the most important duty is the constant remembrance of divinity throughout the day. This duty should be upheld above all else, especially when push comes to shove. 
 
My mother will show herself when it is the right time. All her decisions are the law for me. All I have to do is to give myself fully to the duties laid out for me according to the situations and station of life. Until she materialises, she whispers her presence into my ear through countless little actions of divine mercy and grace. The one and only [[Truth]] in this life is “She loves me and I love her”. As I deepen my love she will begin unfolding her true and vast nature to me without any effort. All that I have to do is love her “Truly”. 
 
This divine knowledge is also her grace’s doing. Satsang is given to a devotee when [[God]] is especially pleased with them. I am showered in this satsanga all day, may a few drops stay on me throughout my day and I could always bathe in the love that she is showering me with constantly.
