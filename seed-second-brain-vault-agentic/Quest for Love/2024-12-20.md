# Her Love Will Set Us Free
---

Another day, another opportunity! When I did not know [[God]] every day was a laborious thing in front of my eyes. Everything I had to do was scary and big because it was rooted in mineness and [[Ahankar]]. When I started to know [[God]] every day has become so sweet. Every morning has become another great learning opportunity and a new energy flows through me that fills my very being to accomplish all tasks with a new found energy because it is not rooted in [[Ahankar]] but her. 

![[Pasted image 20241220090828.jpg|center]]

It is her task that I am doing, with this consciousness gone is that confusion, gone is the feeling "I really don't want to do this". When everything is to please her then why would I question anything or why would I question myself if that is worth it or not.

This happens by her [[kripa]] alone. When she decides to change the course of someone's life she wishes and exactly that begins to happen. All worries and problems start to appear small and like little pebbles in one's path. That's why a devotee becomes fearless and care-free, not by his own psychological strength or any physical characteristic or by any personal conviction, but by only her [[Grace]]. 

There have been countless mornings and yet nothing becomes crystal clear for anyone where he is going or what one has to achieve that day. No personal conviction has stuck with anyone with personal motivation because ultimately it wanes off into dust. Only the devotee finds that quiet and peace within that lets him to do the best at wherever he might be.

All hurdles and struggles become like flowers in the path of a devotee. Her love is the only thing that he craves. This realisation sets him free even from the cycle of death and re-birth, then who cares about the little failures one faces throughout the day. It is evident to the devotee that, if nothing can happen without her acquiescence then every problem he faces becomes a gift from the divine. A gift that he accepts with reverence and proudly proclaims his duty as the duty divine. 

Love is not earned by the devotee by any qualification. We cannot "buy" God by doing such and such actions or by this much "[[naam-jap]]" or by this much number of [[Kriya]]. The true devotee realises he is never free from the chains of the maya through his actions, so he seeks the shelter of the divine [[Guru]] and through him finds his eternal home. 

Just like, the journey ends when we come back home, our life ends when we come back to our true home, our origin, our end that is [[God]]. A new life awaits such devotee, a life full of joy, a happiness indescribable, which a lifetime of hardship can't diminish. Come lets walk together now [[God]] comes by his side living and breathing strength and courage in his being. It is aptly said that "Her love will set us free!"
