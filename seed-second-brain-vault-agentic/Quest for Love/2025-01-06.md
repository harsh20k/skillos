# The Path of Small Steps: A Journey to [[Self-realisation]]

The path to [[Self-realisation]] is a path for the lifetime. In this path ups and downs don't matter that much. What matters is that we are putting some effort to improve everyday. Yes it is difficult but with small improvements we don't have to aim really high for something that feels completely out of reach.

[[God]]'s mercy is infinite and we are receiving it that's why we are able to even think about improving our life in accordance with the [[Guru]]'s words. 

---

![[Pasted image 20250106102034.jpg]]

---
One big challenge I have right now which I will have to improve upon is the rampant loss of vital fluid through [[Masturbation]]. For years I have masturbated regularly almost everyday. This will change with my continuous effort and by the grace of my mother. I will improve that I know, I have witnessed how much I can change how anyone can change when they walk this path. I will have to take small steps and then in a few years, I wouldn't be able to recognise myself.

I will aim for leaving masturbation for 24 hours in the beginning. Then gradually aim for 36 hours and then 48 hours and so on. 

---
On the surface this plan might look not going anywhere, but the alternative is to just resign everything to my fate and keep doing what I am doing right now for the rest of my life. In the grand scheme of things like in terms of decades, this plan will change me tremendously and that too effortlessly. Everything in this plan is within my reach I just have to fight a little bit. 

---
Naam-jap is the greatest weapon I have against my base pleasures and then I rest the divine mother will take care of. I am so blessed to have found the path of small improvements because in this path the goal is not some lofty ideal. The goal is something very close almost like taking a next step. 

When walking a long trek we might not even feel anything when we think about just taking the next step, allowing the journey to unfold each step at a time. But when we look at the destination we might feel overwhelmed and balk at even taking one step.

---
Just like everything all my habits can be improved upon by taking small steps. This is the beginning at every step. I can be present at each step. Fully aware of the whole journey but focussed on just the next step in my evolution. By the grace of my [[Gurudeva]] and divine mother I have stumbled upon this technique of small improvements. Lets see where it leads me.
