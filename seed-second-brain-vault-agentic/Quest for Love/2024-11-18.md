# Unconditional Love: A Devotee’s Reflection on Divine Grace
 
Another new day. I am amazed every day how much my [[Guru]] and God loves me. Each day i find a new example of his love, ever increasing in intensity and ingenuity. 
 
![](https://i.imgur.com/jkdAzS8.png)

 
The love and care one starts to feel is indescribable in words but I will still try my best to say something about it. The thing that feels so touching is that there is no reason for that love, it just flows from their heart since their nature is ocean of love. They cannot resist loving all of us. 
 
The ability to remember is given by them. The knowledge to understand some part of their words is given to us through them. They are the very reason that we can even feel the speck of the love that they shower upon us each moment. Love can be only described as “indescribable”. True love is without reason, without any conditions, without any discernible cause, it just flows from the lover to the object of love. 
 
I had doubt about a thing quality of love that I heard about that, true love is always is always expressed in action. I thought that by action it meant outward action. But the word action encompasses more than just outward activities or rituals, it also envelops within itself bhajan. That is, remembarance of the loved object by the lover.
