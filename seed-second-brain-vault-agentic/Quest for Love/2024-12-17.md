# Path of Illusions: The Struggle for [[Self-realisation|Self-Realization]]

The path to [[Self-realisation|self-relisation]] is a very complex and unique for everyone in this universe. I did not personally know that I was already walking on this path through the blessings of this body's mother. 

![[Pasted image 20241217083446.jpg]]

I was given the gifts of introversion and contemplation from a really young age by God. It made me reflective and thoughtful and I always investigated why something made me feel hurt and I resolved to remove it from my life. All the reasons that the world give us to stay motivated to work towards having a "good life", by which they mean earning good money and buy houses and cars; stops us to stand still for a moment and evaluate the path on which we are moving forward.

The path to self-relisation just pops out of nowhere(because it is everywhere), and suddenly whole trajectory of our life is completed shifted. Following this path is our true calling. It is what we are trying to find after we do anything, like getting a girlfriend, or buy a new object of our desire or even stealing from others. Its driving us to even breathe! 

This quest is eternal and it will not end until we find ourselves before the feet of our [[Guru]], our saviour. The culmination of all the searching is the feet of the [[Divine Mother]]. The final resting place for everyone, no matter their origin, qualification or karma. 

Load after load we gather all these object on our backs and think that we might feel sufficient one day. The thirst for money is particularly poisonous. A greedy person is completely possessed by the this thirst and his every thought and action revolves around how he could earn/make/steal more money. He forgets God altogether and keeps walking on the path of self destruction. So many people have fallen as its victim and still fail to realise their mistakes because of the blindness of their own making. The fall of man is then sealed and after many many lifetimes he might then be chance to reclaim the spiritual path, his true calling, only when the divine mercy might pity his cries one death after another. People like these, those who constantly suffer in the fires of greed and hoarding have a special power to never be satisfied. Their thirst and hunger fits to the size of the meal they get. They want always more and more. 

Why don't then people get scared of wealth and its effects in general is beyond our comprehension. Even if someone might ask why is wealth good, he is immediately answered by everyone he knows that it is the ultimate goal of life. [[Satsanga]] cuts through all this noise and shouts at him to stop and open his eyes, result being the eyes then start to see this faltering path taking straight to his doom in this life and the next. Forever shrouded in delusion is the mind of such a person, possessing ability to make others like him. 

Just like the world told him that money is the ultimate reality and the only thing to strive for he starts yelling to others the same, repeating everything like a parrot sitting in a cage craving for the next fruit and repeating his sins all over again. A handful from this pitiful lot, start to see the hollow lives they had created for themselves devoid of anything real and lasting. They start to tire of the thrill the world has promised them behind every object be it a new Voiture/Maison or another femme. For the first time they start feeling the scorching heat beneath their feet on which they thought they were revelling in countless. [[Satsanga]] breaks through all the debris of this false happiness and lets the individual see his own **hell**.
