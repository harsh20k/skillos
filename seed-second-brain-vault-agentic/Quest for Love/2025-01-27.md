# The Eternal Embrace of the Divine Mother

I had yet another dream witnessing infidelity in Suman. It is a constant reminder by my lovely [[Mother]] to protect me from the attachment to a wife. My mother I see is very jealous and doesn't want her son's attachment to her mother to be diluted by any other person be it wife or worldly mother or any in-laws. 

![[Pasted image 20250127093013.jpg]]

It might seem inappropriate a mother telling a son that his soon to be wife might be infidel and show him that scenario in great detail. But in fact it is her love and constant protection by helping me not be attached to any outward form be it wife or any other person.

---
The only thing that [[God]] desires from us and the only condition he has put on the individual soul to meet him is that the should have that undiluted devotion and love towards [[Divine Mother]]. 

I feel more convinced now that my mother will never my side and she will always protect me from entangling in any inward attachment. The feeling that this is "My wife" or "my husband" is poisonous because that is rooted in the ignorant consciousness of ego. When through satsang we realise that everything we will every have is only [[God]]'s then we can actually see that this "Wife/husband" and every one that we think loves us is not ours but [[God]]'s. Every form is God, every cause is God then we should not be scared of any loss or feel happiness by any gain. 

My mother expresses her love in countless forms and countless ways. And by giving me this knowledge she is making sure that I her beloved son does not forget her by being so close to a woman in a familial bond. And this very act has solidified my love for my mother even more and trust in her that no matter what my condition or memory she will come to me whenever I need her.

---
I am truly blessed to have such a [[Mother]] other than whom no one is making such cosmic promises. The promise to stay always near and dear to the devotee.
