# Gathering for the Eternal

No one will or any thing will stay with us. Only the name of God will stay with us after the end of this life.

Right at the moment of death we will have that same fear of losing everything that we have gathered all our lives. But a devotee will not have that same fear because he will have gathered everything required. God consciousness is the goal of all and a devotee is always striving for that. 

![[Pasted image 20250421101031.jpg]]

Even a godly person who throughout their life keeps striving for money and become rich, doesn't regret because he will have given everything for it. Likewise, a devotee who has laboured throughout their lives for God consciousness but failed to do so will have no regrets and even then at the end moment he will be given by the grace of the Guru and God that divine consciousness and all the fears and suffering will find that final end.

All we can do is try to get there. Getting there is their responsibility and our responsibility is trying to get there. 

---
Our whole life should be dedicated for achieving this wonderful place.
