# The Search for Divine Mother – A Path Beyond Illusions

The kind of love we have from God is ignored by us. How unlucky am I that forgetting that love and pure mercy I look for other ways to fulfil my desires and animalistic tendencies. 

Its during busy and stressful times that we forget the divine protection of [[Mother]] that we all have in our lives.

When we forget that and move away from that we sign our own agreement to stay unhappy and unsatisfied forever. Let's give up this thirst for bodily pleasures. Let's give up this false search for something other than our divine mother. It is the only search that will satisfy whatever we are searching for. Be it money or job, or wife or loving husband or anything in this universe looking for our divine mother is the solution to all our problems and sufferings. 

---
No matter our station in life, no matter the amount of money in our bank account all sufferings and delusions will be exactly like they are unless we look for our [[Divine Mother]]. Unless we look for her love we are doomed to burn in the fires of hell. 

**Mind you that I am only saying about _LOOKING FOR HER_. Merely looking for our divine mother and her qualities like love and mercy will give us the direction and guidance to walk on the correct path based on our situation.**

---
This puzzle is only solved with the help of [[Gurudeva]] but to get to our true Guru, our eyes should have that fire to look for our one and only mother divine.

No matter how many distractions we have in life or how many pleasures or lack of them, the world throws at us our constant looking and search for the divinity should always be our top priority. 

Our only goal should be how do I get close to that divine presence inspite of all responsibilities and rights in life. In spite of all the distractions and pleasures of the world we must find a way to constantly thrive in her presence.
