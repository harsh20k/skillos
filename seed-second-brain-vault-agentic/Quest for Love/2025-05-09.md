# The Cosmic Dream and the Eternal Love of Divine Mother

Lord Shiva, one of the Gods in the [[Trinity]] says to his wife, Parvati ji, that this whole samsara is but a dream, only thing real is Bhajan (Sat Hari-bhajan, jagat sab sapna). Now however we try to put this truth into practice our bodies cannot avoid the sufferings of this phenomenal universe. All the liberated souls ultimately died, at least their bodies did. We all will meet the same end.

In the midst of all the drama and suffering in the world we have to create that inner sanctum, that inner bunker that is our only shelter where we can go anywhere at any time where no one can touch us. Practically, that won't matter in the physical sense. But in the ultimate sense this has a drastic significance.

When this whole world is compared to a dream, in fact not compared to a dream, it is said to be a dream on a cosmic scale. Why would anyone worry about the dream conflicts, dream victories or dream pains and dream gains? 

![[A serene cosmic dreamscape with Lord Shiva and Parvati, surrounded by ethereal light, symbolizing divine love and truth..jpg]]

---
[[Divine Mother]] loves all that is the only truth. And with that truth we have to do our own [[Dharma]] day and night and everything is better. When we are in the consciousness of the truth, it does not matter what is happening to the body or mind, we will direct them according to our dharma. The body and mind are being governed by Dharma, and the soul is already untouchable by anything. Only one thing can touch the soul and that is [[Divine Love]] of my [[Mother]]. 

We have to strive to be in that love, at all times, even when we are performing our most complicated duties and responsibilities, our heart should be in a place of love for [[Divine Mother]]. 

Not even the breaking universe can touch the peace and love of that soul. The whole world will melt away but this love only increases in intensity. A devotee, hence should be not in the consciousness of the world/Dream, but in the consciousness of the truth/Love for God, then everything becomes the means of liberation.
