# Rediscovering Our Infinite Self

Most of us are not familiar with ourselves. We might think that we are this body-mind complex. We even have taken this far enough to include in our identity the ephemeral possessions of material nature. Some include in this identity our close relationships and bonds of the world. All the spirituality is about changing this assumption we have. We are not the body, not the mind and neither its thoughts and emotions. We are not the sum total of our account balance, trophies or relationships. We are the embodiment of pure cosmic consciousness! Pure cosmic consciousness is omnipresent, omnipotent and omniscient with pure bliss and love at the heart of it all.

![[A cosmic scene with a person meditating, surrounded by infinite light and stars, symbolizing the connection to pure cosmic consciousness..jpg]]

---
Now this identity is a lot more like our ideal want in our life. Everything that we crave for is embedded in this simple definition of pure cosmic consciousness. There is complete absence of suffering or any imperfection, there is no birth or death, there is no loss or gain, no heat or cold, just an ocean of bliss and love. We are the atom of that same ocean.

Now we are not aware of this, instead what we do know is that we have a body and mind. The body has its infinite needs and mind is just like a 3 year old which cannot settle on anything. We have been identified with the finite and yet our own true identity is infinite. 

---
Meditation and [[Sadhana]] of any sort is a way to establish our long forgotten identity and ending our attachment to this pathological one. This identity has led to our suffering since the day we were born.
