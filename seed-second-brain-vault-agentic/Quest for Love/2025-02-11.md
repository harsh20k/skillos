# Refuge in the Divine: A Path Beyond Illusion

I haven't written since 3-4 days and I believe that is not too big of a deal considering the fever and wedding celebrations I have been through this time. 

The various reason I have written are in my mind and they continue to be important. Their importance and transformative nature is not unknown to me, yet I have to make a conscious effort to solidify this practice of daily writing; this gift of writing that [[Divine Mother]] gave me is too big to give up. 

Only the true Guru and their grace provide this knowledge and I can't be more grateful and in debt of these divine blessings by these divine beings.

---
My motives should be clear throughout this journey of life and that will protect me from everything. For example my wife said to me that I should start earning money. This statement however innocuous, is fundamentally wrong because it does not allow the listener the room for look for any other way to be happy. It puts too much importance to the idea of money and its fulfilment the ultimate source of happiness. 

Right from the beginning of this game if our objectives are misplaced and we take our refuge in the material things and money then we have failed from the very start. We should only take refuge in the divine presence and the blessings that come from that ultimate source of everything. 

![[Pasted image 20250211103442.jpg]]

---
The organisation of our lives should be around that truth, around that purpose of love and divinity. Only way to succeed in this life is to play it according to the divine rules, any other way will lead us straight to suffering and pain and loss that we may not even be able to bear. Inspite of our mistakes and wrong judgements if we take refuge in our [[Mother]] and her love for us we will be guided in a way that makes our mistakes a stepping stone to our salvation and take us closer to her presence.

---
The time and space are just mere illusions created by her divine powers that try to separate us from her in every possible way we can imagine.

If only our true purpose and allegiance stay in the feet of our mother then only can we make the right decisions and walk steadfastly on our path to freedom and love.

Love is a divine force and it transcends all of our shortcomings and all the laws.
