# [[Order]] from [[Chaos]]: The Power of Mental and Physical Cleanliness

The thoughts we have all day are often disorganised and we are bombarded with information from sources all around us throughout the day. But we fail to retain most of the information. We also don't even try to organise our own thinking.

Now if we consider from a bird's eye view the main objective being to escape [[suffering]], then the habit of organising one's thoughts could be useful practice just like cleaning someone's room.
#### Why is cleaning the room so fundamentally positive?
We are the sum total of everything we are surrounded by, the things around us, the people we chose to be surrounded by be it miles aways and most importantly the thoughts we have while traversing this world. The act of cleaning the room changes something fundamentally in our orientation in the world that is ultimately [[chaos]]. It is an act of bringing back [[order]] in our lives. 

![[Pasted image 20241213103100.jpg]]

[[Chaos]] is [[Maya]] and order is the opposite i.e. [[God]]. The same analogy is applicable in the world of thoughts. The act of organising one's thinking makes a fundamental difference in a person's objective. It helps us to stand our ground rooted firmly in our structure, order and [[God]]liness, against all the currents of chaos or [[Maya]]. 

Just as the saying goes [[cleanliness is Godliness]], one might be inclined to think it in only outward terms, like cleaning the things around us and our bodies. We are forgetting one important things that we are not just bodies but we have minds too and heart nonetheless. Trying to organise our thoughts helps declutter our thoughts and keeping it up to date where the relevant parts are connected to one another and the thoughts that are unrelated to our core philosophies just have no place to be. 

This practice of mental hygiene could be thought of as [[meta thinking]], thinking about thinking. Giving a thought about how we think is like asking ourselves how to live. Its very hard to imagine that we might not have the best strategies to live and exist, although we have been alive for so many years. Then I can understand it would be very to imagine too that the thought patterns we have employed since our birth may not be in our best interest.
