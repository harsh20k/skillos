# Becoming the Identity We Aspire To

Do we become what we think or wish we will become or do we become what we think? I believe there is no reason for an individual to become what they wish for, instead they will effortlessly become an individual with an identity that resembles their thought processes.

The thoughts we are currently having will shape our tomorrow. The flaw with wishful thinking is that when an inspiration hits us we get an instantaneous push to be someone we are not. Yet our underlying thought processes throughout the day remain exactly like before. Therefore, we might get a kick to set big resolutions or follow their routines for a few days, but the identity we carry every day in our deep thoughts remain the same.

---

![[Pasted image 20250113095627.jpg]]

When we think I want to write a novel or like I want to become a writer in 4-5 years, we are wishing for something that will happen suddenly and then we will become writers. But instead we should be looking at our day and convert it into something resembling a writer's day. How does a writer think and what does a writer do throughout their day. If we just copy these things then we inherit that identity of a writer just by living like one. With these things in motion we can see the opportunities and the universe will then unfold itself in a way like a writer sees the world.

---
I don't know when I will become a good devotee and when will the [[Kriya]] come to lift me up to [[Bhakti]] and [[Vairagya]]. But what I can strive for is to trying to incorporate routines and thought processes associated with a good devotee's identity. By doing this, I will make myself rightful able to stand in that mantle of "Good Devotee" or a "Bhakta".

---
It is a mantle that we are striving for in the future. We are not trying to be someone but we want to be at least able to stand in that mantle where they are standing and slowly by integrating what we can into our lives and thoughts we will have nowhere to go but to our ideal.

Rest is take care of by [[Divine Mother]]. She will take care of the details the opportunities you need along the way or the challenges you need to get more resilient or complete torture to make you feel the reality of being nothing more than dirt.

![[Pasted image 20250113095720.jpg]]

Love for [[Divine Mother]] is the source of all knowledge and what we should worry about is to love our mother. Because loving her is the root of all truths. Truth is what is left standing by the test of time and something real not constructed by web of lies and selfishness. Truth doesn't need any support just like love it doesn't require anything, it is complete in itself. Moreover, it makes us feel complete no matter our place in the world.
