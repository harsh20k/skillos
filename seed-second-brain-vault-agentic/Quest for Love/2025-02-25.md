# Embracing Divine Love: Surrendering the Weight of the World

Why do we think we can feel content from the things of the world. Why are we so ignorant? 

We have to at some point let go of all our worries and responsibilities at the hand of the God. He only can provide the perfect counsel based on the past, present and future. 

The weight of self-reliance is too heavy for us and we are tired of carrying them on our shoulders continuously throughout our lives. We are just not strong enough to carry this load all alone. We have to and inside we already want to give this load to someone else, and that someone else is only God. 

![[Pasted image 20250225121204.jpg]]

When we try to look elsewhere for support we will only find disappointment and despair. No person is capable to help us only those can help us whose lives are completely surrendered to the divine mother. Its kind of a paradox that only those are helpful to anyone who know that they themselves are completely helpless without God. Those who found strength in the divine mother's feet, are the ones that are powerful.

---
We live in this world not because of our own will; our very breaths are not controlled by us, and no matter how much we try we cannot start it if it decides to stop it.

The spiritual engine is the source of all power and we have to connect to these engines to enjoy the fruits of love and everything that we have thirst for. Divine mother is all powerful and have limitless love for us, the one thing that we have been craving for our whole lives. If we do not realise this true love then we have not yet fulfilled our true purpose of our lives. 

Our whole live is merely a mechanical movement if all we do is earn money and make relationships with worldly people everywhere no matter the amount of material success. All the riches and relationships fade away in the face of time and death. All that will remain with us true love, the love of our [[Divine Mother]] for us that we all forget to cultivate and realise in our lives.

---
Inspite of my bad habits and weaknesses I can't live without the love of the divine love. Without it I might be a rich person and yet the poorest of all. All money can do is buy bodily pleasure which all will become useless when our bodies become useless. 

With the divine love and surrender we can carry on in any outward condition and take away that love and we will given in to multitude of problems like depression and maladies. We daily see people killing themselves because of depression inspite of their celebrity status and mountains of wealth.

So let's decide to take refuge in our mother's arms and with her [[Divine Love]] and all powerful name we will swim across this ocean of [[Samsara]].
