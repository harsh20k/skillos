# Walking the Guru’s Path: A Commitment to Divine Routine
---

Today I realised something important during my daily meditations. It must be important because anything related to [[spirituality]] that is conceived in meditation is true without a doubt.

![[Pasted image 20241225083852.jpg]]

It was regarding following my Guru's words verbatim without any changes from my side. I've also heard this from [[Premanandji]]. He has elaborated, that the routine given by [[Guru]] must be followed as they said without any dilution or editing. I have been lazy to incorporate many things into my routine according to my [[Gurudeva]]'s wishes. If I keep ignoring these signs and direct order from God and Guru I won't be able to progress quickly on this path. 

---
So from now on I will have to incorporate these routines as described by my beloved [[Guru]] for my own sake. One is the following of the energisation exercises. Second is to read my Guru's word through the given lessons as they are the direct line of communication from him and his direct disciples. 

The reason that this is important is because without the blessings of Guru a devotee doesn't progress, he even goes downward into the spiral of [[Maya]] and become worldly more than before. 

This is a scary thought because if He leaves us then we are mere animals. He is the source of our spirituality, we don't possess the strength to follow his principles and routines. It is his own strength and knowledge that we can understand his word and also, get up and work towards our goal. Through following the word of Guru as described by him in the lessons I will prove myself a good devotee and soon reach the true abode of my home sweet home!

---
If I wish for to ignore this direct message from God himself I will have signed on my own death warrant. If love is what I want, if nirantar bhajan is what I wish for then this has to be my utmost priority to follow Guru's word as he said it, not how I find it convenient in my daily life.

![[Pasted image 20241225084650.jpg]]

I have to fit my daily life according to the wishes of the divine, his wishes should not conform to my comforts or whims. He is my master not the other way around as the Maya try to convince us. It is true that prodigal or not we inherit our father's wealth but if we want to enjoy the real benefit, to face the divine within our lifetimes, then this has to be done. 

---
One important point to remember is that, although I might try to follow his word completely with my strength and vigour, the true strength required to follow and understand his true wishes is the result of his own [[kripa]]. 

So let's wake up from this delusion and follow our preceptor word by word, day by day.

---
Let's make a plan to do the same efficiently and practically(thank you for this divine intervention :). Let's focus on two things that we can try to do from today itself:
- [ ] **After writing, lets read a passage from the lessons and assimilate it through the day(15 minutes).** 
- [ ] **Find the energisation exercises video and try to follow it before meditation twice daily.**
