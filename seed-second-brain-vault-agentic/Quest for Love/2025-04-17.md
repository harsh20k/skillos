# Waves of Her Love 🌊

The love that devotee feels, the love that I feel constantly cannot be described in any words. Its like the heart is about to explode at any moment. My [[Divine Mother]] loves so much but slowly gives that same love to the devotee along with the strength to hold that unlimited supernatural love.

Immense waves of love come suddenly on the shore of the heart which has been stilled and whose direction has been changed from the world to [[Mother]]. 

---
No matter the situation good or bad, respect or disrespect [[Mother]] loves us tremendously and we perceive that when we have no one except our mother in our life.

![[Pasted image 20250417090752.jpg]]

Oh love's source, oh the holy ganga's origin fill me with your love completely and incessantly. The mutual love of God and his devotee can only be had after the devotee finds a true Guru and takes refuge at his holy feet. The only thing left to do for such a devotee is "I will sing thy name, I will drink thy name, and get all drunk oh! with thy name!!". 

What else remains for that devotee? Nothing in this world, not the most expensive cars, not the beautiful houses or the most sophisticated pieces of technology lose their pull for such a devotee devoted to only the feet of the lord.
