# Breath of the Divine: The Power of Name and Perception
 
Today there was a small hiccup in my daily routine. Last night my throat was feeling a bit uneasy so feel asleep later than usual and therefore woke up late in the morning. 
 
![](https://i.imgur.com/P2QMnGx.png)

 
This does mean that without the God’s interference we are nothing but dust without his breadth we are just dust and will soon become dust. Its when we remember about this breath of the lord that we become something more powerful than the dirt where this body comes from. There is very strange potential energy behind anything related to God. The mere rememberance of him, or the name of the lord will completely set us free from the bondage of our own fruits of action for an instant. Additionally, it will create a new habit that will ultimately lead to complete destruction of any bondage in the devotee’s heart. 
 
That is why saints of all religion tell us to pray, to say lord’s name, to devotee our every waking moment in consciously thinking about him. But this will not instantly remove all our problems. Our perception has been so shrouded by the soot of our passions and desires that we won’t be able to see the inner workings of the name of lord. 

As our perception becomes clearer and clearer with daily [[Guru]] given practice, and as the lenses of our perception become clearer and close to the [[Truth]], we will begin to realise the far reaching effects of Name within ourselves. Otherwise we will be stranded like a lamb in between the lions of our own wrong-doings.
