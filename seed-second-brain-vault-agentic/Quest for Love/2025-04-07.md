The reason for anger is the root cause of all the suffering. It is desire. The desire for the events to happen a certain way or in other words, or the desire for the things to follow a certain path is the root cause of all the suffering. 

Instead when we take the path of least resistance i.e. anything that happens is taken as the will of the lord, we start following the divine way of life. A life like this cannot be described in words to anyone by anyone, nor it can be transformed into simple actionable items checklist.
